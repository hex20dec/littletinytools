Wind chill is how cold it feels when wind is factored into the actual air temperature. It helps you understand the impact of wind on your body's perception of cold.

How Does the Wind Chill Calculator Work?

The wind chill formula combines air temperature and wind speed to determine how cold it feels on your skin. Here’s the breakdown in simple terms:
