How It Works

The Difference Calculator uses a straightforward formula to determine the difference between two numbers: it subtracts the smaller number from the larger one. The result is always a positive value, ensuring clarity and ease of understanding. Here's how it breaks down:

  • Step 1: Enter two numbers in the input fields.
  • Step 2: Click "Calculate Difference."
  • Step 3: The tool subtracts the smaller number from the larger one and displays the result.
