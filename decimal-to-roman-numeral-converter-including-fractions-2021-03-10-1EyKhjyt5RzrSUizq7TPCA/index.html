How It Works

The tool works by breaking down your decimal number into smaller parts that match the values of Roman numeral symbols. Each part is then converted step by step, starting from the largest value (1000 = M) down to the smallest (0.25 = ··). Here's a quick breakdown:

For example, to convert 12.5, the tool uses X (10) + II (2) + S (0.5) to give you XII·S. It’s that simple!
