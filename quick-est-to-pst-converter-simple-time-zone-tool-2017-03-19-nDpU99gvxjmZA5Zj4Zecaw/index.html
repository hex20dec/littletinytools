How It Works

The EST to PST Converter works by adjusting the time you enter in Eastern Standard Time (EST) to Pacific Standard Time (PST). Since PST is 3 hours behind EST, the tool subtracts 3 hours from your input time. For example, if it's 2:00 PM EST, the converter will show 11:00 AM PST. It also handles the AM/PM switch seamlessly, so you don’t have to worry about confusing morning and evening times. Simple, right?
