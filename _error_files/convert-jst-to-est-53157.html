JST to EST Time Converter

Our JST to EST Time Converter is a free online tool that helps you convert time from Japan Standard Time (JST) to Eastern Standard Time (EST). This converter is useful for individuals who need to schedule meetings, appointments, or events across different time zones. With our converter, you can easily calculate the time difference between JST and EST, ensuring that you stay on schedule and avoid any confusion.

Note: JST is 13 hours ahead of EST

How the Converter Works

The JST to EST converter uses a simple formula to calculate the time difference between the two time zones. The formula is based on the fact that JST is 13 hours ahead of EST. To convert time from JST to EST, you need to subtract 13 hours from the JST time. For example, if it's 10:00 AM JST, the equivalent time in EST would be 9:00 PM (previous day).
