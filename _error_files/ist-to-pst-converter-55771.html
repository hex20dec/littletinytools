IST to PST Converter

The IST to PST Converter is an online tool designed to help users convert time from Indian Standard Time (IST) to Pacific Standard Time (PST). This tool is particularly useful for individuals who need to schedule meetings, appointments, or events across different time zones, ensuring seamless communication and coordination. With its user-friendly interface and instant conversion capabilities, this tool has become an essential resource for travelers, business professionals, and anyone looking to stay connected with others across the globe.

How it Works

The IST to PST Converter uses a simple formula to calculate the time difference between the two time zones. The formula is based on the fact that PST is 12 hours and 30 minutes behind IST. To convert a time from IST to PST, you can use the following steps:

1. Subtract 12 hours and 30 minutes from the IST time.

2. If the resulting time is before 12:00 PM, add 24 hours to get the correct time in PST.

For example, if the IST time is 14:30 (2:30 PM), you would subtract 12 hours and 30 minutes to get 02:00 (2:00 AM) in PST.
