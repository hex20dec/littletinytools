How It Works

The Half-Day Difference Calculator works by calculating the total time difference between two timestamps and then converting that difference into half-day units. Here's the simple formula behind it:

  • 1. Subtract the start time from the end time to get the total time difference in milliseconds.
  • 2. Convert milliseconds into half-days by dividing the total difference by 12 hours (since 1 half-day = 12 hours).
  • 3. Break down the result into days, hours, and minutes for a detailed understanding.

For example, if you input a start time of 9:00 AM and an end time of 9:00 PM, the tool will show you that the difference is exactly 1 half-day. It's that straightforward!
