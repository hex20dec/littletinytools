How It Works:

The tool follows a simple yet effective process to clean and sort your list:

  1. Input Your List: Paste your items, each on a new line, into the input box.
  2. Choose Sorting Order: Select whether you want your list sorted in ascending (A-Z) or descending (Z-A) order.
  3. Remove Duplicates (Optional): Toggle the checkbox to automatically remove duplicate items from your list.
  4. Process and Enjoy: Click the "Process List" button, and voilà! Your clean, sorted list appears in the output box.
