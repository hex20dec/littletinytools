    How it Works

    The Keyword Counter App uses a simple yet effective formula to calculate the frequency of each keyword in the given text. The formula can be explained as follows: Keyword Frequency = (Number of occurrences of the keyword in the text) / (Total number of words in the text). This formula provides a clear understanding of how often each keyword appears in the text, allowing users to adjust their content accordingly.
