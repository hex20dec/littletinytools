How It Works

The magic behind this tool is a simple formula: Celsius = Kelvin - 273.15. Kelvin is the scientific standard for temperature, but Celsius is what most of us use in everyday life. This tool bridges the gap by subtracting 273.15 from the Kelvin value to give you the Celsius equivalent. For example, if you input 300 Kelvin, the tool calculates 300 - 273.15 = 26.85°C.
