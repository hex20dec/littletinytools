Unique Keys Sorter App

The Unique Keys Sorter App is a powerful online tool designed to help users organize and manage their keys efficiently. With its user-friendly interface, this app enables you to remove duplicates, sort keys in ascending or descending order, and separate them using a variety of options, including commas, spaces, and newlines. Whether you're dealing with a large dataset or a small list, this tool is perfect for streamlining your workflow and boosting productivity.

How it Works

The Unique Keys Sorter App employs a simple yet effective formula to deliver accurate results. Here's a step-by-step breakdown of the process:

  1. Input your keys, one per line, into the designated text area.
  2. Select a separator to use between your keys, such as a comma, space, or newline.
  3. Choose from three options: Remove Duplicates, Sort Ascending, or Sort Descending.
  4. The app processes your input and produces a sorted list of unique keys, which are then displayed in the output text area.
