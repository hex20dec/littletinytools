Sales Tax Calculator

The Sales Tax Calculator is a simple online tool designed to help individuals and businesses calculate the total cost of a purchase, including sales tax. By entering the purchase amount and sales tax rate, users can quickly determine the total cost and the amount of sales tax owed, making it an essential resource for anyone looking to navigate the complexities of sales tax calculations. With its user-friendly interface and precise calculations, this tool is perfect for a wide range of applications, from personal finance to business operations.

How it Works

The Sales Tax Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the total cost and sales tax amount: Total Cost = Purchase Amount + (Purchase Amount x Sales Tax Rate). This formula is based on the principle that the sales tax rate is a percentage of the purchase amount, and the total cost is the sum of the purchase amount and the sales tax amount.
