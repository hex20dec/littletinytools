How Does It Work?

The Non-Empty Value Sorter follows a simple yet effective process:

  1. You enter your values—either one per line or separated by commas.
  2. The tool removes any empty or whitespace-only entries automatically.
  3. It sorts the remaining values alphabetically or numerically, depending on the content.
  4. You get a clean, organized list ready for use or further processing.
