How It Works

The tool works by taking two temperature values in Kelvin (K) and multiplying them together. The result is expressed in Kelvin squared (K²), which is often used in scientific calculations involving heat, energy, or thermodynamics. Here’s the formula in plain English:

Temperature 1 (K) × Temperature 2 (K) = Product (K²)

For example, if you input 300 K and 400 K, the tool calculates 300 × 400 = 120,000 K². Easy, right?
