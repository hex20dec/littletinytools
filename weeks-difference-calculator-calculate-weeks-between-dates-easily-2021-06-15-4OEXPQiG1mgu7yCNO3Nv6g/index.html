How It Works

The Weeks Difference Calculator makes calculating the time between two dates as simple as pie. Here’s the magic behind it:

  1. Pick your start date and end date using the easy-to-use date picker.
  2. Click the "Calculate" button—no math, no stress!
  3. The tool calculates the total number of milliseconds between the two dates (yes, milliseconds!), converts them into days, and then divides by 7 to give you the number of weeks.

It’s like having a tiny time wizard in your pocket!
