Profit and Loss Calculator

Profits and losses don’t have to be a mystery anymore.

Whether you’re selling handmade crafts, flipping gadgets, or running a small business, knowing your profit or loss is crucial. But let’s be honest—doing the math manually can feel like a chore. That’s where this Profit and Loss Calculator comes in. Just plug in your cost price and selling price, and voilà! It tells you exactly how much you’ve gained or lost—no headaches, no spreadsheets, just quick results. Perfect for anyone who wants to stay on top of their finances without breaking a sweat.

Enter the cost price and selling price to calculate your profit or loss.

How It Works

This tool calculates your profit or loss with a simple formula:

Profit or Loss = Selling Price - Cost Price

If the result is positive, you’ve made a profit. If it’s negative, you’ve incurred a loss. And if it’s zero? Well, you’ve broken even. It’s that straightforward!
