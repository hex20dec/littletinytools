How It Works

The Date Gap Summary Calculator works by taking two dates and breaking down the difference between them into digestible chunks. Here’s the magic behind it:

  • Days: The total number of days between the two dates.
  • Weeks: Divide the days by 7 to get the number of weeks.
  • Months: Divide the days by 30 to estimate the number of months.
  • Years: Divide the days by 365 to approximate the number of years.

It’s that simple! No complex formulas or mental gymnastics needed.
