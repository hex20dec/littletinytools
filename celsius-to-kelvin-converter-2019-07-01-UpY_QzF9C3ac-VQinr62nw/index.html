How the Conversion Works

The conversion from Celsius to Kelvin is based on a straightforward formula that adds 273.15 to the temperature in Celsius. This formula is derived from the definition of the Kelvin scale, where 0 Kelvin is absolute zero, and the Celsius scale, where 0 degrees Celsius is the freezing point of water. The formula can be expressed as follows: Kelvin (K) = Celsius (°C) + 273.15.
