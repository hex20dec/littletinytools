How it Works

The Base64 decoding process involves a simple yet effective formula: it takes a Base64 encoded string, which is a sequence of characters that represents binary data, and converts it back into its original text format. This is achieved through a series of steps that involve recognizing the Base64 characters, translating them into their corresponding binary values, and then reassembling the binary data into its original form.
