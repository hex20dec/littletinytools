How Does It Work?

The Hyphen Counter works by scanning your text and identifying every instance of the hyphen character (-). Here’s the simple formula it follows:

Total Hyphens = Number of times "-" appears in your text

For example, if your text is "This is a test-text with multiple-hyphens," the tool will count 2 hyphens. It’s that straightforward!
