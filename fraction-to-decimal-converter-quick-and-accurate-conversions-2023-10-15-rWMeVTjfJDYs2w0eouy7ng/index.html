How It Works

The Fraction to Decimal Converter uses a simple formula: divide the top number (numerator) by the bottom number (denominator). For example, if you input 3/4, the tool calculates 3 ÷ 4 = 0.75. It’s that easy! The result is displayed as a decimal rounded to four decimal places, so you get precise answers every time.
