How It Works

This tool uses a simple formula to calculate the remainder when one number (the dividend) is divided by another (the divisor). Here’s the breakdown:

  • Dividend: The number you want to divide.
  • Divisor: The number you’re dividing by.
  • Remainder: What’s left after dividing the dividend by the divisor.

For example, if you divide 10 by 3, the remainder is 1 because 3 goes into 10 three times (totaling 9), leaving 1 left over. Easy, right?
