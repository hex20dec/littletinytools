How it Works

The conversion process involves a simple formula: UTC time = (KST time - 9 hours). This means that if it is 12:00 PM (noon) in KST, it would be 3:00 AM in UTC. The tool takes into account the 9-hour difference between the two time zones and provides the equivalent UTC time.
