How Does It Work?

The JST to AEDT Converter works by adding 2 hours to the Japan Standard Time (JST) because AEDT is 2 hours ahead of JST. For example, if it's 14:00 (2 PM) in Tokyo, it’s 16:00 (4 PM) in Sydney. The tool ensures the time stays within the 24-hour format, so even if you input 23:00 (11 PM), the converter will correctly display 01:00 (1 AM) the next day in AEDT.
