24-hour to 12-hour Time Converter

The 24-hour to 12-hour time converter is a useful tool for converting time from a 24-hour clock format to a 12-hour clock format. This online time converter is designed to simplify the process of converting time, making it easier to understand and work with different time formats. Whether you're working with international schedules, coordinating events across time zones, or simply need to convert time for personal use, this tool is an essential resource. With its user-friendly interface and straightforward conversion process, the 24-hour to 12-hour time converter is the perfect solution for anyone looking to quickly and easily convert time between these two formats.

How it Works

The conversion process is based on a simple formula: if the hour is less than 12, it remains the same, and AM is added. If the hour is 12 or greater, it is converted to a 12-hour format by subtracting 12 from the hour, unless it's exactly 12, and PM is added. For example, 14:30 in 24-hour format is converted to 02:30 PM in 12-hour format.
