How the Converter Works

The conversion process involves a simple formula: subtract 32 from the Fahrenheit temperature, multiply by 5/9, and then add 273.15. This formula is based on the definition of the Kelvin scale, where 0 Kelvin is absolute zero, and the size of one degree Kelvin is equal to the size of one degree Celsius. The formula can be expressed as: Kelvin = (Fahrenheit - 32) * 5/9 + 273.15.
