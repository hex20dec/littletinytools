How It Works

The Price Per Unit Calculator uses a simple formula to help you compare costs effectively. Here’s how it breaks down:

Price Per Unit = Total Price ÷ Quantity

For example, if you’re comparing two bags of coffee—one priced at $10 for 500 grams and another at $15 for 750 grams—the calculator will show you the cost per gram for each. This way, you can easily spot the better deal without pulling out a calculator or scratching your head!
