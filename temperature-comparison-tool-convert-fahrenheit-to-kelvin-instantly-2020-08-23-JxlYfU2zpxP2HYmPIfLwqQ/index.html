How It Works

This tool uses simple formulas to convert temperatures between Fahrenheit and Kelvin:

  • Fahrenheit to Kelvin: Subtract 32 from the Fahrenheit value, multiply by 5/9, and then add 273.15.
  • Kelvin to Fahrenheit: Subtract 273.15 from the Kelvin value, multiply by 9/5, and then add 32.
