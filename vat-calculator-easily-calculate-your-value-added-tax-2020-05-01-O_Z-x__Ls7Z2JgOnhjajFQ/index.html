How the VAT Calculator Works: A Step-by-Step Explanation

The VAT calculator operates on a simple yet effective formula: VAT Amount = Price (excluding VAT) x VAT Rate. This formula is then used to calculate the Total Price by adding the VAT Amount to the initial Price. To illustrate this concept further, consider the following table showcasing different VAT calculations based on various prices and VAT rates:
