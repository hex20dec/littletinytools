How It Works

The Unique Line Counter works by breaking down your text into individual lines, trimming any extra spaces, and then filtering out duplicates. Here’s the magic formula:

  1. Input Your Text: Paste or type your text into the box.
  2. Split into Lines: The tool splits your text into separate lines based on where you hit "Enter."
  3. Trim and Clean: It removes any extra spaces or blank lines that don’t count.
  4. Filter Uniqueness: The tool then identifies and counts only the unique lines.
  5. Display the Count: Finally, it shows you the total number of unique lines in your text.

It’s like having a personal assistant who sorts through your text and gives you the clean, unique version—fast and hassle-free!
