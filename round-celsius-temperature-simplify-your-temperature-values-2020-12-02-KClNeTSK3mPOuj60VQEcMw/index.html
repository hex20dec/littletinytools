How It Works

This tool uses a straightforward formula to round your Celsius values:

If the decimal part is 0.5 or higher, the number rounds up. If it’s less than 0.5, the number rounds down. For example, 23.7°C becomes 24°C, while 23.4°C becomes 23°C.
