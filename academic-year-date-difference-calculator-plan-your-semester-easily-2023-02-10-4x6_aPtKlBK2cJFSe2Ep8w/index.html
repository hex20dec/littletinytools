How It Works

This tool calculates the difference between two dates in days, weeks, and months. Here’s the breakdown:

  • Days: The total number of days between the two dates.
  • Weeks: The total number of weeks, calculated by dividing the days by 7.
  • Months: The approximate number of months, calculated by dividing the days by 30.

For example, if your start date is January 1 and your end date is January 31, the tool will show:

  • Days: 30 days
  • Weeks: 4 weeks (30 ÷ 7)
  • Months: 1 month (30 ÷ 30)

It’s that simple! Just pick your dates, hit calculate, and let the tool do the rest.
