How It Works

The Speed Unit Conversion Calculator uses a straightforward formula to convert between units. Each speed unit has a predefined conversion factor that relates it to other units. Here's how it works:

Formula: Converted Speed = Original Speed × Conversion Factor

For example, to convert 100 km/h to mph, you multiply 100 by 0.621371 (the conversion factor for km/h to mph), which gives you 62.1371 mph. The calculator handles all these factors behind the scenes, so you don’t have to.
