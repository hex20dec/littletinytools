How Does It Work?

The tool works by taking your input (like "PST" or "GMT+3") and matching it to a database of IANA time zone formats. These formats are the standard used by software and systems worldwide to handle time zones accurately. Think of it like a translator for time zones—turning everyday abbreviations into something computers can work with seamlessly.
