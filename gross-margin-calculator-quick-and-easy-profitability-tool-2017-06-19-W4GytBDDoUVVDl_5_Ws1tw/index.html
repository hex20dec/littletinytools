Gross Margin Calculator

Running a business or managing finances and feeling overwhelmed by the numbers? You're not alone. Let’s make financial planning less of a chore and more of a win!

Gross margin is the percentage of revenue remaining after subtracting the cost of goods sold. A higher percentage indicates better profitability.

Please enter valid numbers for both Revenue and COGS.

How It Works

The Gross Margin Calculator uses a simple formula to determine your profitability. Here's the magic behind it:

Gross Margin (%) = [(Revenue - COGS) ÷ Revenue] × 100

For example, if your business generates $10,000 in revenue and your COGS is $6,000, the calculation would look like this:

[($10,000 - $6,000) ÷ $10,000] × 100 = 40%

This means 40% of your revenue is profit after covering the cost of goods sold. Easy, right?
