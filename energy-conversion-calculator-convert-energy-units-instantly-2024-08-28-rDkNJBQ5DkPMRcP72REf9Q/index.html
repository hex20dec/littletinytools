How It Works

This tool uses a straightforward formula to convert energy values between different units. Here's the magic behind it:

Converted Value = (Input Value × Conversion Factor of From Unit) ÷ Conversion Factor of To Unit

For example, to convert 100 joules to kilowatt-hours, the tool multiplies 100 by 1 (the conversion factor for joules) and then divides by 3,600,000 (the conversion factor for kilowatt-hours). The result? A neat 0.0000278 kWh. Easy, right?
