How Does It Work?

The Profit Margin Calculator uses a simple formula to determine how much profit you're making on each sale. Here’s the breakdown:

Profit Margin = ((Selling Price - Cost Price) / Selling Price) × 100

For example, if you sell a product for $50 and it costs you $30 to make, your profit margin is 40%. That means 40% of your selling price is pure profit. Easy, right?
