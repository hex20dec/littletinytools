Weekday Difference Calculator

The Weekday Difference Calculator is a free online tool designed to calculate the number of weekdays between two dates, excluding weekends and holidays. This tool is ideal for event planners, business owners, and individuals who need to schedule appointments, track workdays, or plan projects. By simply entering the start and end dates, users can quickly determine the number of weekdays and plan accordingly. The calculator takes into account the total number of days between the two dates and subtracts the number of weekend days, providing an accurate count of weekdays. This makes it an essential tool for anyone looking to optimize their schedule and increase productivity.

How it Works

The Weekday Difference Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the number of weekdays between two dates. The formula is as follows: total days between the two dates minus the number of weekend days. This can be represented as: Weekday Difference = Total Days - Weekend Days.
