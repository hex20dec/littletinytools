Enter the Sort by Lowercase Letters tool—your new best friend for effortlessly arranging text in alphabetical order. How It Works

The tool takes your list of words or phrases, compares them in lowercase (so capitalization doesn’t mess things up), and sorts them alphabetically. Think of it like arranging books on a shelf—except this shelf is perfectly organized in seconds. Here’s the formula:

  • Step 1: Paste your list into the input box.
  • Step 2: Click "Sort" to arrange the items alphabetically.
  • Step 3: Copy the sorted list and use it wherever you need!
