Enter tally marks (e.g., ||||) and get the corresponding number instantly.

Use | for single marks and / or \ for groups of 5.

Tally marks are counted in groups of 5, with every fifth mark represented as a diagonal line crossing the previous four (e.g., ||||).

How Does It Work?

The Tally Marks to Decimal Converter works by counting each mark and converting it into its corresponding number. Here’s the breakdown:

  • • Each | (single tally mark) represents 1.
  • • Each / or \ (diagonal line) represents a group of 5.

The tool adds up the values of all the marks you enter and gives you the total in decimal form. It’s like having a tiny calculator just for tally marks!
