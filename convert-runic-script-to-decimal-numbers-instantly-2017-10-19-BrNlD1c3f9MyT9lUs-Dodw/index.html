How It Works

Our Runic Script to Decimal Converter is super simple to use. Each rune corresponds to a specific decimal value (e.g., ᚠ = 1, ᚢ = 2, ᚦ = 3). When you enter a sequence of runes, the tool adds up their individual values to give you the total decimal number.
