How it Works

The Planetary Orbital Period Difference Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the difference in orbital periods between two planets. The formula is: Difference = |Orbital Period of Planet 1 - Orbital Period of Planet 2|, where Orbital Period is the time it takes for a planet to complete one orbit around the Sun.
