How it Works

The AEDT to PST converter uses a simple formula to calculate the time difference between the two time zones. Since AEDT is 17 hours ahead of PST, we subtract 17 hours from the AEDT time to get the equivalent PST time. For example, if it's 10:00 AM AEDT, the equivalent PST time would be 5:00 PM (previous day).
