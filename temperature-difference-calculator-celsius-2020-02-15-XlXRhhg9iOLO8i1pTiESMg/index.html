How it Works

The Temperature Difference Calculator uses a straightforward formula to calculate the absolute difference between two temperatures: Temperature Difference = |Temperature 1 - Temperature 2|. This formula ensures that the result is always a positive value, representing the actual difference between the two temperatures.
