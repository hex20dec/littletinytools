The Decimal Rounding Calculator uses a simple formula to round your numbers: Original Number → Round to Desired Decimal Places → Output the Rounded Value. For example, if you input 3.14159 and ask for 2 decimal places, the calculator will give you 3.14. It’s that straightforward!
