How Does It Work?

The Decimal to Barcode Converter works by taking each digit of your decimal number and assigning it a visual representation. Odd digits are displayed as thick bars, while even digits are shown as thin bars. This creates a unique barcode pattern that corresponds to your original number. It’s like translating numbers into a visual language—no coding or technical knowledge required!
