How It Works

The List Size-Aware Sorter is as simple as it gets. Here's the magic formula:

  1. Input Your List: Paste or type your list items, one per line, into the input box.
  2. Choose Your Order: Click "Sort by Size (Ascending)" to sort items from shortest to longest, or "Sort by Size (Descending)" for the opposite.
  3. Review the Results: The tool instantly reorganizes your list, showing each item alongside its size (number of characters).
