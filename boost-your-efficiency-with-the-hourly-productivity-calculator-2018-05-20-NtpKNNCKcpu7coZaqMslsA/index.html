Just plug in your tasks and time, and voilà—your productivity score appears! No more guesswork, just simple, actionable insights to help you stay on track (and maybe even impress your boss). Let’s make productivity feel less like a mystery and more like a win!

How It Works

The Hourly Productivity Calculator uses a straightforward formula to measure your efficiency: Productivity Score = Number of Tasks Completed ÷ Time Spent (in hours). For example, if you complete 10 tasks in 2 hours, your productivity score is 5 tasks per hour. It’s that simple! The tool ensures you get accurate, real-time feedback on how effectively you’re using your time.
