    How It Works

    The tool works by converting all your time durations into seconds, sorting them in descending order, and then converting them back to hours, minutes, and seconds. Here's a simple breakdown:

    • Step 1: Add your time durations in hours (HH), minutes (MM), and seconds (SS).
    • Step 2: Click "Sort" to organize them from longest to shortest.
    • Step 3: Reset and start fresh anytime!
