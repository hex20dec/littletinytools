How It Works

The Alphabet Sequence Counter works by breaking down your input into individual letters and tallying up how often each one appears. Think of it as a friendly librarian who sorts and counts every letter in your sequence. For example, if you type "HELLO," the tool will tell you that "H" appears once, "E" once, "L" twice, and "O" once. It's that simple!
