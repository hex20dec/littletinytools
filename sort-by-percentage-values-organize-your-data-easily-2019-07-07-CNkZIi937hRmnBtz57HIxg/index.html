Sort by Percentage Values

Why does organizing percentages feel like solving a puzzle? Whether it’s splitting tasks, tracking project progress, or comparing scores, percentages can quickly turn into a jumbled mess. Enter the Sort by Percentage Values tool—your quick and easy solution to sort items by their percentages in just a few clicks. Add your items, hit sort, and watch everything fall into place. No more headaches, no more confusion. Just simple, clean, and organized data. Life’s too short to wrestle with numbers—let this tool do the heavy lifting!

Enter items with their percentage values below, and sort them in ascending or descending order.

Item Percentage

No data is stored or shared. All calculations are done locally in your browser.

How It Works

The tool follows a straightforward process to sort your items by percentage:

  1. Add Items: Enter your items in the format "Item Name: Percentage" (e.g., "Project A: 75"). You can add as many items as you need.
  2. Choose Order: Decide whether you want to sort the items in ascending (lowest to highest) or descending (highest to lowest) order.
  3. Sort & View: Click the sort button, and the tool instantly organizes your data into a clear, easy-to-read table.
