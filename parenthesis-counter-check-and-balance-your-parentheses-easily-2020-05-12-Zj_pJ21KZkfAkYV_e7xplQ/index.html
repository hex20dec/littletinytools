How Does It Work?

The Parenthesis Counter works by scanning your text and counting the number of opening ( and closing ) parentheses. It then checks if the counts match. If they do, your parentheses are balanced! If not, it’ll let you know so you can fix them. No complex formulas or math required—just paste your text, click a button, and get instant results.
