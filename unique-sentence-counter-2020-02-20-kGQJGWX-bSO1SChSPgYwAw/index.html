How it Works

The Unique Sentence Counter employs a simple yet effective formula to identify and count unique sentences in a given text. The process involves the following steps:

  • Sentence Segmentation: The tool divides the input text into individual sentences based on punctuation marks such as periods, exclamation points, and question marks.
  • Sentence Normalization: Each sentence is then normalized by converting it to lowercase and trimming any leading or trailing whitespace.
  • Unique Sentence Identification: The normalized sentences are then compared to identify unique sentences, ignoring any duplicates.

The result is a list of unique sentences, along with their frequency count, providing valuable insights into the text's structure and content.
