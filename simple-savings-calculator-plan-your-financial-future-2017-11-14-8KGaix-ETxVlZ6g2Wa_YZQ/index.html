How It Works

The Simple Savings Calculator uses a straightforward formula to give you a clear picture of your financial future. Here’s the magic behind it:

  1. Initial Savings: Start with the amount you already have saved.
  2. Monthly Contributions: Add how much you plan to save every month.
  3. Annual Interest Rate: Enter the expected interest rate your savings will earn over time.
  4. Time Period: Specify how many years you’ll be saving.

The tool calculates your total savings by combining your initial amount, monthly contributions, and the interest earned over the years. It’s like compounding your confidence in your financial plans!
