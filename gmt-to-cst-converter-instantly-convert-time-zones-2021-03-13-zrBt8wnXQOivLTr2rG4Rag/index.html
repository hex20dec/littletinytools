How It Works

The GMT to CST Converter works by calculating the time difference between Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) and Central Standard Time (CST). Since CST is 6 hours behind GMT, the tool subtracts 6 hours from the GMT time you input. If the result goes below midnight, it wraps around to the previous day. For example, if you enter 3:00 AM GMT, the tool will display 9:00 PM CST from the previous day.
