How It Works

The Meal Cost Per Day Calculator is as simple as adding up your meal expenses. Here’s the formula it uses:

Total Daily Meal Cost = Breakfast + Lunch + Dinner + Snacks

Just enter the cost of each meal (or leave it blank if you skipped one), and the tool adds them up automatically. It even checks for errors, like negative numbers or non-numeric inputs, so you don’t have to worry about mistakes. It’s like having a personal accountant for your food budget!
