How It Works:

The Reverse Alphabetical Sorter takes your list of words or phrases, splits them into individual lines, and sorts them in reverse order (from Z to A). It’s like flipping a dictionary upside down! The tool ignores letter case, so "apple" and "Apple" are treated the same. Here’s a quick breakdown:

  • 1. Enter your words or phrases—one per line.
  • 2. Click "Sort" to reverse the order alphabetically.
  • 3. Copy the sorted list or reset to start fresh.
