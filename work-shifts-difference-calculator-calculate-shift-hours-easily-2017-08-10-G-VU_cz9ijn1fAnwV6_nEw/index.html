How It Works

The Work Shifts Difference Calculator works by comparing the total duration of two shifts. Here's the simple formula it uses:

  • 1. Convert the start and end times of each shift into total minutes.
  • 2. Subtract the total minutes of the shorter shift from the longer one.
  • 3. Convert the difference back into hours and minutes for an easy-to-understand result.

For example, if Shift 1 runs from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (8 hours) and Shift 2 runs from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM (6 hours), the tool calculates the difference as 2 hours. Easy, right?
