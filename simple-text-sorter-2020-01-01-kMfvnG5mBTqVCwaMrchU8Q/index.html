How the Text Sorter Works

The text sorter operates based on a straightforward principle: it takes the input text, breaks it down into individual lines, and then rearranges these lines in alphabetical order based on the first character of each line. This process is case-insensitive, ensuring that the sorting is not affected by the use of uppercase or lowercase letters.
