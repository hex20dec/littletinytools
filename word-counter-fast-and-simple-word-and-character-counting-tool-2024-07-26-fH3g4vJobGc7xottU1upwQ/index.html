The Word Counter tool works by analyzing your text in real-time. Here’s how it breaks it down:

  • Words: The tool splits your text into individual words by detecting spaces between them. It then counts each word to give you the total.
  • Characters: Every letter, number, space, and symbol in your text is counted, giving you the total number of characters.
