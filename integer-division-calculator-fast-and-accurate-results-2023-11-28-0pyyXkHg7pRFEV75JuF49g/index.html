How It Works

The Integer Division Calculator works by taking two numbers: the dividend (the number you want to divide) and the divisor (the number you’re dividing by). It then performs two simple calculations:

  • Quotient: The whole number result of dividing the dividend by the divisor.
  • Remainder: The leftover amount after the division is complete.

For example, if you divide 10 by 3, the quotient is 3 (since 3 fits into 10 three times), and the remainder is 1 (because 3 x 3 = 9, and 10 - 9 = 1). Easy, right?
