How It Works

The JST to CST Time Converter is as simple as it gets. Here's the magic formula in plain English:

  1. Enter the time in JST (Japan Standard Time) using the HH:MM format.
  2. Select whether it's AM or PM.
  3. Click "Convert" and voilà! The tool subtracts 15 hours (the time difference between JST and CST) and gives you the equivalent time in CST.

For example, if it's 9:00 PM in Tokyo, the tool will tell you it's 6:00 AM in Chicago. Easy peasy!
