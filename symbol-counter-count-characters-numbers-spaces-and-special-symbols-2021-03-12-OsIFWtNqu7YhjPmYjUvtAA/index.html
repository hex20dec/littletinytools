How It Works

The Symbol Counter works by analyzing your text in a few simple steps:

  1. Total Characters (Including Spaces): Counts every single character in your text, including spaces.
  2. Total Characters (Excluding Spaces): Removes spaces and counts only letters, numbers, and special characters.
  3. Letters: Counts only alphabetic characters (a-z, A-Z).
  4. Numbers: Counts only numeric digits (0-9).
  5. Spaces: Counts the number of spaces between words or characters.
  6. Special Characters: Counts everything else, like punctuation marks or symbols (!, @, #, etc.).

It’s like having a personal text detective that breaks down every detail for you!
