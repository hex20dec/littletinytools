Enter the cost of your clothing item and the number of times you've worn it to calculate the cost per wear.

Cost Per Wear:

How It Works

The Clothing Cost Per Wear Calculator uses a simple formula to determine how much each wear of your clothing item costs. Here's the breakdown:

Formula: Cost Per Wear = Total Cost of Item ÷ Number of Times Worn

For example, if you bought a pair of jeans for $100 and wore them 20 times, the cost per wear would be $5. The more you wear it, the lower the cost per wear becomes. It's a great way to evaluate whether your purchases are worth it!
