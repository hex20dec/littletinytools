How It Works

The Efficiency Percentage Calculator uses a straightforward formula to determine how close you are to achieving your maximum potential. Here’s the breakdown:

Efficiency Percentage = (Actual Output ÷ Theoretical Maximum Output) × 100

For example, if your actual output is 75 units and your theoretical maximum is 100 units, the efficiency percentage would be 75%. It’s like measuring how full your glass is compared to how full it could be!
