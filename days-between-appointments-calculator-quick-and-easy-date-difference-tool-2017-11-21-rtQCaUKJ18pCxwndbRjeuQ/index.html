How It Works: Simple as 1-2-3!

The Days Between Appointments Calculator uses a straightforward formula to give you the exact number of days between two dates. Here's how it works:

  1. Enter the first date (e.g., your last appointment).
  2. Enter the second date (e.g., your next appointment).
  3. Click "Calculate," and the tool does the rest—calculating the total days between the two dates.

Behind the scenes, the tool subtracts the first date from the second date and converts the result into days. It’s like having a personal assistant for date math!
