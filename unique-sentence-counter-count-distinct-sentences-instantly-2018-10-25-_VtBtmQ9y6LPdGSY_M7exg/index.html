How It Works

The Unique Sentence Counter works by breaking down your text into individual sentences using common punctuation marks like periods, exclamation points, and question marks. It then filters out duplicates, ensuring only unique sentences are counted. This process is quick, accurate, and hassle-free, giving you a clear picture of your text's structure.
