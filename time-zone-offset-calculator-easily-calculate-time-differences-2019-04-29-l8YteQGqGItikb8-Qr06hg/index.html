How It Works

The Time Zone Offset Calculator works by comparing the difference in hours and minutes between two selected time zones. Here’s the simple formula it uses:

Time Difference = (Offset of Time Zone 2) - (Offset of Time Zone 1)

Once the difference is calculated, the tool adjusts the entered time accordingly to show you the corresponding time in the second time zone. For example, if you enter 3:00 PM in EST (UTC-5) and compare it to IST (UTC+5.5), the tool will add 10.5 hours to give you the time in IST.
