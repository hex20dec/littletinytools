Here's how the magic happens:

The tool works by breaking your text into sentences using periods, exclamation marks, and question marks as guides. It then counts the total number of sentences and words. Finally, it divides the total words by the number of sentences to give you the average sentence length. Think of it like slicing a pizza—each sentence is a slice, and the words are the toppings. The tool simply tells you how "loaded" each slice is!
