How It Works

The Tab Counter uses your browser's built-in capabilities to count the number of open tabs in your current window. It’s as simple as this:

  • Step 1: Click the "Refresh Count" button.
  • Step 2: The tool instantly checks your browser window and displays the total number of open tabs.
  • Step 3: If something goes wrong (like a browser limitation), you’ll see a friendly error message prompting you to try again.

It’s fast, accurate, and designed to work seamlessly in your browser. No installations, no hassle—just a quick way to keep track of your tabs.
