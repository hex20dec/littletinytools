How It Works

The tool calculates the difference between two calendar eras by converting the selected date into both systems and then comparing them. Here's the breakdown:

  • Step 1: Select a date and two calendar eras (e.g., Gregorian and Hijri).
  • Step 2: The tool converts the date into both selected eras.
  • Step 3: It calculates the difference in days, months, and years between the two converted dates.
