Unit Price Calculator

The Unit Price Calculator is a handy online tool designed to help consumers make informed purchase decisions by comparing the prices of different products based on their unit prices. Whether you're shopping for groceries, household essentials, or any other product, this calculator provides a quick and easy way to determine which option offers the best value for your money, taking into account the price and quantity of each item, and expressing the result in a convenient unit of measurement.

How the Unit Price Calculator Works

The calculator uses a simple formula to determine the unit price of a product: Unit Price = Total Price / Quantity. This formula allows you to easily compare the prices of different products, even if they have different quantities or units of measurement.
