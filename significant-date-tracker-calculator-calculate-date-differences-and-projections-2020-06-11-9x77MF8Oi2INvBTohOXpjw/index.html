How It Works

The Significant Date Tracker Calculator works by taking your input dates and performing two key functions:

  1. Date Difference: It calculates the exact number of days, weeks, months, or years between two dates. For example, if you input your birthday and today’s date, it’ll tell you exactly how old you are—down to the day!
  2. Date Projection: It lets you add or subtract a specific amount of time (days, weeks, months, or years) from a given date. For instance, if you want to know what the date will be 90 days from now, this tool will give you the answer instantly.

It’s like having a personal time wizard in your pocket—no magic spells required!
