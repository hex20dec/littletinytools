How It Works

Here’s the magic behind the Rounding Calculator: you provide a number and the number of decimal places you want it rounded to. The tool then adjusts the number to match your requested precision. For example, if you enter 3.14159 and ask for 2 decimal places, the calculator will give you 3.14. It’s that simple!
