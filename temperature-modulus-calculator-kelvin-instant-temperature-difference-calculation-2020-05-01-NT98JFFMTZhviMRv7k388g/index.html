Calculate the modulus of a temperature difference in Kelvin.

The modulus of a temperature difference represents the absolute difference between two temperatures.

How the Calculator Works

The calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the modulus of the temperature difference: Modulus = |Temperature 1 - Temperature 2|, where Temperature 1 and Temperature 2 are the two temperatures in Kelvin. This formula ensures that the result is always a positive value, representing the absolute difference between the two temperatures.
