How It Works

The Title Case Word Counter uses a straightforward formula to transform your text. Here’s the magic behind the scenes:

  • Step 1: The tool takes your input text and converts it to lowercase to ensure consistency.
  • Step 2: It then capitalizes the first letter of every word, except for small connecting words like "and," "the," or "of."
  • Step 3: Finally, it counts the number of words in your text, so you don’t have to.

It’s like having a personal editor in your pocket!
