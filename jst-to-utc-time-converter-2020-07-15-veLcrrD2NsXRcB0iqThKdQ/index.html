How the JST to UTC Time Converter Works

The conversion from JST to UTC involves subtracting 9 hours from the JST time. This can be represented by the following formula: UTC Time = (JST Time - 9 hours). For example, if it is 12:00 PM JST, the equivalent UTC time would be 3:00 AM.
