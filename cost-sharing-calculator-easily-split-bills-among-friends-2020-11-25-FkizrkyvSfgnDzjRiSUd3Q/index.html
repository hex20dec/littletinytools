Whether you're splitting a dinner bill, planning a group vacation, or organizing a joint purchase, this tool ensures everyone pays their fair share. Say goodbye to awkward money talks and hello to stress-free cost splitting.

Each Person Owes:

The Cost Sharing Calculator works by dividing the total cost equally among the group or allowing you to assign custom shares. Here's how it works:

  • Equal Split: Enter the total cost and the number of people. The tool divides the amount equally, so everyone pays the same.
  • Custom Shares: Enable custom shares to assign specific amounts to each person. The tool ensures the sum matches the total cost.
