How It Works

The Square Root Calculator works by taking the number you input and finding the value that, when multiplied by itself, equals that number. For example, the square root of 16 is 4 because 4 × 4 = 16. It’s a straightforward process, but it’s made even simpler with this tool. Just enter your number, click the button, and the calculator does the rest.
