How the Division Calculator Works

The division process involves dividing a number (the dividend) by another number (the divisor) to find the quotient and remainder. This can be represented by the formula: Dividend = Divisor * Quotient + Remainder. The calculator takes the dividend and divisor as inputs and then calculates the quotient by dividing the dividend by the divisor. If there's a remainder, it's also displayed alongside the quotient.
