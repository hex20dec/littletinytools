How It Works

Converting CST to AEDT is as easy as 1-2-3! Since Central Standard Time (CST) is 17 hours behind Australian Eastern Daylight Time (AEDT) during daylight saving, all you need to do is add 17 hours to your CST time. For example, if it’s 2:30 PM CST, adding 17 hours brings you to 7:30 AM AEDT the next day. Our tool does all the heavy lifting for you—just input the time in the correct format, and it’ll handle the rest.
