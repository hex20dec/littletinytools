Enter a decimal number to see its cuneiform representation.

Cuneiform numerals are based on the ancient Mesopotamian system. This tool is for educational purposes only.

The Decimal to Cuneiform Converter works by taking each digit of your input number and mapping it to its corresponding cuneiform symbol.
