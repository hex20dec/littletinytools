Decimal to Cuneiform Converter

Whether you’re a history buff, a teacher looking for a fun classroom activity, or just someone who loves quirky tools, this converter transforms ordinary numbers into the wedge-shaped symbols used by the Mesopotamians thousands of years ago. It’s as simple as typing in a number and watching it come to life in cuneiform. Who knew math could be this cool?

Enter a decimal number to see its cuneiform representation.

Cuneiform numerals are based on the ancient Mesopotamian system. This tool is for educational purposes only.

The Decimal to Cuneiform Converter works by taking each digit of your input number and mapping it to its corresponding cuneiform symbol. For example, the number 123 is broken down into 1, 2, and 3, which are then converted into their respective cuneiform symbols: 𒐖, 𒐗, and 𒐘.
