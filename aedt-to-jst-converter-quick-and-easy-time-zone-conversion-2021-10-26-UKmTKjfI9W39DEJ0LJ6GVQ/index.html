Note: AEDT is UTC+11, JST is UTC+9. AEDT is 2 hours ahead of JST.

How It Works

The AEDT to JST Converter works by calculating the time difference between Australian Eastern Daylight Time (AEDT) and Japan Standard Time (JST). Since AEDT is 2 hours ahead of JST, the tool subtracts 2 hours from your input AEDT time. For example, if it's 15:00 (3:00 PM) in AEDT, it becomes 13:00 (1:00 PM) in JST. It’s that simple!
