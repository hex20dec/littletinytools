Enter the base and height of the triangle to calculate its area.

The Triangle Area Calculator works using a simple formula: Area = (Base × Height) ÷ 2. Here’s how it breaks down:

  • Base: The length of the triangle’s bottom side.
  • Height: The perpendicular distance from the base to the top vertex.
  • Area: The total space inside the triangle, calculated by multiplying the base and height, then dividing by 2.

For example, if your triangle has a base of 10 units and a height of 5 units, the area would be (10 × 5) ÷ 2 = 25 square units. Easy, right?
