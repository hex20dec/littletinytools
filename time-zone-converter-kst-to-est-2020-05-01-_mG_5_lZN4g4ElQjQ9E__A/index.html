How it Works

The conversion from KST to EST involves subtracting 13 hours from the KST time. This can be represented by the formula: EST = (KST - 13) mod 24, where mod denotes the modulo operation to ensure the result falls within a 24-hour range. For example, if it's 10:00 AM KST, you would subtract 13 hours to get 9:00 PM EST the previous day.
