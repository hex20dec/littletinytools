How it Works

The Kelvin to Celsius Converter Tool uses a simple formula to convert temperatures from Kelvin to Celsius: Celsius = Kelvin - 273.15. This formula is based on the definition of the Celsius scale, which is a temperature scale that is derived from the Kelvin scale.
