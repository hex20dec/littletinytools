How It Works

The Whitespace Character Counter works by scanning your text for all types of whitespace characters—spaces, tabs, and line breaks. Here's the simple formula it follows:

Total Whitespace = Number of Spaces + Number of Tabs + Number of Line Breaks

For example, if you input the text "Hello World\nThis is a test.", the tool will count:

  • 1 space between "Hello" and "World"
  • 1 line break after "World"
  • 3 spaces in "This is a test."
