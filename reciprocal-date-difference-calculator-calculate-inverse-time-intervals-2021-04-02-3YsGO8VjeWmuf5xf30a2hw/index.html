How It Works

The tool calculates the difference between two dates and then finds the reciprocal of that difference. Here’s the formula in plain English:

  1. Pick a start date and an end date.
  2. The tool calculates the total number of days between them.
  3. It then flips that number upside down (mathematically speaking) to give you the reciprocal. For example, if the difference is 10 days, the reciprocal is 1/10, or 0.1.
