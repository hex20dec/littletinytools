How Does It Work?

The Exclamation Mark Counter works like a diligent proofreader. It scans your text line by line, looking for every instance of the exclamation mark symbol (!). Once it finds one, it adds it to the count. The formula is simple:

Total Exclamation Marks = Number of "!" in your text

For example, if your text says, "Wow!! That’s amazing!!!", the tool will count 5 exclamation marks. It’s that straightforward!
