Convert timestamps in your CSV file to a different format quickly and easily.

How It Works

This tool follows a simple process to transform your CSV timestamps:

  1. Upload your CSV file: The tool reads your file locally, ensuring your data stays private.
  2. Select the current timestamp format: Choose from common formats or specify a custom one if needed.
  3. Choose the desired timestamp format: Pick how you want your timestamps to look after conversion.
  4. Convert and download: The tool processes your file and provides a downloadable CSV with updated timestamps.

It’s like having a translator for your timestamps—no coding or technical skills required!
