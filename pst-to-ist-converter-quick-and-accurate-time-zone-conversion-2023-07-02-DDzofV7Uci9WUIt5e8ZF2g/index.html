How Does It Work?

Converting PST to IST is all about adding the right number of hours and minutes. Since Indian Standard Time (IST) is 13 hours and 30 minutes ahead of Pacific Standard Time (PST), our tool does the heavy lifting for you. Here’s the simple formula:

IST = PST + 13 hours and 30 minutes

For example, if it’s 10:00 AM PST, adding 13.5 hours gives you 11:30 PM IST. No need to stress about AM/PM conversions or time differences—just type in your PST time, and we’ll handle the rest!
