How It Works

The tool breaks down your text into paragraphs and calculates the average word count per paragraph. Here's the simple formula it follows:

  • Step 1: Count the number of paragraphs by splitting the text at line breaks.
  • Step 2: Count the total number of words by splitting the text at spaces.
  • Step 3: Divide the total word count by the number of paragraphs to get the average words per paragraph.

It's that straightforward! No complex algorithms, no hidden steps—just clear, actionable insights to help you refine your writing.
