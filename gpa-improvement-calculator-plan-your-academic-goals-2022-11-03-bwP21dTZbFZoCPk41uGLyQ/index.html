How It Works

The tool uses a simple formula to calculate the GPA you need in your next course to reach your desired overall GPA. Here’s the breakdown:

  1. Multiply your desired GPA by the total credit hours you’ll have after completing your next course.
  2. Subtract the total quality points you’ve earned so far (current GPA × completed credit hours).
  3. Divide the result by the credit hours of your next course (assumed to be 1 in this case).

Voilà! You’ve got the GPA you need to aim for. It’s like a math problem, but way more fun because it’s about your future.
