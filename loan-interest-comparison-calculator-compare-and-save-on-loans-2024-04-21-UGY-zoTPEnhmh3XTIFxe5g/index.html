How It Works

The Loan Interest Comparison Calculator uses a straightforward formula to calculate the total interest paid and the total repayment amount for two different loan scenarios. Here’s the breakdown:

  • Total Interest Paid = Loan Amount × (Interest Rate / 100) × Loan Term
  • Total Repayment Amount = Loan Amount + Total Interest Paid

This formula assumes simple interest, meaning it doesn’t account for compounding or additional fees. It’s perfect for quick comparisons without overcomplicating things.
