How It Works

This tool takes any temperature in Celsius and converts it into scientific notation. Scientific notation is a way to express very large or very small numbers in a compact form. Here's the formula in simple terms:

Celsius Temperature → Scientific Notation

For example, if you input 25.5, the tool will convert it to 2.55e+1, which means 2.55 multiplied by 10 to the power of 1. Similarly, -10.75 becomes -1.075e+1.
