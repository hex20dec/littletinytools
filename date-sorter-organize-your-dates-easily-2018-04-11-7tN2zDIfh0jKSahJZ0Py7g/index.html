Date Sorter

Organize Your Dates Effortlessly

Ever found yourself staring at a messy list of dates, unsure where to start? Whether you're planning events, tracking deadlines, or organizing historical data, sorting dates can feel like untangling headphones—frustrating and time-consuming. That's where the Date Sorter comes in. This nifty tool takes your jumble of dates, sorts them in seconds, and gives you a clean, organized list. Just paste your dates, choose your order (oldest to newest or vice versa), and voilà—your life just got a little simpler. No more manual sorting, no more headaches. It’s like having a personal assistant for your dates!

Enter your dates below, choose the sorting order, and get your sorted list instantly.

This tool does not store or share your data. All processing happens in your browser.

How It Works

The Date Sorter works like a digital filing cabinet. Here’s the magic behind it:

  1. Input Your Dates: Paste or type your dates (one per line) in the provided text box. Make sure they follow the YYYY-MM-DD format for seamless sorting.
  2. Choose Your Order: Select whether you want your dates sorted from oldest to newest (ascending) or newest to oldest (descending).
  3. Hit Sort: Click the "Sort Dates" button, and the tool instantly organizes your dates in the desired order.
  4. Copy and Go: Once sorted, you can copy the results to your clipboard with a single click, ready to use wherever you need them.

It’s that simple—no complicated steps, no hidden catches. Just clean, organized dates in seconds.
