Keyword Highlighter

Ever Lost in a Sea of Text? Find What Matters in Seconds!

We've all been there—scrolling through endless paragraphs, trying to spot that one important word or phrase. Whether it’s a lengthy report, a research paper, or even your grocery list, finding keywords can feel like searching for a needle in a haystack. But what if you could instantly highlight every important term with just a few clicks? Enter the Keyword Highlighter—a simple, no-fuss tool that turns your text into a colorful map of key ideas. Paste your text, add your keywords, and watch as they light up like neon signs on a dark road. It’s like giving your brain a pair of super-powered glasses. Say goodbye to skimming fatigue and hello to effortless clarity!

Enter your text and keywords to highlight all occurrences.

How It Works (No Tech Jargon, Promise!)

Think of the Keyword Highlighter as your personal text detective. Here’s the simple formula it follows:

  1. Step 1: You paste or type your text into the box.
  2. Step 2: Add the keywords you want to highlight, separated by commas.
  3. Step 3: Click "Highlight," and voilà! Every instance of your keywords is instantly marked in bright colors.

It’s like magic, but without the wand—just clever coding and a whole lot of user-friendly design.
