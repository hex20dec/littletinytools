How It Works

The Investment Return Calculator uses a straightforward formula to predict your financial growth. Here’s the breakdown:

Final Amount = Initial Investment × (1 + Annual Return Rate) ^ Investment Duration

This formula calculates the total amount you’ll have after a certain number of years, including both your initial investment and the returns earned over time.
