Shortest Paragraph

Here’s how the Shortest Paragraph Counter works in simple terms: it scans your text, splits it into individual paragraphs (separated by line breaks), and then counts the number of words in each one. The paragraph with the fewest words is crowned the winner! Think of it as a word-counting detective that zeroes in on the most concise part of your text. Below is a quick example to show you how it works:

Paragraph Word Count

Here’s another one, slightly longer but still manageable. 8
