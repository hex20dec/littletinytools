Sort by Field

Sort Your Lists Like a Pro

Ever found yourself staring at a messy list of data, wishing it could magically organize itself? Whether it’s a spreadsheet of names, ages, or cities, sorting by a specific field can feel like solving a puzzle. That’s where this tool comes in! Simply paste your list, pick the field you want to sort by, and watch everything fall into place—ascending or descending, your call. No more manual sorting headaches or endless scrolling. This tool is your shortcut to cleaner, more organized data, making life just a little easier.

Enter your list and sort it by a specific field.

How It Works

The tool works by breaking down your list into rows and columns. Here’s the step-by-step magic:

  1. Enter your list in the input box, with each item on a new line and fields separated by commas.
  2. Specify the field you want to sort by (e.g., "Age" or "City").
  3. Choose whether you want the data sorted in ascending or descending order.
  4. Click the "Sort List" button, and voilà—your data is neatly organized!

It’s like having a personal assistant for your data, without the coffee breaks.
