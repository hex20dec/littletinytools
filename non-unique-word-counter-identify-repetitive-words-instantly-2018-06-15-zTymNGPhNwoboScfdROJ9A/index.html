The Non-Unique Word Counter works by scanning your text and identifying words that appear more than once. It uses a straightforward formula:

  1. Extract Words: Breaks your text into individual words, ignoring punctuation and capitalization.
  2. Count Occurrences: Tracks how many times each word appears.
  3. Filter Non-Unique Words: Highlights only the words that are repeated, along with their counts.
