How it Works

The Average Speed Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate your average speed: average speed = total distance / total time. This means that if you travel a certain distance in a given amount of time, your average speed is the total distance divided by the total time. For example, if you travel 100 kilometers in 2 hours, your average speed is 50 kilometers per hour.
