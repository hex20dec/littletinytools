PST to AEDT Time Zone Converter

Whether you’re planning a virtual coffee catch-up or a business call, this tool makes it as easy as pie. No more math, no more confusion—just smooth, stress-free time zone conversions!

Enter a time in PST to see the corresponding time in AEDT.

Note: AEDT is 19 hours ahead of PST during daylight saving time.

How It Works

The PST to AEDT Time Zone Converter works by adding 19 hours to the Pacific Standard Time (PST) you input. This accounts for the time difference between PST and Australian Eastern Daylight Time (AEDT) during daylight saving time. For example, if it's 10:00 AM in PST, the tool calculates 19 hours ahead and shows you that it's 5:00 AM the next day in AEDT. It’s that simple!
