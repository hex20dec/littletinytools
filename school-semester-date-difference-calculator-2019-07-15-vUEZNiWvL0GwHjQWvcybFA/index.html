How it Works

The calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the date difference: it subtracts the start date from the end date and then converts the result into the desired format (days, weeks, or months). This formula is based on the number of milliseconds in a day, which is then divided by the number of days in a week or month to get the corresponding value.
