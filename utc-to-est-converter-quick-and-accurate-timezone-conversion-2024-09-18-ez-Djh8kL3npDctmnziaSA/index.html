How It Works

This tool takes a UTC timestamp (in the format YYYY-MM-DDTHH:MM:SS) and converts it to Eastern Time (EST or EDT). It automatically adjusts for daylight saving time, so you don’t have to worry about whether it’s summer or winter. Here’s the simple formula:

UTC Timestamp + Time Zone Offset = Eastern Time

For example, if the UTC timestamp is 2023-10-15T14:30:00, the tool will convert it to 10:30:00 AM EDT during daylight saving time or 09:30:00 AM EST otherwise.
