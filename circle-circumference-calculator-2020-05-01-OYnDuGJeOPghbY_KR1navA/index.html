How it Works

The Circle Circumference Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the circumference of a circle: C = 2πr or C = πd, where C is the circumference, π is a mathematical constant representing the ratio of a circle's circumference to its diameter, and r is the radius or d is the diameter of the circle.
