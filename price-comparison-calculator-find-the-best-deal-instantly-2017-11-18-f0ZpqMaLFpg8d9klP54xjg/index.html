Price Comparison Calculator

Ever stood in the grocery aisle, calculator in hand, trying to figure out which bulk pack of cereal is actually the better deal? Or maybe you’ve spent way too much time comparing prices online, only to realize you’re more confused than when you started. That’s where the Price Comparison Calculator comes in—your personal shopping sidekick. This nifty tool takes the guesswork out of comparing prices by instantly calculating the total cost of up to three items, factoring in both price and quantity. Whether you’re shopping for groceries, office supplies, or even DIY materials, this calculator ensures you’re making the smartest choice without breaking a sweat. Say goodbye to mental math and hello to smarter shopping!

How It Works

The Price Comparison Calculator works by multiplying the price of each item by its quantity to determine the total cost. It then compares the total costs of up to three items and ranks them from the most to the least cost-effective option. Here’s a simple example:

Item Name Price ($) Quantity Total Cost ($)
Item A 5.00 2 10.00
Item B 3.50 4 14.00
Item C 2.00 6 12.00

In this example, the calculator would rank Item A as the most cost-effective option, followed by Item C and then Item B.
