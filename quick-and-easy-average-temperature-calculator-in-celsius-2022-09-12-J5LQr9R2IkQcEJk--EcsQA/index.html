How Does It Work?

The tool uses a simple formula to calculate the average temperature. Here's how it works in plain terms:

  1. You enter your temperature values in Celsius, separated by commas (e.g., 25, 30, 22, 18).
  2. The tool adds up all the values you entered.
  3. It then divides the total by the number of values to give you the average temperature.
