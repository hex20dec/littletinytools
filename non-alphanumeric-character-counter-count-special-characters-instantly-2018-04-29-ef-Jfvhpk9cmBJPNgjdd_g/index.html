How It Works

The tool scans your text and identifies everything that isn’t a letter (A-Z, a-z) or a number (0-9). This includes spaces, punctuation marks, and symbols like @, #, $, %, and more. Here’s the breakdown:

  1. Paste or type your text into the input box.
  2. Click the “Count Characters” button.
  3. The tool instantly calculates and displays the number of non-alphanumeric characters.

For example, if your text is “Hello, world!”, the tool will count the comma and exclamation mark—resulting in 2 non-alphanumeric characters. Simple, right?
