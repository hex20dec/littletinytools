Sort by First Occurrence

Sorting a list of items but want to keep only the first occurrence of each? This tool is your lifesaver! Whether you're organizing a grocery list, cleaning up duplicate entries, or managing data for a project, our Sort by First Occurrence tool makes it effortless. Simply paste your items, click the button, and voilà—no more duplicates cluttering your list. It’s like magic, but without the wand. Perfect for perfectionists and procrastinators alike!

How It Works

The tool works by scanning your list of items and identifying the first time each item appears. It then creates a new list, preserving only those first occurrences. Think of it as a bouncer at a club—only the first guest with a specific name gets in, and the rest are turned away. No duplicates, no fuss!
