How It Works

Think of this tool as your data chef. Here’s the recipe:

  1. Upload your CSV file: Like handing over the ingredients.
  2. Select the columns to merge: Pick the two columns you want to combine.
  3. Name your new column: Give it a title that makes sense for your data.
  4. Merge and download: The tool combines your columns and gives you a fresh CSV file to use.
