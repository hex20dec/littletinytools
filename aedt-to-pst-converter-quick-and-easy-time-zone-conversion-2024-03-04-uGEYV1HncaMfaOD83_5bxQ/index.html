How Does It Work?

Converting AEDT (Australian Eastern Daylight Time) to PST (Pacific Standard Time) is all about accounting for the time difference. AEDT is UTC+11, while PST is UTC-8. That means there's a 19-hour gap between the two. Here's the simple formula:

PST Time = AEDT Time - 19 hours

If the result goes below 0, we simply add 24 hours to keep it within a single day. Easy, right? Here's a quick reference table to see how some common times convert:
