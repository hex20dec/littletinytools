How the Net Worth Calculator Works

The Net Worth Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate an individual's net worth: Net Worth = Total Assets - Total Liabilities. This formula is based on the principle that an individual's net worth is equal to the total value of their assets minus the total amount of their liabilities.

For example, if an individual has $100,000 in assets (such as cash, investments, and real estate) and $50,000 in liabilities (such as credit card debt and loans), their net worth would be $50,000 ($100,000 - $50,000).
