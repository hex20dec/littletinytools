How It Works

The Minutes Difference Calculator works by converting your start and end times into total minutes, then subtracting the two to give you the difference. Here's the breakdown in plain English:

  1. Enter your Start Time and End Time in the HH:MM format (e.g., 09:15 for 9:15 AM).
  2. The tool converts each time into total minutes since midnight (e.g., 09:15 becomes 555 minutes).
  3. It calculates the absolute difference between the two times, giving you the total minutes elapsed.
  4. Voilà! You get the difference in minutes, displayed in bold for your convenience.
