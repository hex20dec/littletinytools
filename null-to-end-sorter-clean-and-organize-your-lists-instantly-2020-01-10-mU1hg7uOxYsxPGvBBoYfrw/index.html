How It Works

The Null-to-End Sorter follows a simple yet effective process:

  1. Input Your List: Paste or type your list into the input box, with each item on a new line.
  2. Separate Non-Empty and Empty Items: The tool identifies all non-empty items and keeps them in their original order. Empty lines are set aside for now.
  3. Combine and Sort: The non-empty items are placed at the top, followed by the empty lines at the bottom. Your list is now clean and organized!
