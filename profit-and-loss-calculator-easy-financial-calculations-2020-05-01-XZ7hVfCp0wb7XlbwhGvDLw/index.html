Profit and Loss Calculator

The Profit and Loss Calculator is a free online tool designed to help individuals and businesses calculate their financial gains or losses from a transaction. By entering the cost price and selling price of a product or service, users can easily determine whether they have made a profit or incurred a loss, as well as calculate the percentage gain or loss. This calculator is an essential tool for anyone involved in buying and selling, including entrepreneurs, investors, and small business owners, and is a valuable resource for understanding the financial implications of business decisions.

How it Works

The Profit and Loss Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the financial gain or loss from a transaction. The formula is: Profit = Selling Price - Cost Price, and Loss = Cost Price - Selling Price. By plugging in the cost price and selling price, users can calculate the profit or loss, as well as the percentage gain or loss. For example, if the cost price is $100 and the selling price is $120, the profit would be $20, and the percentage gain would be 20%.
