How It Works

Our tool calculates the hours since midnight for two different times and then finds the difference between them. Here’s the magic behind it:

  1. You enter two times in the format HH:MM AM/PM (e.g., 09:30 AM or 03:45 PM).
  2. The tool converts each time into total hours since midnight. For example, 09:30 AM becomes 9.5 hours, and 03:45 PM becomes 15.75 hours.
  3. It then calculates the absolute difference between the two values, giving you the result in hours.
