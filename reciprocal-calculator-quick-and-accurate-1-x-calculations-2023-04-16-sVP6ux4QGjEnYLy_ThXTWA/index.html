Enter a number to calculate its reciprocal (1/x).

How It Works

The reciprocal of a number is simply 1 divided by that number. For example, the reciprocal of 5 is 1/5, which equals 0.2. This tool takes any number you input, flips it, and gives you the result instantly. It’s that straightforward!
