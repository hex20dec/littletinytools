Here’s how it works: The tool analyzes your text by splitting it into individual words and then checking each word for hyphens. If a word contains a hyphen (like "mother-in-law" or "eco-friendly"), it gets counted. The total number of hyphenated words is displayed instantly. It’s that simple!

For example, if your text is:

"The state-of-the-art technology is user-friendly and eco-conscious."

The tool will count 3 hyphenated words: "state-of-the-art," "user-friendly," and "eco-conscious."
