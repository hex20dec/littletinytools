How It Works

The IST to AEDT Time Converter works by adding 5.5 hours to the Indian Standard Time (IST) you input. Here's a quick breakdown:

  • Step 1: Enter the time in IST (e.g., 10:30 AM).
  • Step 2: The tool calculates the equivalent AEDT by adding 5.5 hours.
  • Step 3: Voilà! You get the corresponding AEDT time.

For example, if it's 10:30 AM in India, the tool will show 4:00 PM in AEDT. It’s that simple!
