How It Works

The tool works by breaking down your decimal number into parts that match specific Roman numeral values. It starts with the largest Roman numeral (M for 1000) and works its way down to the smallest (I for 1). Each part of your number is replaced with its Roman numeral equivalent, and the results are combined to give you the final answer.
