How It Works:

Calculating paint coverage is easier than you think. Here’s the formula the tool uses:

  • Total Area: Multiply the width of your wall by its height (in feet).
  • Total Paint Needed: Multiply the total area by the number of coats, then divide by the paint's coverage rate (how many square feet one gallon covers).

For example, if your wall is 10 feet wide and 8 feet tall, and your paint covers 350 square feet per gallon, here’s how it breaks down:

Width (ft) Height (ft) Coverage Rate (sq ft/gallon) Coats Paint Required (gallons)
10 8 350 1 0.23
10 8 350 2 0.46

See? It’s as simple as measuring, multiplying, and dividing!
