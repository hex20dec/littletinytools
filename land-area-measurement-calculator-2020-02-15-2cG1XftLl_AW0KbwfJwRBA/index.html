How the Land Area Measurement Calculator Works

The calculator uses simple geometric formulas to calculate the area of each shape. The formulas are as follows:

  • Rectangle: Area = Length x Width
  • Square: Area = Side x Side
  • Circle: Area = π x Radius x Radius
  • Triangle: Area = 0.5 x Base x Height

The calculator also allows users to select from different units of measurement, including meters, feet, yards, and acres. This makes it easy to convert between different units and ensures that the calculations are accurate and relevant to the user's specific needs.
