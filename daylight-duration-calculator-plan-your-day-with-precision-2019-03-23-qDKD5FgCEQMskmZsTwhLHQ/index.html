How It Works

The tool calculates the difference between sunrise and sunset times for a specific date and location. Here's the simple formula it uses:

Daylight Duration = Sunset Time - Sunrise Time

For example, if the sun rises at 6 AM and sets at 8 PM, the daylight duration would be 14 hours. The tool automatically adjusts for different locations and dates, so you don’t have to worry about the math!
