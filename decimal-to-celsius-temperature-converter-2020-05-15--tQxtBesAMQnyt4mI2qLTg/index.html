How it Works

The conversion formula used in this tool is based on the principle that to convert decimal temperatures to Celsius, you subtract 32 from the decimal temperature and then multiply the result by 5/9. This can be represented by the formula: Celsius Temperature = (Decimal Temperature - 32) * 5/9.
