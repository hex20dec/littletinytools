Tab Counter App - Boost Your Productivity

Tab Counter

The Tab Counter App is a productivity tool designed to help users manage their browser tabs efficiently. By providing a simple and intuitive interface, this app enables users to keep track of their open tabs, set custom limits, and receive warnings when exceeding those limits. With its user-friendly features, the Tab Counter App aims to boost productivity and organization for individuals who work with multiple tabs open simultaneously, making it an essential browser extension for anyone looking to streamline their workflow.

How it Works

The Tab Counter App utilizes a straightforward formula to track and manage open tabs: Current Tab Count = Total Number of Open Tabs. This formula allows users to monitor their tab count in real-time, enabling them to make informed decisions about their browser organization.

Tab Count Warning Status

6-10 Warning: approaching custom limit
11+ Warning: exceeded custom limit
