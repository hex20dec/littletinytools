How It Works

The Circle Diameter Calculator uses simple geometry formulas to determine the diameter of a circle based on the information you provide. Here's how it breaks down:

  • From Radius: Diameter = 2 × Radius
  • From Circumference: Diameter = Circumference ÷ π (approximately 3.1416)
  • From Area: Diameter = 2 × √(Area ÷ π)

For example, if you know the radius is 5 units, the diameter is simply 10 units. It’s that straightforward!
