How It Works

This tool uses a straightforward formula to convert Celsius to Kelvin. The magic lies in adding 273.15 to the Celsius temperature. Why 273.15? Because that’s the exact difference between the two scales’ starting points. For example, 0°C (the freezing point of water) equals 273.15K. Here’s a quick look at how it works:

Kelvin = Celsius + 273.15
