The First and Last Day Difference Calculator is a web-based tool designed to help users calculate the difference between two dates. This calculator takes into account the time period between the first and last day, providing the result in days, weeks, months, and years. It's a useful tool for event planners, project managers, and anyone looking to track time periods. With its simple and intuitive interface, users can easily calculate the difference between two dates and plan their events or projects accordingly.
