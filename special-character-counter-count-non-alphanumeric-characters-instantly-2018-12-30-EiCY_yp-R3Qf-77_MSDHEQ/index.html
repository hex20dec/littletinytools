How It Works

The Special Character Counter works by scanning your text and identifying all characters that aren’t letters (A-Z, a-z), numbers (0-9), or spaces. It then gives you a total count of these unique symbols. Here’s a simplified breakdown:

Total Special Characters = All Characters - (Letters + Numbers + Spaces)

For example, if you type "Hello, World! #2023", the tool will count the comma, exclamation mark, and hashtag as special characters.
