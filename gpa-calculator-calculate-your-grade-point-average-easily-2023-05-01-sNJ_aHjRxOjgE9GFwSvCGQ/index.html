How It Works

The GPA Calculator uses a simple formula to determine your Grade Point Average (GPA):

GPA = (Sum of (Grade Points × Credit Hours)) ÷ Total Credit Hours

Here's how it breaks down:

  • For each course, multiply the grade points (A = 4.0, B = 3.0, etc.) by the credit hours.
  • Add up all these values to get the total quality points.
  • Divide the total quality points by the total number of credit hours to get your GPA.

In this example, the GPA would be 26.0 ÷ 9 = 2.89.
