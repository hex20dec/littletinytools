How It Works

The area of a trapezoid is calculated using a straightforward formula: Area = ½ × (Base 1 + Base 2) × Height. Imagine you’re building a garden bed in the shape of a trapezoid. You measure the two parallel sides (bases) and the distance between them (height). Plug these numbers into the calculator, and it instantly gives you the area you need to plan your project. No math anxiety required—just simple, reliable results.
