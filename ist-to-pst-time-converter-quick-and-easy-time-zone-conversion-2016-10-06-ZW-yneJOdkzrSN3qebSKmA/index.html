How Does It Work?

The IST to PST Time Converter works by calculating the time difference between Indian Standard Time (IST) and Pacific Standard Time (PST). Since PST is 13 hours and 30 minutes behind IST, the tool simply subtracts this difference from the IST time you provide. For example, if it’s 10:30 AM in IST, the converter will show 9:00 PM of the previous day in PST. It’s like having a tiny time-travel machine at your fingertips!
