How it Works

The Binary to Decimal Converter uses a simple formula to convert binary numbers to decimal: decimal = binary[0] * 2^(n-1) + binary[1] * 2^(n-2) + ... + binary[n-1] * 2^0, where n is the number of bits in the binary number.
