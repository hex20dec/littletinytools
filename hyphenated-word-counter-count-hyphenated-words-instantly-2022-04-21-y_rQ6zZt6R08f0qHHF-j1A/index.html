How It Works

The Hyphenated Word Counter uses a straightforward approach to identify and count hyphenated words in your text. Here’s the magic behind it:

  1. It scans your text for words connected by hyphens, like "mother-in-law" or "self-confidence."
  2. It groups these words together and counts them, ensuring even complex hyphenated phrases (like "up-to-date") are included.
  3. Finally, it displays the total number of hyphenated words in your text—simple, fast, and accurate.

For example, if you paste the sentence, "The state-of-the-art technology is user-friendly and eco-friendly," the tool will count 3 hyphenated words.
