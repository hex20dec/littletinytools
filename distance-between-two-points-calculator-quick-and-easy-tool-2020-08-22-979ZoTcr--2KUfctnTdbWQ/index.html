How It Works

The tool uses a straightforward mathematical formula to calculate the straight-line distance between two points on a plane. Here’s the secret sauce:

  • 1. Find the difference between the x-coordinates of the two points.
  • 2. Find the difference between the y-coordinates of the two points.
  • 3. Square both differences and add them together.
  • 4. Take the square root of the sum, and voilà—you’ve got the distance!
