How It Works

The Final Grade Calculator uses a straightforward formula to determine the score you need on your final exam or assignment. Here’s how it breaks down:

Required Score = (Desired Grade - (Current Grade × (1 - Weight of Final))) ÷ (Weight of Final)

For example, if your current grade is 80%, the final exam is worth 30% of your total grade, and you want an overall grade of 85%, the calculator will show you the exact score needed to hit that target. It’s like having a personal tutor in your pocket!
