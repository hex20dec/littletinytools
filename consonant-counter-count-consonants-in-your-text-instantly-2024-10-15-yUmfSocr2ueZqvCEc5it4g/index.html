How It Works

The Consonant Counter works by scanning your text and identifying all the letters that are not vowels (a, e, i, o, u). It ignores spaces, numbers, punctuation, and special characters, so you get an accurate count of only the consonants. Think of it as a virtual assistant that picks out all the "B"s, "C"s, "D"s, and so on, while skipping the "A"s and "E"s. It's like having a proofreader who's obsessed with consonants!
