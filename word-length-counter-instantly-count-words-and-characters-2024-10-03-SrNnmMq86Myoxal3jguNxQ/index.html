Here's how the Word Length Counter works:

The tool analyzes your text in real-time, splitting it into individual words by detecting spaces. It then counts the total number of words and characters, updating the results instantly as you type or paste. It’s like having a personal editor who’s always ready to help you stay on track.

For example, if you type: "Hello, world!", the tool will count 2 words and 13 characters (including spaces and punctuation). It’s that simple!
