How It Works

The String Length Sorter works by taking your input text, splitting it into individual lines, and then sorting those lines based on their length. You can choose to sort from shortest to longest or longest to shortest. Here's a simple breakdown:

  • Input: Enter your strings, one per line, in the text box.
  • Sort: Choose your preferred sorting order—shortest to longest or longest to shortest.
  • Output: The tool rearranges your strings accordingly and displays the sorted list.
