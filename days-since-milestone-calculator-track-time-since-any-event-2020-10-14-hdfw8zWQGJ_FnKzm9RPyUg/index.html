How Does It Work?

It’s as simple as pie! The tool calculates the number of days between the date you choose and today. Here’s the magic behind it:

  1. Pick Your Date: Select a past milestone date using the date picker.
  2. Click Calculate: The tool compares your chosen date with today’s date.
