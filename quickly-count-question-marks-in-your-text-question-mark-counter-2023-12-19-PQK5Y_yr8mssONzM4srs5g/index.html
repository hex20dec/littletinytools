How It Works

The Question Mark Counter works by analyzing your text and identifying every instance of the question mark symbol (?). Here’s the simple formula it follows:

  1. Input your text: Paste or type your text into the provided box.
  2. Click "Count Question Marks": The tool scans your text and counts every question mark.
