How the PST to IST Time Converter Works

The PST to IST time conversion is based on the principle that IST is 13.5 hours ahead of PST. Our tool takes this difference into account and applies it to the input time in PST to generate the equivalent time in IST.

Here's a step-by-step breakdown of the conversion process:

  1. Enter the time in PST in the format HH:MM AM/PM.
  2. Our tool adds 13.5 hours to the input time to calculate the equivalent time in IST.
  3. The result is then displayed in the format HH:MM AM/PM, taking into account the time difference between the two zones.
