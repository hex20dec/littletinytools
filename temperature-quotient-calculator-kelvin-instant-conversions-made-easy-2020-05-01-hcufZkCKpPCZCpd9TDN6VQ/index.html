How it Works

The Temperature Quotient Calculator (Kelvin) employs a straightforward formula to convert Celsius to Kelvin: Kelvin = Celsius + 273.15. This formula is based on the definition of the Kelvin scale, where 0 Kelvin is absolute zero, and each subsequent degree represents a specific increase in temperature.
