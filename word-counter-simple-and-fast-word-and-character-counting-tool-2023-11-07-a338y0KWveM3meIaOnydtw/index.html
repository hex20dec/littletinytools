How It Works

The Word Counter tool works by analyzing your text in real-time. Here's the simple formula it follows:

  • Words: The tool splits your text into individual words by looking for spaces. It then counts how many words are in your text.
  • Characters: It counts every single character in your text, including letters, numbers, spaces, and punctuation.
