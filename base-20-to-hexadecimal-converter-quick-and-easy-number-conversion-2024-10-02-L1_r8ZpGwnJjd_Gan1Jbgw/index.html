How It Works

Converting base-20 to hexadecimal might sound complex, but our tool simplifies it in just a few steps. Here’s the magic behind it:

  1. Each character in your base-20 number (digits 0-9 and letters A-J) is assigned a value from 0 to 19.
  2. The tool calculates the decimal equivalent of your base-20 number by multiplying each digit by 20 raised to the power of its position (starting from the right).
  3. Finally, the decimal number is converted to hexadecimal (base-16) using a simple algorithm, and voilà—you have your result!
