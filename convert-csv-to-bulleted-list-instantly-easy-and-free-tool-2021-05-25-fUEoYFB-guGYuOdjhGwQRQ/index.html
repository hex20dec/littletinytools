    How It Works

    The CSV to Bulleted List Converter works like a digital organizer. Here’s the simple formula:

    1. Upload your CSV file (it’s like handing over your messy notes).
    2. The tool reads each row of your CSV and separates the data by commas (think of it as sorting items into neat piles).
    3. It then transforms each row into a bullet point, creating a clean, easy-to-read list (your notes, but now Instagram-worthy).
