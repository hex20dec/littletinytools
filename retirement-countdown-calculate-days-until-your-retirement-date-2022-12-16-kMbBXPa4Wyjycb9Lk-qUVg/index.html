How It Works

The tool works by calculating the difference between today’s date and your selected retirement date. Here’s the magic formula in plain English:

  1. Pick your retirement date using the calendar.
  2. The tool subtracts today’s date from your retirement date.
  3. It converts the time difference into days and shows you the countdown.
