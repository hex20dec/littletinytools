How Does It Work? 🤔

This nifty tool works by analyzing your text and counting how many times a line break (\n) appears. It ignores empty lines, so only the lines with actual content are counted. Here's a simple breakdown:

  1. 1️⃣ Paste or type your text into the box.
  2. 2️⃣ The tool scans for line breaks and counts the non-empty lines.
  3. 3️⃣ The total number of lines is displayed instantly.
