How Does It Work?

The Keyword Counter works by scanning your text and matching it with the keyword you provide. It counts every instance of the keyword, ensuring accuracy even if the text is long or complex. Here’s how it breaks down:

  • Step 1: Paste your text into the input box.
  • Step 2: Enter the keyword you want to count.
  • Step 3: Click "Count" and see the magic happen!

For example, if you’re analyzing a speech and want to know how often the speaker says “innovation,” the tool will give you the exact number in seconds. No fuss, no stress—just quick and reliable results.
