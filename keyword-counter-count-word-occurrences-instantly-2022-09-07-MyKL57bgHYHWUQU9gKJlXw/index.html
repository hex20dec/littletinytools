Whether you're editing an essay, analyzing a speech, or optimizing a blog post, manually counting how often a specific word appears can be tedious and error-prone. That's where the Keyword Counter comes in! This simple yet powerful tool lets you paste your text, enter a keyword, and instantly see how many times it shows up. No more squinting at paragraphs or losing your place mid-count. It’s like having a personal word detective at your fingertips, making your writing or analysis tasks smoother and faster. Let’s make counting keywords as easy as pie!

Enter your text and keyword to count how many times the keyword appears.

How Does It Work?

The Keyword Counter works by scanning your text and matching it with the keyword you provide. It counts every instance of the keyword, ensuring accuracy even if the text is long or complex. Here’s how it breaks down:

  • Step 1: Paste your text into the input box.
  • Step 2: Enter the keyword you want to count.
  • Step 3: Click "Count" and see the magic happen!

For example, if you’re analyzing a speech and want to know how often the speaker says “innovation,” the tool will give you the exact number in seconds. No fuss, no stress—just quick and reliable results.
