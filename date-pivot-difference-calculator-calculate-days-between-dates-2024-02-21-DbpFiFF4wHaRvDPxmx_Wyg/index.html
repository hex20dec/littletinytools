How It Works

The Date Pivot Difference Calculator uses a straightforward formula to determine the difference between two dates. Here's how it works:

Formula: The tool subtracts the pivot date from the comparison date, converts the result into milliseconds, and then divides it by the number of milliseconds in a day (86,400,000). This gives you the exact number of days between the two dates. Simple, right?
