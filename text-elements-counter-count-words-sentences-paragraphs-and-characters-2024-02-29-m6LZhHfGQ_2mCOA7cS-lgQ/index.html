How It Works

The Text Elements Counter works by analyzing your input text based on specific rules:

  • Words: Counts every sequence of characters separated by spaces.
  • Sentences: Counts every group of words ending with punctuation marks like periods, exclamation points, or question marks.
  • Paragraphs: Counts every block of text separated by line breaks.
  • Characters: Counts every single character, including spaces and punctuation.
