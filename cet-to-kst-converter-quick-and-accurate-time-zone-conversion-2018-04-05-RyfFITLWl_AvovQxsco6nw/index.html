How It Works

Converting CET to KST is as simple as adding 8 hours to your CET time. Since KST is 8 hours ahead of CET, the formula is straightforward: KST = CET + 8 hours. For example, if it’s 10:00 AM in CET, it’s 6:00 PM in KST. The tool automatically handles the time conversion, even when the calculation pushes the time into the next day. Just enter your CET time, click "Convert," and voilà—your KST time appears like magic!
