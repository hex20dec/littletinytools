How the Binary to Decimal Converter Works

The binary to decimal converter uses the following formula to perform the conversion: decimal = (binary[0] * 2^(n-1)) + (binary[1] * 2^(n-2)) + ... + (binary[n-1] * 2^0), where n is the number of bits in the binary number.
