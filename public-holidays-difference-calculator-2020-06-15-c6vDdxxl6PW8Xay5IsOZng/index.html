With a simple and user-friendly interface, users can select two countries or regions and calculate the difference in their public holidays, making it easier to plan and adapt to different holiday schedules.

How it Works

The Public Holidays Difference Calculator uses a simple formula to compare and calculate the difference in public holidays between two countries or regions. The formula involves the following steps:

  • Select two countries or regions from the dropdown menu.
  • The calculator retrieves the public holiday data for each selected country or region.
  • The calculator compares the public holiday dates and names for each country or region.
  • The calculator calculates the difference in public holidays between the two countries or regions.
