The tool multiplies each digit by 15 raised to the power of its position (starting from the right at position 0) and adds them all together to get the decimal value. Sounds complicated? Don’t worry—our converter does all the heavy lifting for you!

Here’s a quick example: If you enter "1A3" in base-15, the tool calculates it as:

(1 × 15²) + (10 × 15¹) + (3 × 15⁰) = 225 + 150 + 3 = 378
