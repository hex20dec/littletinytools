How It Works

The tool uses straightforward formulas to convert temperatures between Fahrenheit (°F), Celsius (°C), and Kelvin (K). Here’s how it works:

  • From Fahrenheit to Celsius: Subtract 32, then multiply by 5/9.
  • From Celsius to Fahrenheit: Multiply by 9/5, then add 32.
  • From Celsius to Kelvin: Simply add 273.15.
  • From Kelvin to Celsius: Subtract 273.15.
  • From Fahrenheit to Kelvin: Subtract 32, multiply by 5/9, then add 273.15.
  • From Kelvin to Fahrenheit: Subtract 273.15, multiply by 9/5, then add 32.
