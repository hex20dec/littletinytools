How It Works

The unique word counter app employs a straightforward yet effective formula to calculate the number of distinct words in a given text. The process involves the following steps:

  1. Text normalization: The input text is converted to lowercase to ensure consistency and accuracy.
  2. Tokenization: The text is broken down into individual words, and non-alphanumeric characters are removed.
  3. Unique word identification: A set data structure is used to store unique words, eliminating duplicates and providing an accurate count.
