Enter your list of words or phrases below, and they will be sorted alphabetically, ignoring capitalization.

How It Works

This tool sorts your list alphabetically by treating uppercase and lowercase letters as equals. Here’s the simple formula:

Step 1: Paste your list into the input box (one item per line).

Step 2: Click “Sort” to alphabetize the list, ignoring capitalization.

Step 3: Copy the sorted list with one click or reset to start fresh.

For example, if your list is apple, Banana, cherry, Apricot, the tool will sort it as apple, Apricot, Banana, cherry. Easy, right?
