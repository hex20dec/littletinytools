How It Works

The ROI Calculator uses a straightforward formula to measure the profitability of your investment. Here’s the breakdown:

ROI = [(Final Value - Initial Investment) / Initial Investment] × 100

In plain English: Subtract what you initially invested from the final value of your investment. Divide that result by your initial investment, then multiply by 100 to get a percentage. This percentage tells you how much profit (or loss) you’ve made relative to your original investment.
