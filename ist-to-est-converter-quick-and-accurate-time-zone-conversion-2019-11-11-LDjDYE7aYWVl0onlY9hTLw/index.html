How It Works

The IST to EST Converter works by calculating the time difference between Indian Standard Time (IST) and Eastern Standard Time (EST). EST is 9 hours and 30 minutes behind IST. Here’s the formula in plain English:

  • 1. Enter the IST time (including AM or PM).
  • 2. Subtract 9 hours and 30 minutes from the IST time.
  • 3. Adjust for AM/PM if needed, and voilà—you have the EST time!

For example, if it’s 10:00 AM in IST, subtracting 9 hours and 30 minutes gives you 12:30 AM EST. It’s that simple!
