How It Works

The CSV Row Trimmer works like a pair of digital scissors for your data. Here's the simple formula:

  1. Upload Your CSV: Select your file, and the tool reads it instantly.
  2. Choose Rows to Trim: Decide how many rows to remove from the top or bottom of your file.
