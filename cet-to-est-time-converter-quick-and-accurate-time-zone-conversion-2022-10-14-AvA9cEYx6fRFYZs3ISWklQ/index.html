How Does It Work?

Converting CET to EST is as easy as 1-2-3! Here’s the breakdown:

  1. Enter Your CET Time: Type in the time you want to convert (e.g., 14:30 for 2:30 PM).
  2. Select AM or PM: Let the tool know if it’s morning or afternoon.
  3. Click Convert: The tool subtracts 6 hours from your CET time (since CET is 6 hours ahead of EST) and gives you the EST equivalent in a flash.

For example, if it’s 8:00 PM CET, the tool will show 2:00 PM EST. Simple, right?
