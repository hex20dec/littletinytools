How It Works

The Work Completion Calculator uses a straightforward formula to determine your progress. Here’s the magic behind it:

Percentage Completed = (Tasks Completed ÷ Total Tasks) × 100

For example, if you have 20 tasks and you’ve completed 5, the calculator divides 5 by 20 and multiplies the result by 100 to give you 25%. It’s that simple! Below is a table showing how the tool works with different numbers:
