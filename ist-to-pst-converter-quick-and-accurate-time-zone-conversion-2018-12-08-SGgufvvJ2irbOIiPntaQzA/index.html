How It Works

The IST to PST Converter works by calculating the time difference between Indian Standard Time (IST) and Pacific Standard Time (PST). Since PST is 13 hours and 30 minutes behind IST, the tool subtracts this difference from the time you input. For example, if it's 10:00 AM in IST, the converter subtracts 13 hours and 30 minutes to give you the corresponding PST time of 8:30 PM (from the previous day). It’s like having a personal time zone assistant in your pocket!
