Input your list of values, and we'll remove duplicates and sort them for you.

This tool works entirely in your browser. No data is sent to external servers.

How It Works

This tool follows a simple yet powerful process to transform your cluttered list into a clean, organized one:

  1. Input: Paste your list of values (numbers, words, or a mix) into the input box. You can separate them with commas, spaces, or even line breaks—no strict formatting required.
  2. Process: The tool scans your list, removes any duplicate values, and sorts the remaining items in ascending order (alphabetically or numerically, depending on your input).
  3. Output: Voilà! Your clean, sorted list appears in the output box, ready to be copied and used wherever you need it.

It’s like having a personal assistant for your data—fast, efficient, and always reliable.
