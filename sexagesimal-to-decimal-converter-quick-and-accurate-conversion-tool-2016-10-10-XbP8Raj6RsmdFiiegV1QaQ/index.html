How It Works

Converting sexagesimal (degrees, minutes, seconds) to decimal is easier than you think! Here's the simple formula behind the magic:

Decimal = Degrees + (Minutes / 60) + (Seconds / 3600)

For example, if you have 45 degrees, 30 minutes, and 15 seconds, the calculation would be:

45 + (30 / 60) + (15 / 3600) = 45.504167
