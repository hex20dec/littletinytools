How It Works

The Paragraph Counter works by analyzing your text and identifying breaks between paragraphs. It counts each block of text separated by one or more blank lines as a single paragraph. Here’s a simple breakdown:

  • 1. Paste or type your text into the input box.
  • 2. Click "Count Paragraphs" to instantly see the total number of paragraphs.
  • 3. Use the "Reset" button to clear the text and start over.

It’s that easy! Whether you’re working on a short story or a lengthy report, this tool ensures you stay on top of your paragraph count.
