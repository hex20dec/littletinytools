How it Works

The conversion process involves a simple mathematical formula that can be broken down into the following steps:

  1. First, the decimal number is split into two parts: the whole number and the fractional part.
  2. Next, the fractional part is converted into a fraction by dividing it by a power of 10, depending on the number of decimal places.
  3. Finally, the resulting fraction is simplified by dividing both the numerator and denominator by their greatest common divisor (GCD).
