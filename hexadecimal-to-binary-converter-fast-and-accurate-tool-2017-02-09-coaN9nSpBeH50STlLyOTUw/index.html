How Does It Work?

The tool works by taking each character of your hexadecimal input (which can be numbers 0-9 or letters A-F) and translating it into its corresponding 4-digit binary value. For example, the hexadecimal "A" becomes "1010" in binary. The tool stitches these 4-digit binary chunks together to give you the final result.
