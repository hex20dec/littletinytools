How It Works

The Decimal to Tally Marks Converter works by breaking down a number into groups of five, just like traditional tally mark counting. For every five units, it adds a diagonal line (\) to represent a completed group. For numbers not divisible by five, vertical lines (|) are used to represent the remaining units. Here’s a quick example:

  • Number 7: |||| \\ (5 + 2)
  • Number 12: |||| \\ |||| \\ || (5 + 5 + 2)

It’s that simple! Just enter your number, and the tool does the rest.
