How It Works

The tool works by taking your input—whether it’s a list of numbers, words, or phrases—and splitting it into individual items. It then counts how many times each item appears. Items that appear only once are categorized as unique, while items that appear more than once are labeled as duplicates. The result? A neatly sorted list that makes your life easier.
