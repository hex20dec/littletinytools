How It Works

The Power Consumption Calculator uses a straightforward formula to determine how much energy your device consumes over time. Here’s the breakdown:

Total Power Consumption = Power Rating (Watts) × Usage Time (Hours per Day) × Number of Days

For example, if your device has a power rating of 100 watts, you use it for 5 hours a day, and you’re calculating for 30 days, the total consumption would be 100 × 5 × 30 = 15,000 watt-hours (Wh). Easy, right?
