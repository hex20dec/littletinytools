How It Works

The CSV to Text List Converter works like magic (but without the wand). Here's the formula:

  1. Upload your CSV file. The tool reads the data row by row.
  2. Choose your desired format: bullet points, numbered lists, or a custom separator (like commas or dashes).
  3. The tool processes each row and formats it neatly into your chosen style.
  4. The result? A beautifully organized text list, ready to copy and use wherever you need it.
