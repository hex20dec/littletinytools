How It Works

The Weighted Average Calculator uses a straightforward formula to give you accurate results. Here's how it works:

  • Step 1: Multiply each value by its corresponding weight.
  • Step 2: Add up all the weighted values.
  • Step 3: Divide the total weighted values by the total weights.

In simple terms: (Value₁ × Weight₁ + Value₂ × Weight₂ + ...) / (Weight₁ + Weight₂ + ...)

Here's a quick example table to show how it works in practice:

Value Weight Weighted Value
10 2 20
20 3 60
30 1 30
Total 6 110

The weighted average here would be 110 / 6 = 18.33. Easy, right?
