Sort by List Intersection

Sorting through two lists to find common items can feel like searching for a needle in a haystack. But what if you could instantly pinpoint shared items and even sort them in the order you need? That’s where the Sort by List Intersection tool comes in. Whether you’re comparing grocery lists, organizing tasks, or analyzing data, this tool simplifies the process by identifying overlapping items and neatly sorting them—no manual cross-referencing required. It’s like having a personal assistant who loves making your life easier!

A simple tool to find and sort intersecting items between two lists.

How It Works

The tool works by comparing two lists of items and finding the common elements between them. Here’s the simple formula it follows:

  1. Enter items into List 1 and List 2, separating them by commas or new lines.
  2. Choose your preferred sort order: Ascending (A-Z) or Descending (Z-A).
  3. Click Find Intersection to see the common items sorted as per your selection.

It’s that straightforward! No complex formulas or manual work—just quick, accurate results.
