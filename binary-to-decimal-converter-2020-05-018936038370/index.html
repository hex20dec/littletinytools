How the Binary to Decimal Converter Works

The Binary to Decimal Converter uses a simple yet effective formula to perform conversions: Decimal Number = (Binary Number)2. This means that each digit in the binary number is multiplied by 2 raised to the power of its position, starting from the right (position 0). The results are then added together to produce the decimal equivalent.
