Convert Japan Standard Time (JST) to Coordinated Universal Time (UTC).

Please enter a valid time in JST format (HH:MM).

JST is UTC+9. This tool calculates the conversion based on this offset.

Here’s how the magic happens: Japan Standard Time (JST) is 9 hours ahead of Coordinated Universal Time (UTC). To convert JST to UTC, the tool simply subtracts 9 hours from the JST time you provide. For example, if it’s 3:00 PM in JST, the tool calculates 3:00 PM minus 9 hours, giving you 6:00 AM UTC. It’s that straightforward!
