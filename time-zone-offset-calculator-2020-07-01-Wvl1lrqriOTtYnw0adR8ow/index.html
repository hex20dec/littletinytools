Time Zone Offset Calculator

The Time Zone Offset Calculator is a web-based tool designed to calculate the time difference between two time zones. It allows users to select two time zones from a drop-down list and calculates the offset in hours and minutes. This tool is particularly useful for individuals who need to schedule meetings or coordinate with people across different time zones. By using this tool, users can easily determine the time difference and plan their schedules accordingly, making it an essential resource for global communication and collaboration.

How it Works

The Time Zone Offset Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the time difference between two time zones. The formula is based on the UTC (Coordinated Universal Time) offset of each time zone. The tool subtracts the UTC offset of the first time zone from the UTC offset of the second time zone to determine the time difference.
