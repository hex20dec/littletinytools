How the Calculator Works

The Flight Cost Per Mile Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the cost per mile of your flight. The formula is: Cost Per Mile = Total Cost of Flight / Total Distance of Flight. This means that if you know the total cost of your flight and the total distance you will be traveling, you can easily calculate your cost per mile.
