How It Works

The Lunar Calendar Difference Calculator uses a simple formula to determine the difference between two dates in a lunar calendar. Here’s the breakdown:

  • Step 1: Enter your start and end dates in the provided fields.
  • Step 2: The tool calculates the difference in days by subtracting the start date from the end date.
  • Step 3: It then converts the days into weeks (by dividing by 7) and months (by dividing by the average lunar month length of 29.5 days).
