How It Works

The Line Character Counter is as simple as it gets. Here’s the magic behind it:

  1. You type or paste your text into the box.
  2. The tool splits your text into individual lines.
  3. It counts the number of characters in each line.
  4. It adds up all the characters to give you the total count.

It’s like slicing a cake into pieces, counting the sprinkles on each slice, and then telling you how many sprinkles you’ve got in total. Sweet, right?
