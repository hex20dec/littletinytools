How it Works

The subtraction process is straightforward: it involves finding the difference between two numbers. The formula for subtraction can be explained as follows: if you have a certain quantity (the minuend) and you take away a part of it (the subtrahend), the result is the difference between the two quantities. For instance, if you have 10 items (minuend) and you give away 3 items (subtrahend), the result would be 7 items, which is the difference between the initial quantity and the quantity taken away.
