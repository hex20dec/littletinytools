How It Works

The Character Repetition Counter works by scanning your text and tallying up every single character, from letters and numbers to spaces and punctuation. Here’s the breakdown in simple terms:

  • 1. Input Your Text: Paste or type your text into the box.
  • 2. Click "Count Characters": The tool processes your text, counting each character.
  • 3. View Results: A table displays each character and how many times it appears, sorted alphabetically.

It’s like having a magnifying glass for your text, revealing patterns you might not notice otherwise.
