Sales Tax Calculator

Why does calculating sales tax always feel like solving a math puzzle? Whether you're shopping online, planning a budget, or running a small business, figuring out the total cost after sales tax can be a headache. That's where this Sales Tax Calculator comes in handy! Simply plug in the base price and the sales tax percentage, and voilà—your total cost is calculated in seconds. No more mental math or scribbling on receipts. Whether you're buying a $50 gadget with a 7% tax or a $1,000 laptop with a 10% tax, this tool makes life a little simpler and a lot less stressful. Say goodbye to guesswork and hello to clarity!

Enter the base price and sales tax percentage to calculate the total cost.

Here's how the Sales Tax Calculator works: it takes the base price of an item and the sales tax percentage, then calculates the total cost by adding the tax amount to the original price. The formula is straightforward:

Total Cost = Base Price + (Base Price × Sales Tax Percentage / 100)

For example, if you buy a $100 item with a 6% sales tax, the total cost would be $106. It's that simple! Below is a quick reference table for common base prices and tax percentages:
