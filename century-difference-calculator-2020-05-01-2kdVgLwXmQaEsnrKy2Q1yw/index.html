How it Works

The Century Difference Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the difference in centuries between two years. The formula is based on the idea that each century is a period of 100 years, and the calculator determines the century of each year by dividing the year value by 100 and taking the floor of the result. The difference between the two centuries is then calculated by subtracting the century of the first year from the century of the second year.
