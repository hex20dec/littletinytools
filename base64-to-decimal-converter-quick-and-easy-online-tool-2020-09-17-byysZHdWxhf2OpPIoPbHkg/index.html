How It Works

The Base64 to Decimal Converter works by breaking down your Base64 string into its binary components, then converting each binary value into its corresponding decimal number. Think of it as translating a secret code into something you can actually use. For example, the Base64 string "SGVsbG8=" translates to the decimal sequence 72 101 108 108 111, which corresponds to the word "Hello" in ASCII.
