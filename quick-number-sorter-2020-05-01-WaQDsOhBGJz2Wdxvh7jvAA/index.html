      How it Works

      The Quick Number Sorter uses a simple yet effective algorithm to sort your list of numbers. Here's a step-by-step explanation of the process:

      • Enter your list of numbers separated by commas into the input field.
      • The tool splits your input into individual numbers and filters out any non-numeric values.
      • The remaining numbers are then sorted in ascending order using a fast and efficient sorting algorithm.
      • The sorted list is displayed below the original list, making it easy to compare and verify the results.
