How It Works

The Temperature Linear Scale Calculator uses straightforward formulas to convert temperatures between Kelvin (K), Celsius (°C), and Fahrenheit (°F). Here's the magic behind it:

  • From Kelvin to Celsius: Subtract 273.15 from the Kelvin value.
  • From Celsius to Fahrenheit: Multiply the Celsius value by 9/5, then add 32.
  • From Fahrenheit to Celsius: Subtract 32 from the Fahrenheit value, then multiply by 5/9.
  • From Celsius to Kelvin: Add 273.15 to the Celsius value.

These formulas ensure that every conversion is precise and reliable, no matter which scale you start with.
