How It Works

The Date Calculator works by breaking down your date-related problems into simple steps. Here’s the formula it uses:

Resulting Date = Start Date ± (Value × Unit Conversion)

Or, for calculating the difference between two dates:

Difference = (Date2 - Date1) ÷ Unit Conversion

The tool automatically handles the math, whether you’re adding days, weeks, months, or years, or finding the gap between two dates. It’s like having a personal calendar assistant!
