The Fahrenheit to Celsius Converter is an online tool designed to simplify temperature conversions from Fahrenheit to Celsius. This converter uses a straightforward formula to calculate the Celsius equivalent of a given Fahrenheit temperature, providing users with a fast and reliable way to perform temperature conversions.

How it Works

The conversion process involves a simple formula: Celsius = (Fahrenheit - 32) * 5/9. This formula takes the Fahrenheit temperature as input, subtracts 32, multiplies the result by 5, and then divides it by 9 to obtain the equivalent temperature in Celsius.
