How the Amortization Calculator Works

The Amortization Calculator uses a formula to calculate the monthly payment, total interest paid, and total amount paid over the life of the loan. The formula takes into account the loan amount, interest rate, and loan term to provide an accurate picture of the loan's amortization schedule.

The formula is as follows: Monthly Payment = (Loan Amount x Monthly Interest Rate x (1 + Monthly Interest Rate)^Loan Term) / ((1 + Monthly Interest Rate)^Loan Term - 1)
