Note: CST is -6 hours from UTC (or -5 hours during daylight saving time)

How it Works

The UTC to CST converter works by using a simple formula to calculate the time difference between the two time zones. The formula is based on the fact that CST is 6 hours behind UTC (or 5 hours during daylight saving time).
