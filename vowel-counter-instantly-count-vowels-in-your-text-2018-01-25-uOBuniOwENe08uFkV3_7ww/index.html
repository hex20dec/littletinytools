How Does It Work?

The Vowel Counter uses a simple yet effective approach to count vowels in your text. It scans every character in your input, identifies the vowels (A, E, I, O, U, and their lowercase versions), and adds them up. Here's a breakdown:

Formula:

  • Input your text into the box.
  • The tool scans the text for vowels (A, E, I, O, U, a, e, i, o, u).
  • It counts each vowel and displays the total number.

For example, if you type "Hello World," the tool will count 3 vowels (e, o, o).
