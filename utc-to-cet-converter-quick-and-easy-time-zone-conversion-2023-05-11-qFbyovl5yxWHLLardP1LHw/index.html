CET is UTC+1 (standard time) or UTC+2 (daylight saving time).

How It Works

Converting UTC to CET is as simple as adding 1 hour to the UTC time. That's because CET (Central European Time) is always 1 hour ahead of UTC during standard time. For example, if it's 14:30 UTC, the tool adds 1 hour to give you 15:30 CET. During daylight saving time (CEST), CET is 2 hours ahead of UTC, but our tool automatically adjusts for this. No need to stress about the details – we handle all the tricky bits for you!
