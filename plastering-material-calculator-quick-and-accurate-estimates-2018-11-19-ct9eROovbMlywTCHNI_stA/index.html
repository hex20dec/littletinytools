How It Works

The Plastering Material Calculator uses a simple formula to determine the exact amount of materials you need. Here’s the breakdown:

  1. Calculate Total Area: Multiply the wall length by the wall height to get the total area in square meters.
  2. Determine Plaster Volume: Multiply the total area by the plaster thickness (converted from millimeters to meters).
  3. Calculate Cement and Sand: Based on the cement-to-sand ratio, the tool divides the plaster volume into the required amounts of cement and sand. Cement is measured in bags (50 kg each), and sand is measured in cubic meters.
  4. Estimate Plaster Quantity: Multiply the plaster volume by the density of plaster (1440 kg/m³) to get the total weight of plaster needed.

Here’s a quick example: For a wall that’s 5 meters long and 3 meters high, with a plaster thickness of 10 mm and a cement-to-sand ratio of 1:4, the calculator will give you precise measurements for cement, sand, and plaster.
