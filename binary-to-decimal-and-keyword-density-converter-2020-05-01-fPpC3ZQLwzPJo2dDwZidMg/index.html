The keyword density calculator uses the following steps to analyze the text and calculate the keyword density:

  1. Split the text into individual words.
  2. Remove punctuation and convert all words to lowercase.
  3. Count the occurrences of each keyword in the text.
  4. Calculate the density percentage for each keyword by dividing the total occurrences by the total number of words in the text and multiplying by 100.
