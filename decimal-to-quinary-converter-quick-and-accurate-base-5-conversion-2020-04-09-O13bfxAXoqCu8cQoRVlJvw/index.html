How It Works

Converting a decimal number to quinary is all about dividing and conquering! Here’s the secret sauce:

  1. Take your decimal number and divide it by 5.
  2. Write down the remainder (it will always be between 0 and 4).
  3. Repeat the process with the quotient until it becomes 0.
  4. Now, read the remainders in reverse order—that’s your quinary number!

For example, to convert 13 to quinary:

  • 13 ÷ 5 = 2 with a remainder of 3
  • 2 ÷ 5 = 0 with a remainder of 2
  • Reading the remainders backward gives you "23" in quinary.
