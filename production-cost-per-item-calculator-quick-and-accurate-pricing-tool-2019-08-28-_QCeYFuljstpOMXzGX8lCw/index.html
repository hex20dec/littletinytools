How It Works

The Production Cost Per Item Calculator uses a straightforward formula to determine the cost of producing each item. Here’s how it breaks down:

Cost Per Item = (Total Production Cost + Additional Costs) ÷ Number of Items Produced

For example, if your total production cost is $1,000, you produce 200 items, and you have $50 in additional costs, the calculator will divide $1,050 by 200 to give you a cost of $5.25 per item. Easy, right?
