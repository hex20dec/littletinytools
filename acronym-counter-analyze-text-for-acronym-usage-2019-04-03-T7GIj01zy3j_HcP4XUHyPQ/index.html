    How It Works

    The Acronym Counter works by scanning your text for words written in ALL CAPS that are longer than one character and don’t contain any numbers or symbols. It identifies these as acronyms, counts them, and even gives you the option to see a list of all the unique ones found. It’s like having a magnifying glass for uppercase words!
