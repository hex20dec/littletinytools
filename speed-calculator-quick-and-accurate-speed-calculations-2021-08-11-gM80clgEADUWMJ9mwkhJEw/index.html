How It Works

Speed is calculated using a simple formula: Speed = Distance ÷ Time. In other words, how fast you’re going depends on how far you’ve traveled and how long it took you to get there. For example, if you ran 100 meters in 10 seconds, your speed would be 10 meters per second. The tool does all the heavy lifting for you, so you don’t have to stress over the math.
