How It Works

The MST to PST Converter is as simple as pie. Here’s the formula it uses behind the scenes:

PST Time = MST Time - 1 Hour

That’s it! Since Mountain Standard Time (MST) is always 1 hour ahead of Pacific Standard Time (PST), the tool subtracts 1 hour from your input time to give you the correct PST equivalent. Whether you’re using a 12-hour format (like 2:00 PM) or a 24-hour format (like 14:00), the converter handles it all seamlessly.
