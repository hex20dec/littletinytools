Here’s how the Days Until Next Leap Year Calculator works: It uses a simple formula to determine the next leap year based on the year you input (or the current year if you don’t). A leap year occurs every 4 years, except for years that are divisible by 100 but not by 400. Once it identifies the next leap year, it calculates the exact number of days remaining until February 29th of that year. Easy, right?
