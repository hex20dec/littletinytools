How It Works

The tool works by taking your binary input (a sequence of 0s and 1s) and breaking it into 8-bit chunks. Each chunk represents a single character in the ASCII table. For example, the binary code 01001000 translates to the letter "H." The converter does all the heavy lifting, ensuring your output is accurate and easy to read.
