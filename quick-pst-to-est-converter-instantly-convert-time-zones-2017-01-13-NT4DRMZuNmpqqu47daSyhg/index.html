How It Works

The PST to EST Converter is as simple as it gets. Here's the magic behind the scenes: Pacific Standard Time (PST) is 3 hours behind Eastern Standard Time (EST). So, when you input a time in PST, the tool adds 3 hours to it to give you the corresponding EST time. For example, if it's 10:00 AM in PST, adding 3 hours gives you 1:00 PM in EST. Easy, right? No complicated math, no stress—just a quick conversion at your fingertips.
