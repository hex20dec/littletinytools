How It Works

The tool uses the Pythagorean theorem—a classic geometry formula—to calculate the diagonal of a rectangle. Here’s the simple breakdown:

Diagonal = √(Width² + Height²)

So, if your rectangle has a width of 3 units and a height of 4 units, the diagonal would be √(3² + 4²) = √(9 + 16) = √25 = 5 units. Easy, right?
