How Does It Work?

Think of the Custom Pattern Counter as a super-smart highlighter. Here's how it works:

  1. Input Your Text: Paste or type the text you want to analyze into the "Text to Analyze" box.
  2. Enter Your Pattern: Type the word, phrase, or pattern you want to count in the "Pattern to Count" field.
  3. Choose Case Sensitivity: Toggle the "Case Sensitive" checkbox if you want the tool to match the exact uppercase and lowercase letters.
  4. Hit Count: Click the "Count Pattern" button, and voilà! The tool will instantly tell you how many times your pattern appears in the text.

It’s that simple! No complicated formulas, no guesswork—just fast, accurate results.
