How It Works

The Reversed List Sorter operates on a simple principle: it takes your list of items, reverses their order, and presents them back to you neatly. Here’s the formula in plain terms:

  • Step 1: Enter your list items, one per line.
  • Step 2: Click "Sort in Reverse."
  • Step 3: Instantly see your reversed list in the output box.
