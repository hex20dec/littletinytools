How Does It Work?

Converting KST to AEDT is simple! AEDT is always 2 hours ahead of KST during daylight saving time (October to April). Here’s how the tool calculates it:

  • Step 1: Input your KST time in HH:MM format (e.g., 14:30).
  • Step 2: Choose whether you’re using 24-hour format, AM, or PM.
  • Step 3: The tool adds 2 hours to your KST time to give you the AEDT equivalent.

For example, if it’s 10:00 AM in Korea, it’s 12:00 PM in Australia. Easy, right?
