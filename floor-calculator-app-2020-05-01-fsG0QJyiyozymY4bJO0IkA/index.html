Floor Calculator

The Floor Calculator is a free online tool that helps homeowners, contractors, and builders estimate the total cost of flooring materials for a given room or space. By inputting the length, width, and unit of measurement of the room, as well as the cost per square unit of the desired flooring material, users can quickly and easily calculate the total area and total cost of the flooring project. With its user-friendly interface and accurate calculations, the Floor Calculator is an essential tool for anyone looking to plan and budget for a flooring installation or renovation project, and is also a great resource for those looking for a room dimensions calculator, a flooring cost estimator, or a construction cost estimator.

How the Floor Calculator Works

The Floor Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the total area and total cost of the flooring project. The formula is as follows: Total Area = Length x Width, and Total Cost = Total Area x Cost per Square Unit. This formula is used to provide accurate calculations for a variety of flooring materials, including hardwood, carpet, and tile.
