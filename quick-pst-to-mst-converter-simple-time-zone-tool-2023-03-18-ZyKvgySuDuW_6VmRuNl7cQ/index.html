MST is 1 hour ahead of PST.

How Does It Work?

Our PST to MST Converter works like a time-traveling wizard. Here’s the magic formula in plain English:

  1. Step 1: You enter the time in Pacific Standard Time (PST).
  2. Step 2: Choose whether it’s AM or PM.
  3. Step 3: Click the “Convert” button.
  4. Step 4: The tool adds 1 hour to your PST time (since MST is 1 hour ahead) and adjusts the AM/PM accordingly. Boom! You’ve got the time in Mountain Standard Time (MST).

It’s that simple. No equations, no stress—just instant results.
