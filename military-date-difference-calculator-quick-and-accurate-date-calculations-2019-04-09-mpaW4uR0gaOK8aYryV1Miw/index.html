How It Works

The Military Date Difference Calculator works by breaking down the dates into their individual components—day, month, and year—and then calculating the difference between them. Here's the magic formula in plain English:

  • Days: Subtract the start date from the end date and convert the result into days.
  • Weeks: Take the total days and divide by 7 (because there are 7 days in a week).
  • Months: Calculate the difference in years, multiply by 12, and add the difference in months.
  • Years: Subtract the start year from the end year.
