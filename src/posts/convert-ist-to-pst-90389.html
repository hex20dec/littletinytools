IST to PST Converter

The IST to PST Time Zone Converter is a handy online tool designed to help individuals convert Indian Standard Time (IST) to Pacific Standard Time (PST) with ease. This converter is particularly useful for people who work remotely, travel frequently, or need to coordinate with others across different time zones. Whether you're planning a trip, scheduling a meeting, or simply want to stay in sync with friends and family in different parts of the world, this tool provides a quick and reliable way to convert IST to PST time zones.

How it Works

The conversion from IST to PST is based on the time difference between the two zones. Pacific Standard Time (PST) is 12 hours and 30 minutes behind Indian Standard Time (IST). This means that when it is 12:00 PM (noon) in IST, it is 11:30 PM in PST on the previous day.
