How It Works

The Vacation Budget Calculator uses a straightforward formula to help you manage your expenses. Here’s how it breaks down:

Remaining Budget = Total Budget - (Accommodation + Food + Transportation + Activities + Miscellaneous)

Simply input your total budget and the amounts you plan to spend in each category. The tool instantly calculates your remaining budget, so you can adjust your spending as needed. It’s like having a personal financial advisor for your trip!
