How It Works

The KST to PST Converter works by subtracting 17 hours from the Korean Standard Time (KST) to get the corresponding Pacific Standard Time (PST). Since KST is 17 hours ahead of PST, this adjustment ensures the time is accurate. For example, if it’s 14:30 (2:30 PM) in Seoul, subtracting 17 hours gives you 21:30 (9:30 PM) of the previous day in PST. Easy, right?
