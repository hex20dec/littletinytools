How It Works

The Line Counter works by breaking your text into individual lines using the "newline" character (that invisible marker your keyboard creates when you press Enter). It then counts each line and gives you the total. Think of it as slicing a loaf of bread—each slice is a line, and the counter tells you how many slices you have. Easy, right?
