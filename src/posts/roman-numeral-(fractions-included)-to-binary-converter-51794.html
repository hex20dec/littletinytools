How It Works

Our tool works in two simple steps:

  1. First, it converts the Roman numeral (or fraction) into its decimal equivalent. For example, "XIV" becomes 14, and "½" becomes 0.5.
  2. Next, it transforms the decimal number into binary. So, 14 becomes "1110" in binary, and 0.5 becomes "0.1" in binary.

It’s like magic, but with math! The tool even handles tricky cases, like subtracting smaller numerals (e.g., IV is 4) or converting fractions.
