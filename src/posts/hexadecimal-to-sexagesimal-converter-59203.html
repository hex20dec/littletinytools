The binary and hexadecimal converter uses a simple yet efficient algorithm to convert binary and hexadecimal numbers to their decimal and sexagesimal equivalents. The conversion process is based on the following formulas:

  • Binary to Decimal: decimal = ∑(binary_digit * 2^position), where position starts from 0 and increases from right to left
  • Hexadecimal to Decimal: decimal = ∑(hexadecimal_digit * 16^position), where position starts from 0 and increases from right to left
