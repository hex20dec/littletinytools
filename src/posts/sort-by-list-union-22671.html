Enter two lists of items, and this tool will combine, deduplicate, and sort them for you.

This tool works entirely in your browser. No data is sent to external servers.

How It Works

This tool follows a simple formula to make your life easier:

  1. Input two lists of items (separated by commas or new lines).
  2. The tool combines the items from both lists into one.
  3. It removes any duplicates, so each item appears only once.
  4. Finally, it sorts the combined list alphabetically or numerically for easy reference.
