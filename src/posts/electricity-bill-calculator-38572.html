Electricity Bill Calculator

Electricity bills can feel like a mystery, especially when you’re trying to figure out how much you’ll owe at the end of the month. Why does it always seem like the numbers don’t add up? With this Electricity Bill Calculator, you can finally take the guesswork out of your energy expenses. Simply plug in your electricity usage (in kWh) and the rate per unit, and voilà—your estimated bill is ready in seconds. Whether you’re budgeting for the month or just curious about your energy costs, this tool makes it easy, fast, and even a little fun. Say goodbye to surprises and hello to clarity!

How It Works

The Electricity Bill Calculator uses a simple formula to estimate your bill:

Total Bill = Electricity Usage (kWh) × Rate per Unit (kWh)

For example, if you used 300 kWh of electricity and your rate is $0.15 per kWh, your bill would be 300 × 0.15 = $45. It’s that straightforward!
