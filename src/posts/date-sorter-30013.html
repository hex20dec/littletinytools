How It Works

The Date Sorter works like a digital filing cabinet. Here’s the magic behind it:

  1. Input Your Dates: Paste or type your dates (one per line) in the provided text box. Make sure they follow the YYYY-MM-DD format for seamless sorting.
  2. Choose Your Order: Select whether you want your dates sorted from oldest to newest (ascending) or newest to oldest (descending).
  3. Hit Sort: Click the "Sort Dates" button, and the tool instantly organizes your dates in the desired order.
  4. Copy and Go: Once sorted, you can copy the results to your clipboard with a single click, ready to use wherever you need them.

It’s that simple—no complicated steps, no hidden catches. Just clean, organized dates in seconds.
