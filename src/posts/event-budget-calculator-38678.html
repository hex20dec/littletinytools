How It Works

The Event Budget Calculator is super simple to use. Here’s the formula:

  • 1. Add your Expense Category (e.g., Venue, Catering, Decorations).
  • 2. Enter the Budget Amount for that category.
  • 3. The tool automatically calculates your Total Budget, Total Spent, and Remaining Budget.
  • 4. A progress bar shows how much of your budget you’ve used, so you can stay on track.
