How It Works: Simple Math, Big Results

The Tile Coverage Calculator uses a straightforward formula to determine how many tiles you need:

Total Tiles Required = (Area to be Tiled ÷ Area of One Tile) × (1 + Wastage Buffer)

Here’s how it breaks down:

  • Area to be Tiled: Multiply the width and length of the space you’re tiling.
  • Area of One Tile: Multiply the width and length of a single tile.
  • Wastage Buffer: Add a percentage (usually 10%) to account for cuts, breaks, and mistakes.

For example, if you’re tiling a 5m x 4m wall with 0.5m x 0.5m tiles and a 10% buffer, the calculator will tell you exactly how many tiles to buy—no guesswork required!
