How It Works

Calculating your annual salary from your hourly wage is straightforward with this tool. Here's the magic formula:

Annual Salary = Hourly Wage × Hours Worked Per Week × 52

For example, if you earn $20 per hour and work 40 hours a week, your annual salary would be:

$20 × 40 × 52 = $41,600
