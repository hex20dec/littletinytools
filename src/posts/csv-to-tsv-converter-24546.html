How It Works

This tool follows a straightforward process to convert your CSV (Comma-Separated Values) file into TSV (Tab-Separated Values) format. Here's the magic formula:

  1. Upload your CSV file by clicking the "Upload CSV File" button.
  2. The tool reads your file and replaces every comma (,) with a tab character (\t).
  3. The converted TSV content is displayed in the text area below, ready to be copied or downloaded.
