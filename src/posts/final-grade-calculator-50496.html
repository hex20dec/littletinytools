Final Grade Calculator

The Final Grade Calculator is a free online tool that helps students estimate their final grades based on their assignments and weights. With this calculator, students can easily calculate their overall grade by entering their assignment names, grades, and weights. This tool is designed to provide an accurate estimate of the final grade, helping students to better understand their academic performance and make informed decisions about their studies. Our calculator is user-friendly, easy to use, and provides instant results, making it an essential resource for students of all levels.

How the Final Grade Calculator Works

The Final Grade Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the final grade. The formula is: Final Grade = (Sum of (Grade x Weight)) / Total Weight. This means that for each assignment, the grade is multiplied by the weight, and then all these products are added together. The sum is then divided by the total weight to get the final grade.

For example, if a student has three assignments with grades 80, 90, and 70, and weights 20%, 30%, and 50% respectively, the calculator will calculate the final grade as follows: (80 x 0.2) + (90 x 0.3) + (70 x 0.5) = 16 + 27 + 35 = 78. Therefore, the final grade is 78.
