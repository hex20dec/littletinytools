How It Works

The tool follows a simple mathematical principle: rounding. Here’s the formula in plain terms:

Step 1: You enter a temperature in Fahrenheit, like 98.6°F.

Step 2: The tool checks if the decimal part is 0.5 or higher. If it is, it rounds the number up. If it’s less than 0.5, it rounds down.

Step 3: You get a clean, whole number like 99°F—no decimals, no hassle.
