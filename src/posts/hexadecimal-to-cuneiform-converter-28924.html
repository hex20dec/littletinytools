How it Works

The binary to decimal conversion is based on the formula: decimal = binary * (2 ^ position), where position starts from 0 and increases from right to left.

The hexadecimal to cuneiform conversion is a complex process that involves mapping hexadecimal codes to their corresponding cuneiform characters. Due to the complexity of this conversion, this tool currently displays the hexadecimal code as is.
