How It Works

The Dividend Yield Calculator uses a straightforward formula to give you accurate results. Here's the breakdown:

Dividend Yield = (Annual Dividend Per Share ÷ Current Stock Price) × 100

For example, if a stock pays an annual dividend of $2 per share and the stock price is $50, the dividend yield would be 4%. Simple, right? The calculator does the heavy lifting, so you don't have to.
