How Does It Work?

Here’s the magic behind the scenes: every hex color code is converted into a numeric value, which is then mapped to one of the 12 zodiac signs. Think of it like assigning a personality to your favorite color! The formula is simple:

  • Step 1: The hex code (e.g., #FF5733) is converted into a number.
  • Step 2: This number is divided by 360 (the degrees in a circle) and mapped to a zodiac sign based on the resulting value.
  • Step 3: The tool reveals your color’s zodiac sign, complete with its symbol and name!

It’s like astrology for colors—fun, surprising, and a little cosmic!
