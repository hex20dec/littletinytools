How It Works

The Unique Paragraph Counter uses a straightforward process to analyze your text:

  1. Input Your Text: Paste your content into the text box.
  2. Split Paragraphs: The tool splits your text into individual paragraphs based on line breaks.
  3. Count and Compare: It then counts how many times each paragraph appears and identifies which ones are unique.
  4. Display Results: You’ll see the total number of paragraphs and how many of them are unique. You can even view the list of unique paragraphs with a single click!
