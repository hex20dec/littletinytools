How It Works

This tool calculates your profit or loss with a simple formula:

Profit or Loss = Selling Price - Cost Price

If the result is positive, you’ve made a profit. If it’s negative, you’ve incurred a loss. And if it’s zero? Well, you’ve broken even. It’s that straightforward!
