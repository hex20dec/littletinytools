How It Works:

The Unique Value Sorter follows a simple three-step process:

  1. Input: Paste your list of values (numbers, words, or a mix) into the text box. Separate them with commas, spaces, or new lines.
  2. Process: The tool splits your input into individual items, removes any duplicates, and sorts them. Numbers are sorted numerically, while text is sorted alphabetically.
  3. Output: Your clean, deduplicated, and sorted list appears in the output box, ready to copy and use.
