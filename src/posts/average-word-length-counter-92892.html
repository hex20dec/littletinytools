How It Works

The tool calculates the average word length using a straightforward formula:

Average Word Length = Total Number of Characters in All Words ÷ Total Number of Words

Here’s a quick example: If your text has 3 words with lengths of 5, 7, and 4 characters, the total characters would be 16, and the average word length would be 16 ÷ 3 ≈ 5.33. Easy, right?
