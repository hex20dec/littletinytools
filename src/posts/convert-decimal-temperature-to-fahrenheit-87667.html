How the Converter Works

The conversion process is based on a simple formula: F = (C * 9/5) + 32, where F is the temperature in Fahrenheit and C is the temperature in Celsius. This formula is used to calculate the equivalent temperature in Fahrenheit for a given temperature in Celsius.
