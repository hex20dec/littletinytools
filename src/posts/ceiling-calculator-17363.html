How the Ceiling Tile Calculator Works

The tool uses a straightforward formula to determine the number of tiles needed. Here's how it works:

  1. Convert your room length and width from feet to inches if necessary (1 foot = 12 inches).
  2. Divide the room length by the tile length to determine how many tiles fit along the length.
  3. Divide the room width by the tile width to determine how many tiles fit along the width.
  4. Multiply the two results to get the total number of tiles required.
  5. If there's any remainder, the tool accounts for partial tiles to ensure full coverage.

Example: For a room measuring 10 ft x 12 ft and tiles sized 2 ft x 2 ft, the calculator will tell you exactly how many tiles you need, including any partial tiles.
