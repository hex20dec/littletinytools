How It Works

The Century Difference Calculator uses a straightforward formula to determine how many centuries apart two years are. Here's the magic behind it:

Century Difference = |Year 1 - Year 2| / 100

For example, if you input 1492 and 2023, the calculator will subtract the smaller year from the larger one, divide by 100, and voilà—you get the century difference! It’s that simple.
