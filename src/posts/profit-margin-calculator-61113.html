Profit Margin Calculator

The Profit Margin Calculator is a free online tool that helps businesses and entrepreneurs calculate the profit margin of a product or service. By simply entering the revenue and cost, users can quickly determine the percentage of profit made on a sale, making it an essential tool for any business looking to optimize their pricing strategy and increase profitability. With its easy-to-use interface and accurate calculations, this profit margin calculator is a must-have for anyone looking to make informed business decisions.

This app helps you calculate the profit margin of a product or service. Simply enter the revenue and cost, and click the "Calculate" button to see the result.

How it Works

The profit margin calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the profit margin: (Revenue - Cost) / Revenue * 100. This formula takes into account the revenue generated by a sale and the cost of producing or acquiring the product or service, providing a clear picture of the profit made on each sale.
