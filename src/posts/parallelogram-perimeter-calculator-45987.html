Parallelogram Perimeter Calculator

The Parallelogram Perimeter Calculator is a free online tool that calculates the perimeter of a parallelogram based on its base and side lengths. With its simple and intuitive interface, users can easily input the base and side lengths to get the calculated perimeter, making it a valuable resource for various mathematical and real-world applications.

The formula used to calculate the perimeter is: P = 2(a + b), where P is the perimeter, a is the length of the base, and b is the length of the side.
