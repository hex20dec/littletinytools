How It Works (No Tech Jargon, Promise!)

Think of the Keyword Highlighter as your personal text detective. Here’s the simple formula it follows:

  1. Step 1: You paste or type your text into the box.
  2. Step 2: Add the keywords you want to highlight, separated by commas.
  3. Step 3: Click "Highlight," and voilà! Every instance of your keywords is instantly marked in bright colors.

It’s like magic, but without the wand—just clever coding and a whole lot of user-friendly design.
