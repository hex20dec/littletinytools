How It Works

Our Hashtag Counter is as straightforward as it gets. Simply paste or type your text into the box, and let the tool do the heavy lifting. It scans your text for any word or phrase preceded by a # symbol (that’s a hashtag, in case you didn’t know!). The tool then counts all the hashtags and displays the total number.
