How the Midpoint Calculator Works

The Midpoint Calculator uses a simple yet powerful formula to calculate the midpoint between two points. The formula is as follows: Midpoint = ((x1 + x2) / 2, (y1 + y2) / 2), where (x1, y1) and (x2, y2) are the coordinates of the two points. This formula is based on the concept of averaging the x-coordinates and y-coordinates of the two points to find the midpoint.
