How It Works:

The tool uses straightforward formulas to convert Fahrenheit to Celsius and Kelvin:

  • Fahrenheit to Celsius: Subtract 32 from the Fahrenheit value, multiply by 5, and divide by 9.
  • Fahrenheit to Kelvin: Convert to Celsius first, then add 273.15.

For example, if you enter 32°F, the tool calculates:

  • Celsius: (32 - 32) × 5 ÷ 9 = 0°C
  • Kelvin: 0°C + 273.15 = 273.15K
