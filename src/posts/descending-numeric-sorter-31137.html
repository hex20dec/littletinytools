How It Works

The Descending Numeric Sorter works like a digital assistant for your numbers. Here's the simple formula it follows:

  1. You enter numbers separated by commas (e.g., 5, 3, 9, 1, 7).
  2. The tool splits the numbers, removes any extra spaces, and ensures they're valid.
  3. It then sorts them in descending order—from the highest to the lowest value.
  4. Voilà! You get your neatly sorted list in seconds.
