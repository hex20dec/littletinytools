How the Custom Date Milestone Calculator Works

The calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the milestone date: New Date = Custom Date + Milestone Value. The milestone value is based on the selected milestone type, such as days, weeks, months, or years.

Milestone Type Milestone Value Example Calculation
Days 30 Custom Date + 30 days
Weeks 4 Custom Date + 4 weeks (28 days)
Months 6 Custom Date + 6 months
Years 2 Custom Date + 2 years
