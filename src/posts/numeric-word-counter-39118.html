How It Works

The Numeric Word Counter works by analyzing your text in three simple steps:

  1. Words: It counts every group of characters separated by spaces as a word. Even if you accidentally add extra spaces, it ignores them!
  2. Characters (with spaces): It counts every single character, including spaces, so you know exactly how much space your text takes up.
  3. Characters (without spaces): It skips the spaces and gives you a clean count of just the letters, numbers, and symbols.
