How Does It Work?

The Reading Time Estimator uses a simple formula to calculate how long it takes to read a piece of text. Here's the breakdown:

Reading Time = (Total Words ÷ 200 Words Per Minute)

The average person reads about 200 words per minute. So, the tool counts the number of words in your text, divides that by 200, and gives you an estimated reading time in minutes and seconds. It's that easy!
