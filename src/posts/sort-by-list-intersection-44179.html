How It Works

The tool works by comparing two lists of items and finding the common elements between them. Here’s the simple formula it follows:

  1. Enter items into List 1 and List 2, separating them by commas or new lines.
  2. Choose your preferred sort order: Ascending (A-Z) or Descending (Z-A).
  3. Click Find Intersection to see the common items sorted as per your selection.

It’s that straightforward! No complex formulas or manual work—just quick, accurate results.
