How Does It Work?

The magic behind the Bead String to Decimal Converter is delightfully straightforward. Here's how it works:

  1. Input a Bead String: Use the letter 'O' to represent a bead and '-' to represent an empty space. For example, 'O---OOO' means one bead, three empty spaces, and three beads.
  2. Count the Beads: The tool counts how many 'O's are in your string. In the example above, there are four 'O's.
  3. Get the Decimal Value: The total number of 'O's is your decimal number. So, 'O---OOO' becomes 4.

It’s like counting beads on an abacus, but without the manual effort!
