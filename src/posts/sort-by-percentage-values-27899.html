How It Works

The tool follows a straightforward process to sort your items by percentage:

  1. Add Items: Enter your items in the format "Item Name: Percentage" (e.g., "Project A: 75"). You can add as many items as you need.
  2. Choose Order: Decide whether you want to sort the items in ascending (lowest to highest) or descending (highest to lowest) order.
  3. Sort & View: Click the sort button, and the tool instantly organizes your data into a clear, easy-to-read table.
