Hyphenated Word Counter

The Hyphenated Word Counter is an online tool designed to help writers, editors, and language enthusiasts count the number of hyphenated words in a given text. This powerful tool utilizes advanced algorithms to scan the input text and identify words that contain hyphens, providing an accurate count and list of hyphenated words. With its user-friendly interface and rapid processing capabilities, the Hyphenated Word Counter is an indispensable resource for anyone seeking to analyze and understand the composition of their text.

How it Works

The Hyphenated Word Counter employs a simple yet effective formula to count hyphenated words: it splits the input text into individual words and then checks each word for the presence of a hyphen. If a hyphen is found, the word is added to the list of hyphenated words and the count is incremented.
