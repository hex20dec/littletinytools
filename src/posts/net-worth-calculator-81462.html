Your Net Worth:

How It Works

Calculating your net worth is as simple as subtracting what you owe (liabilities) from what you own (assets). Here's the formula:

Net Worth = Total Assets - Total Liabilities

For example, if you own a house worth $300,000 and have a mortgage of $200,000, your net worth from this asset is $100,000. Repeat this for all your assets and liabilities, and you’ll get your total net worth. Our calculator does all the math for you—no need to grab a calculator or scratch your head!
