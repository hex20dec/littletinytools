How the Volume Conversion Calculator Works

The Volume Conversion Calculator uses a simple formula to convert between different units of volume. The formula is based on the conversion factors between each unit, which are as follows:

Input Unit Output Unit Conversion Factor
Milliliters (mL) Liters (L) 0.001
Milliliters (mL) Gallons (gal) 0.000264172
Milliliters (mL) Quarts (qt) 0.00105669
Milliliters (mL) Pints (pt) 0.00211338
Milliliters (mL) Cups 0.00422675
Milliliters (mL) Fluid Ounces (fl oz) 0.033814

For example, to convert 100 milliliters to liters, you would multiply 100 by the conversion factor of 0.001, which gives you 0.1 liters.
