How It Works

The Semester Date Difference Calculator uses a simple formula to determine the time between two dates. Here’s how it breaks down:

  • Days: The total number of days between the start and end dates.
  • Weeks: The total number of weeks, calculated by dividing the total days by 7.
  • Months: The total number of months, calculated by comparing the year and month of both dates.
