How It Works

The Fuel Cost Calculator uses a straightforward formula to determine your total fuel expenses. Here’s how it breaks down:

Formula: Total Fuel Cost = (Distance ÷ Fuel Efficiency) × Fuel Price

For example, if you’re driving 300 miles with a car that gets 25 miles per gallon and gas costs $3.50 per gallon, the calculation would be:

(300 ÷ 25) × 3.50 = $42.00

It’s that simple! The calculator does all the heavy lifting for you, so you can focus on the open road.
