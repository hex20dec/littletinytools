How It Works

The Whitespace-Insensitive Sorter follows a simple yet powerful formula:

  1. Input: Paste or type your list into the text box. Each item should be on a new line.
  2. Trim Whitespace: The tool automatically removes extra spaces before and after each item (optional).
  3. Sort Order: Choose between ascending (A-Z, 1-10) or descending (Z-A, 10-1) order.
  4. Sort: Click the "Sort" button, and the tool rearranges your list alphabetically or numerically.
  5. Output: Your sorted list appears in the output box, ready to copy and use.
