How It Works

The tool uses two simple formulas to convert temperatures:

  • Celsius = Kelvin - 273.15
  • Fahrenheit = (Celsius × 9/5) + 32

Here's a quick example: If you enter 300 Kelvin, the tool calculates Celsius as 26.85°C and Fahrenheit as 80.33°F. Easy, right?
