How Does It Work?

The Keyword Density Counter follows a simple formula to give you accurate results. Here’s the breakdown:

  • Step 1: Count the total number of words in your text.
  • Step 2: Count how many times your keyword appears in the text.
  • Step 3: Divide the keyword count by the total word count and multiply by 100 to get the density percentage.

For example, if your text has 200 words and your keyword appears 5 times, the density is (5 ÷ 200) × 100 = 2.5%. Easy, right?
