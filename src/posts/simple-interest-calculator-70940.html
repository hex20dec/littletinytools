Simple Interest Calculator

The Simple Interest Calculator is a free online tool designed to calculate the interest earned on an investment or loan, providing users with a quick and accurate method to determine the total amount of interest accrued over a specified period of time, making it an essential resource for individuals, investors, and financial professionals looking to make informed decisions about their financial portfolios.

How it Works

The Simple Interest Calculator uses a straightforward formula to calculate the interest earned: Interest = Principal x Rate x Time. This formula takes into account the initial investment (Principal), the interest rate (Rate), and the time period (Time) to calculate the total interest accrued.
