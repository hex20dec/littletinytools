How it Works

The formula to calculate the area of a square is simple: Area = side length × side length. This means that if you know the length of one side of the square, you can easily calculate the area by multiplying that length by itself. For example, if the side length of a square is 4 meters, the area would be 4 × 4 = 16 square meters.
