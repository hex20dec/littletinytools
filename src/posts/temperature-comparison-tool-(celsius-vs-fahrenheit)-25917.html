How it Works

The Temperature Conversion Tool uses a simple formula to convert temperatures between Celsius and Fahrenheit. The formula is as follows: to convert Celsius to Fahrenheit, multiply the Celsius temperature by 9/5 and then add 32. To convert Fahrenheit to Celsius, subtract 32 from the Fahrenheit temperature and then multiply by 5/9. This formula is applied instantly when you enter a temperature value and click the conversion button, providing you with an accurate and rapid conversion.
