IST to UTC Time Converter

This tool is designed to convert Indian Standard Time (IST) to Coordinated Universal Time (UTC), allowing users to easily switch between the two time zones. The conversion process is straightforward, and this tool provides a simple way to perform the conversion, making it a valuable resource for individuals who need to communicate or coordinate with people in different parts of the world.

How the Tool Works

The conversion process from IST to UTC is based on the time difference between the two time zones. Since IST is 5 hours and 30 minutes ahead of UTC, the tool subtracts this difference from the input IST time to get the equivalent UTC time. This can be expressed through a simple formula: UTC Time = IST Time - 5 hours 30 minutes.
