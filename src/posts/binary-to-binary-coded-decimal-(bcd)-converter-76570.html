How the Binary to Decimal Converter Works

The Binary to Decimal Converter employs a simple yet effective formula to convert binary numbers to decimal: Decimal Number = (2^n-1 * d_n-1) + ... + (2^1 * d_1) + (2^0 * d_0), where d_i represents each binary digit and n denotes the position of the most significant bit.
