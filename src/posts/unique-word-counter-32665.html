How It Works

The Unique Word Counter is designed to be simple yet powerful. Here’s the magic behind it:

  1. Your text is cleaned up by removing punctuation and converting everything to lowercase. This ensures words like "Hello" and "hello" are counted as the same word.
  2. The tool splits your text into individual words, ignoring spaces and empty entries.
  3. It then filters out duplicates, leaving only the unique words.
  4. Finally, it counts these unique words and displays the result instantly.

It’s like having a sieve for your words—only the unique ones make it through!
