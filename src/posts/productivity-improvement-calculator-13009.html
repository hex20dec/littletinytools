How It Works

The Productivity Improvement Calculator operates based on a simple yet effective formula that considers your current productivity level, your desired productivity level, and the time spent on distracting activities. The formula can be explained as follows: Potential Productivity Improvement = ((Desired Productivity - Current Productivity) / Current Productivity) * 100. This calculation provides a clear percentage of how much you can potentially improve your productivity by adjusting your daily routine and minimizing time wasted on non-essential tasks.
