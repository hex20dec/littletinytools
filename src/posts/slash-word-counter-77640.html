How It Works

The Slash Word Counter works by taking your input text and splitting it into individual words or phrases wherever it finds a slash (/). It then counts these separated items and gives you the total number. Think of it as a digital assistant that does the counting for you—no math required!

Here’s a quick example: If you input "apple/banana/orange", the tool splits it into three parts—"apple", "banana", and "orange"—and tells you there are 3 words/phrases. Easy, right?
