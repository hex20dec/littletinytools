Enter a list of words or phrases, and they will be sorted by their last character.

This tool sorts words or phrases based on their last character. No external data is used.

How It Works:

Here’s the secret sauce: the tool looks at the last character of every word or phrase you enter. It then sorts them alphabetically based on those final characters. If two words end with the same letter, it sorts them alphabetically as a tiebreaker. It’s like giving each word a unique identity based on its ending!
