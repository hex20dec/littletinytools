How It Works

The Circle Area Calculator uses a straightforward formula to determine the area of a circle. The formula is:

Area = π × (Radius × Radius)

Here’s how it breaks down:

  • 1. You input the radius of the circle (the distance from the center to the edge).
  • 2. The tool multiplies the radius by itself (squaring it).
  • 3. It then multiplies the result by π (approximately 3.1416).
  • 4. The final result is the area in square units, displayed instantly.
