Unit Cost Calculator

The Unit Cost Calculator is a free online tool that helps businesses and individuals calculate the cost per unit of a product or item. By simply entering the total cost and total quantity, users can quickly determine the unit cost, making it an essential tool for inventory management, product pricing, and cost calculation. With its easy-to-use interface and accurate calculations, this unit cost calculator is a valuable resource for anyone looking to optimize their business operations and make informed financial decisions.

Example Usage

To use the unit cost calculator, simply enter the total cost and total quantity of the product or item, and click the "Calculate Unit Cost" button. The unit cost will be displayed below the button.

For example, if the total cost is $100 and the total quantity is 10, the unit cost would be $10 per unit.

How it Works

The unit cost calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the cost per unit: Unit Cost = Total Cost / Total Quantity. This formula takes into account the total cost of the product or item and the total quantity, providing an accurate calculation of the unit cost.
