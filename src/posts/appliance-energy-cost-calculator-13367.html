Appliance Energy Cost Calculator

The Appliance Energy Cost Calculator is a free online tool that helps you estimate the daily, weekly, monthly, and yearly energy costs of your household appliances. By entering the appliance's power consumption, usage hours, and the cost of electricity, you can calculations to reduce your energy consumption and lower your utility bills, promoting a more sustainable and energy-efficient lifestyle.

Tips to Reduce Energy Consumption:
  • Use energy-efficient appliances
  • Turn off lights, electronics, and appliances when not in use
  • Adjust your thermostat to use less energy for heating and cooling
  • Use power strips to plug in electronics and turn them off with one switch

How the Appliance Energy Cost Calculator Works

The calculator uses a simple formula to estimate the energy costs: Daily Energy Cost = (Power Consumption x Usage Hours) / 1000 x Cost of Electricity. This formula is then used to calculate the weekly, monthly, and yearly energy costs.
