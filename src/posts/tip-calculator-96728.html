How It Works

This tool uses a simple formula to calculate your tip and total bill. Here’s the breakdown:

Tip Amount = Bill Amount × (Tip Percentage ÷ 100)

Total Bill = Bill Amount + Tip Amount

If you’re splitting the bill, the tool also calculates how much each person owes by dividing the total bill by the number of people. It’s math made easy!
