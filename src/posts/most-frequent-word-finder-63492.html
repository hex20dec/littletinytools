How the Binary to Decimal Converter Works

The Binary to Decimal Converter uses a simple formula to convert binary numbers to their decimal equivalents. Decimal Equivalent = (2^0 \* b0) + (2^1 \* b1) + (2^2 \* b2) + ... + (2^n \* bn)
