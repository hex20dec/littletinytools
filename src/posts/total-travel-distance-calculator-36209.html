How It Works

The Total Travel Distance Calculator is as simple as 1-2-3. First, enter a distance (like "10 km") or a location (like "New York to Boston"). Click the + button to add it to your list. Repeat this for all the distances or locations you want to include. Once your list is complete, hit Calculate Total Distance, and voilà—your total distance appears instantly. Need to start over? Just click Reset and begin again. It’s like having a personal travel assistant in your pocket!
