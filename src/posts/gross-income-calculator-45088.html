How It Works

The Gross Income Calculator uses a straightforward formula to determine your annual earnings:

Gross Income = Hourly Wage × Hours Per Week × Weeks Per Year

For example, if you earn $20 per hour, work 40 hours a week, and work 50 weeks a year, your gross income would be $40,000. It’s that simple!
