How the Fence Length Calculator Works

The Fence Length Calculator uses a simple yet effective formula to calculate the total length of fence required. The formula takes into account the shape, size, number of gates, and number of corners to provide an accurate calculation. For example, for a rectangular shape, the formula is: Perimeter = 2 * (Length + Width). The calculator then subtracts the total width of the gates and adds the number of corners to provide the total length of fence required.
