Percentage Calculator

The Percentage Calculator is a free online tool designed to calculate percentages with ease. Whether you need to determine a percentage of a number, calculate the percentage increase or decrease of a value, or simply understand how to work with percentages, this calculator is here to help. With its simple and intuitive interface, you can quickly and accurately perform percentage calculations, making it an essential resource for students, professionals, and anyone looking to simplify their calculations.

How the Percentage Calculator Works

The Percentage Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate percentages. To calculate a percentage of a number, you can use the formula: (number / 100) * percentage. For example, if you want to calculate 25% of 100, the calculation would be (100 / 100) * 25 = 25.
