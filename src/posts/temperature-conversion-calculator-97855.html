How It Works

This tool uses simple formulas to convert temperatures between Celsius (°C), Fahrenheit (°F), and Kelvin (K). Here’s the breakdown:

  • Celsius to Fahrenheit: Multiply by 9, divide by 5, then add 32.
  • Fahrenheit to Celsius: Subtract 32, multiply by 5, then divide by 9.
  • Celsius to Kelvin: Just add 273.15.
  • Kelvin to Celsius: Subtract 273.15.
  • Fahrenheit to Kelvin: Subtract 32, multiply by 5/9, then add 273.15.
  • Kelvin to Fahrenheit: Subtract 273.15, multiply by 9/5, then add 32.
