How It Works

The tool works by analyzing your input text and applying a simple formula based on your selected case type. Here's the breakdown:

  • Uppercase: Converts all letters to uppercase (e.g., "HELLO WORLD").
  • Lowercase: Converts all letters to lowercase (e.g., "hello world").
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each word (e.g., "Hello World").
  • Sentence Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each sentence (e.g., "Hello world.").
  • Alternating Case: Alternates between uppercase and lowercase letters for a quirky look (e.g., "HeLlO WoRlD").

Once you’ve pasted your text and selected your desired case, the tool instantly counts your words, characters, and sentences while converting the text. It’s like magic, but with fewer rabbits and more productivity!
