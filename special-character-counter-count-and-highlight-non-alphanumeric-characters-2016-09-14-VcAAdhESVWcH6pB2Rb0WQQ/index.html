How It Works

The Special Character Counter works by scanning your text and identifying any character that isn't a letter, number, or space. Think of it as a detective that spots all the "odd ones out" in your text. Here's the simple breakdown:

  1. Input Your Text: Paste or type your text into the box.
  2. Count Special Characters: Click the button, and the tool will count every special character (like !, @, #, $, etc.).
  3. Highlight Option: Toggle the highlight feature to visually spot all the special characters in your text.
  4. Reset: Clear everything with a single click and start fresh.

It’s that simple! No complicated steps, no hidden fees—just quick and accurate results.
