How It Works

The Dash Counter works like a magic wand for your text. Simply paste or type your content into the text box, click the "Count Dashes" button, and voilà—it instantly scans your text and counts every dash (-) for you. The formula is straightforward: it looks for every instance of the dash character in your text and tallies them up. No complex algorithms, no hidden steps—just pure simplicity.
