How it Works

The Binary Converter tool utilizes a simple formula to convert binary numbers to decimal: Decimal Number = (2^n * d_n) + (2^(n-1) * d_(n-1)) + ... + (2^0 * d_0), where d_i is the digit at position i in the binary number.
