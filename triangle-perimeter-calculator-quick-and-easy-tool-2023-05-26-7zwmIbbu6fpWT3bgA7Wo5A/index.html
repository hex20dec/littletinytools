The Triangle Perimeter Calculator works on a simple principle: it adds up the lengths of all three sides of a triangle to give you the perimeter. Think of it as wrapping a measuring tape around the triangle—every side counts! Just input the lengths of Side A, Side B, and Side C, and the calculator does the rest.
