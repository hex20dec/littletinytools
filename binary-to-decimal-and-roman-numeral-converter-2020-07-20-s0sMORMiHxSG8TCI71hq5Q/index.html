How it Works

The binary to decimal conversion is based on the positional notation of the binary number system, where each digit (or bit) represents a power of 2. The formula to convert binary to decimal is:

Decimal = (bn × 2n) + (bn-1 × 2n-1) + ... + (b0 × 20)

Where bi is the i-th bit of the binary number and n is the number of bits.
