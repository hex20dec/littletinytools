How the Eclipse Cycle Difference Calculator Works

The Eclipse Cycle Difference Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the difference between two eclipse cycles. The formula is based on the absolute difference between the two dates, which is then converted to days, months, and years.

The formula can be explained as follows: Difference = |Date2 - Date1|, where Date1 and Date2 are the dates of the two eclipses. The difference is then converted to days, months, and years using the following conversions: 1 day = 24 hours, 1 month = 30 days, and 1 year = 365 days.
