How Does It Work?

The tool works by taking your local date and time, adjusting it based on the timezone you select, and then converting it to Coordinated Universal Time (UTC). Here’s a simple breakdown:

Step 1: You select a date and time in your local timezone (or any other timezone you choose).
Step 2: The tool calculates the difference between your selected timezone and UTC.
Step 3: It applies the adjustment and displays the exact UTC time. Easy, right?
