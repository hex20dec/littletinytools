How It Works

The Pipe Counter operates on a simple principle: start with a number (or zero), and adjust it as needed. Here's the breakdown:

  • Increment (+): Adds 1 to the current count.
  • Decrement (-): Subtracts 1 from the current count (if it’s greater than zero).
  • Reset: Brings the count back to zero, ready to start fresh.

It’s like having a digital tally system in your pocket—no batteries required!
