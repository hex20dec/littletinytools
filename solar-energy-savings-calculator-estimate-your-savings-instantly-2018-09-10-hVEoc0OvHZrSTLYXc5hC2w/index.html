How Does the Solar Savings Calculator Work?

The calculator uses a simple formula to estimate your potential savings. Here’s the breakdown:

  • Step 1: Your monthly electricity usage (kWh) is multiplied by the cost per kWh to determine your current monthly electricity cost.
  • Step 2: The size of your solar panel system (kW) is multiplied by the average daily sunlight hours (assumed to be 4 hours) and then by 30 to estimate the monthly energy production.
  • Step 3: The energy produced by your solar panels is multiplied by the cost per kWh to calculate your estimated monthly savings.
  • Step 4: Multiply your monthly savings by 12 to see your annual savings.
