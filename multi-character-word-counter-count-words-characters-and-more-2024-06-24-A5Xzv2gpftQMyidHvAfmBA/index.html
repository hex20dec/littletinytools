The Multi-Character Word Counter works by analyzing your text in three simple steps:

  1. It splits your text into individual words by looking for spaces and line breaks.
  2. It counts the total number of characters, including and excluding spaces, to give you a complete picture of your text’s length.
  3. If you’re curious about a specific character (like how many times you’ve used the letter “E”), it scans your text and gives you the exact count.
