How It Works

Here’s the magic behind the Group Expense Tracker: it takes the total amount paid, divides it equally among the people sharing the cost, and then calculates who owes whom. For example, if Alice pays $100 for groceries and splits it among herself, Bob, and Charlie, the tool divides the amount into $33.33 per person. It then adjusts the balances accordingly, showing who’s owed money and who needs to pay up. It’s like having a personal accountant for group expenses!
