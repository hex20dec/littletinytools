How the Square Bracket Counter App Works

The Square Bracket Counter App uses a simple formula to count and balance square brackets in your text. The formula is as follows: for each character in the text, if the character is an opening square bracket "[", increment the opening bracket counter; if the character is a closing square bracket "]", increment the closing bracket counter. The app then displays the total number of opening and closing square brackets, providing a quick and easy way to check if your brackets are balanced.
