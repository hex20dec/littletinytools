Why does sorting numbers always feel like solving a puzzle? Whether it's organizing exam scores, ranking sales figures, or just figuring out who owes what on a group bill, sorting numbers in descending order can be a headache. Enter the Descending Value Sorter—your new best friend for turning chaos into order. Simply type in your numbers, hit sort, and watch as they magically arrange themselves from highest to lowest. How It Works

The Descending Value Sorter takes your list of numbers, separates them by commas, and rearranges them from the highest to the lowest value. Think of it like lining up your favorite snacks from the tastiest to the least tasty—except with numbers! Here’s a quick example:

  • Input: 5, 3, 9, 1, 7
  • Output: 9, 7, 5, 3, 1

It’s that simple! No math degree required.
