How It Works

The Descending Value Sorter takes your list of numbers, separates them by commas, and rearranges them from the highest to the lowest value. Think of it like lining up your favorite snacks from the tastiest to the least tasty—except with numbers! Here’s a quick example:

  • Input: 5, 3, 9, 1, 7
  • Output: 9, 7, 5, 3, 1

It’s that simple! No math degree required.
