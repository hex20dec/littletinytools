How it Works

The Temperature Average Difference Calculator uses a simple yet effective formula to calculate the average temperature and temperature difference. The formula can be explained as: (Temperature 1 + Temperature 2) / 2 = Average Temperature, and Temperature 1 - Temperature 2 = Temperature Difference.
