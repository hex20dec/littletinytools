How It Works

This tool takes your binary input (a number made up of 0s and 1s) and converts it into its duodecimal (base-12) equivalent. Here’s the magic formula in plain English:

  1. First, the binary number is converted into its decimal (base-10) form. For example, binary 1010 becomes decimal 10.
  2. Next, the decimal number is converted into duodecimal. The decimal 10 becomes duodecimal A.
