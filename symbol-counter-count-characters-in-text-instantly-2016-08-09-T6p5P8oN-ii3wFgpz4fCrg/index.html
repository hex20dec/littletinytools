Symbol Counter

Ever found yourself squinting at a screen, trying to count how many times a specific symbol appears in a block of text? Maybe you're checking for commas in an essay, counting hashtags in a social media post, or verifying formatting in a document. It's tedious, right? That's where the Symbol Counter swoops in to save the day! This simple yet powerful tool lets you paste your text, pick a symbol, and instantly get the count—no more manual scanning or second-guessing. Whether you're a writer, coder, or just someone who loves efficiency, this tool is your new best friend. Say goodbye to frustration and hello to quick, accurate results with just a click!

Enter your text and the symbol you want to count. The app will display the number of occurrences.

How Does the Symbol Counter Work?

It’s as simple as 1-2-3! Here's the formula:

  1. Input your text: Paste or type the text you want to analyze into the text box.
  2. Choose your symbol: Enter the specific symbol, character, or punctuation mark you want to count.
  3. Click "Count": The tool scans your text and instantly tells you how many times the symbol appears.

For example, if you paste the sentence "Hello, world! How are you, world?" and count the comma (,), the tool will tell you it appears twice. Easy peasy!
