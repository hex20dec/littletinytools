How It Works

Our KST to UTC Converter uses a simple formula to calculate the equivalent UTC time. Since KST is always 9 hours ahead of UTC, all you need to do is subtract 9 hours from the KST time. If the result goes below 0, we wrap it around to the previous day by adding 24 hours. For example, if it's 14:30 KST, subtracting 9 hours gives you 05:30 UTC. Easy, right?
