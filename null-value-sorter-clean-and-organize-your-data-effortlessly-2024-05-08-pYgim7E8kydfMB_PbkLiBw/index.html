How does it work?

The Null Value Sorter takes your input data and applies a simple yet effective formula:

  • It scans each line of your input.
  • If a line is empty, null, or just whitespace, it gets flagged as a "null value."
  • Everything else? That’s your "valid data."

It’s like having a digital assistant who sorts your laundry into "clean" and "dirty" piles—except it’s way faster and never complains!
