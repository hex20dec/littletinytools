How It Works

The Character Counter works by analyzing your text in real-time. Here's the breakdown:

  • Characters: Every single letter, number, space, and symbol counts.
  • Words: Groups of characters separated by spaces are counted as words.
  • Sentences: A sentence ends with a period, exclamation mark, or question mark.
  • Paragraphs: Each block of text separated by a line break is counted as a paragraph.

It’s as simple as typing or pasting your text—no complicated steps or hidden tricks!
