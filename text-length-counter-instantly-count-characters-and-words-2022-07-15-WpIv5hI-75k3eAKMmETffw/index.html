How It Works

The Text Length Counter works by analyzing your input text in real-time. Here’s the breakdown:

  • Characters: Every single letter, number, space, or symbol is counted as a character.
  • Words: Words are counted by splitting the text into segments based on spaces. Any group of characters separated by spaces is considered a word.

For example, the sentence "Hello, world!" has 13 characters and 2 words. It’s that simple!
