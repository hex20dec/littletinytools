How It Works

The tool calculates the difference between two business quarters by converting each quarter and year into a total number of quarters since the year 2000. Here’s the breakdown:

  • Step 1: Select the starting quarter and year.
  • Step 2: Select the ending quarter and year.
  • Step 3: The tool calculates the total quarters for both dates and subtracts the starting total from the ending total.
  • Step 4: You get the exact number of quarters between the two dates!

For example, if you select Q1 2020 as the starting point and Q3 2022 as the ending point, the tool computes the difference as 10 quarters. It’s that simple!
