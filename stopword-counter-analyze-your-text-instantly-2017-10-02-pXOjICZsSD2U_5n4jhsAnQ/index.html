    How Does the Stopword Counter Work?

    It’s as easy as 1-2-3! The tool scans your text and checks each word against a predefined list of stopwords (common words like "the," "and," "is," etc.). Here’s the formula it uses:

    Total Stopwords = Number of matches in your text

    For example, if your text has 10 occurrences of "the," 5 of "and," and 3 of "is," the tool will count them all and give you the total.
