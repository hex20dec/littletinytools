How It Works

Converting PST to CET is all about adding 9 hours to your Pacific Standard Time. Here's the simple formula:

CET Time = PST Time + 9 Hours

If the result goes past 24:00, it wraps around to the next day. For example, if it's 17:00 PST, adding 9 hours gives you 02:00 CET the next day. Easy, right?
