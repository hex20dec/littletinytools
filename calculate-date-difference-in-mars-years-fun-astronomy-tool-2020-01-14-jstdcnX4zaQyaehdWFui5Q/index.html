How Does It Work?

It’s simple! Here’s the magic behind the tool:

  1. Enter your two Earth dates (like your birthday and today’s date).
  2. The tool calculates the difference in Earth days between the two dates.
  3. It then converts those Earth days into Mars years using the formula: 1 Mars Year = 687 Earth Days.
  4. You get the result in both Earth days and Mars years—easy peasy!

For example, if you want to know how many Mars years have passed since your last birthday, just plug in the dates, and you’ll have your answer faster than a Martian dust storm!
