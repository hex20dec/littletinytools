Calculate the difference in months between two dates.

The Months Difference Calculator works by breaking down the dates into years and months. Here’s how it calculates the difference in simple terms:

  1. It first determines the difference in years between the two dates.
  2. Then, it calculates the difference in months within those years.
  3. Finally, it combines the two to give you the total number of months.

For example, if your start date is January 2022 and your end date is March 2023, the calculator will tell you it’s 14 months apart. Easy, right?
