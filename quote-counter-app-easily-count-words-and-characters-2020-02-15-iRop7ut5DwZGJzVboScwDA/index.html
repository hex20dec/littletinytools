How it Works

The Quote Counter App uses a simple formula to calculate the word and character count of a given text. The formula is based on the number of spaces and characters in the text, and it works as follows: the app splits the text into individual words and counts the number of words, then it counts the total number of characters in the text, including spaces and punctuation.
