How It Works

The Product Batch Cost Calculator uses a straightforward formula to give you accurate results. For each component, it multiplies the cost per unit by the quantity to calculate the total cost for that item. Then, it adds up all the component costs along with any additional expenses (like shipping or labor) to give you the final batch cost. It’s as simple as:

(Cost per Unit × Quantity) + Additional Costs = Total Batch Cost
