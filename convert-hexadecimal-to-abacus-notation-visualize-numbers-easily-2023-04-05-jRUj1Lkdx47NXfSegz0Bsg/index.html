How It Works

Our tool works by transforming each digit of a hexadecimal number (0-F) into a visual abacus representation. Here's the simple breakdown:

  1. Input a Hexadecimal Number: Enter any valid hexadecimal number (e.g., 1A3F).
  2. Convert to Abacus: The tool maps each hex digit to a column of beads on an abacus, where each bead represents a value of 1.
  3. Visualize the Value: The active beads (up to 15 per column) show the decimal equivalent of each hex digit.

For example, the hex digit "A" (which is 10 in decimal) will display 10 active beads in its column. It's like counting on your fingers, but way cooler!
