How It Works

The Alphabet to Decimal Converter uses a simple formula: each letter of the alphabet is assigned a number based on its position. For example, A is 1, B is 2, all the way up to Z, which is 26. The tool takes your input, breaks it down into individual letters, and maps each one to its corresponding value. It’s like giving each letter its own unique ID number!
