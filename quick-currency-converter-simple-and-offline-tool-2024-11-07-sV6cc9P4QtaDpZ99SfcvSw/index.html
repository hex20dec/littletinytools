How It Works

The tool uses a simple formula to convert your amount from one currency to another:

Converted Amount = Original Amount × Exchange Rate

For example, if you want to convert 100 USD to EUR and the exchange rate is 0.85, the calculation would be:

100 USD × 0.85 = 85 EUR
