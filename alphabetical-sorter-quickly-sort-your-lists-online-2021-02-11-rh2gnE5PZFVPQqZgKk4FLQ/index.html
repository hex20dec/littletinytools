How It Works

The Alphabetical Sorter uses a simple yet effective formula to organize your list:

  1. Input your list of items, with each item on a new line.
  2. Choose your preferred sorting order: ascending (A-Z) or descending (Z-A).
  3. Click "Sort List" and watch as your items are instantly organized.
