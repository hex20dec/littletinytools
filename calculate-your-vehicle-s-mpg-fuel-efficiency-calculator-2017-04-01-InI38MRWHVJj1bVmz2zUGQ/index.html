How Does the MPG Calculator Work?

The formula behind the MPG Calculator is straightforward: Miles Driven ÷ Gallons Used = MPG. It’s like dividing the distance you traveled by the amount of fuel your car consumed. For example, if you drove 300 miles and used 10 gallons of gas, your MPG would be 30. The calculator does the math for you, so you don’t have to!
