How It Works

The magic behind this tool is a simple formula: take your Fahrenheit temperature, subtract 32, multiply by 5/9, and then add 273.15. That’s it! For example, if you input 32°F (freezing point of water), the tool calculates it as 273.15K—the same temperature in Kelvin. It’s like translating languages, but for temperature scales!
