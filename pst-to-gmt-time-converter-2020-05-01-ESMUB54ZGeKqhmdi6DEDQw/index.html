How the PST to GMT Time Converter Works

The converter uses a simple formula to calculate the equivalent GMT time based on the input PST time. The formula is as follows: GMT = PST - 8 hours. This means that if it is 12:00 PM (noon) in PST, the equivalent GMT time would be 8:00 PM.
