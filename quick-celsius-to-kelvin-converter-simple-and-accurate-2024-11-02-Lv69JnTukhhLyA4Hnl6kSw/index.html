How Does It Work?

The formula behind this tool is straightforward: Kelvin = Celsius + 273.15. This means you take the temperature in Celsius, add 273.15 to it, and you’ve got the temperature in Kelvin. It’s as simple as that! Whether you're working with freezing temperatures or boiling points, this tool ensures accurate conversions every time.
