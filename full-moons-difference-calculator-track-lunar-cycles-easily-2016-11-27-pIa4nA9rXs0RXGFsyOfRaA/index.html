How It Works

The Full Moons Difference Calculator uses a simple formula to determine the number of full moons between two dates. Here’s the breakdown:

  • Step 1: Enter your start and end dates.
  • Step 2: The tool calculates the total number of days between the two dates.
  • Step 3: It divides the total days by the average length of a lunar cycle (29.53 days).
  • Step 4: The result is rounded to the nearest whole number to give you the total full moons.
