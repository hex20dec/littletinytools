This tool counts sentences based on periods, exclamation marks, and question marks. Words are counted based on spaces.

Here’s how it works: The tool splits your text into sentences using periods, exclamation marks, and question marks. Then, it counts the total number of words by splitting the text at every space. Finally, it calculates the average sentence length by dividing the total words by the total sentences. It’s like having a personal writing coach who gives you quick, actionable feedback!
