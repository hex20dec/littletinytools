How It Works

The VAT Calculator follows a straightforward formula to give you accurate results:

  1. VAT Amount: Multiply the original amount by the VAT rate (as a percentage), then divide by 100.
  2. Total Amount: Add the VAT amount to the original amount.

For example, if you enter £100 with a VAT rate of 20%, the tool calculates:

  • VAT Amount = (£100 × 20) ÷ 100 = £20
  • Total Amount = £100 + £20 = £120
