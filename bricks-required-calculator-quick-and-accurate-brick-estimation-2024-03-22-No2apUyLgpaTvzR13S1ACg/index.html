How It Works

The Bricks Required Calculator works by calculating the volume of your wall and dividing it by the volume of a single brick (including mortar thickness if provided). Here’s the simplified formula:

Total Bricks = (Wall Volume) / (Brick Volume + Mortar Volume)

Wall Volume is calculated by multiplying the wall’s length, height, and thickness. Brick Volume is determined by the brick’s dimensions, and mortar thickness is factored in if you provide it. The result is rounded up to ensure you have enough bricks for your project.
