How It Works

Converting MST to EST is as easy as 1-2-3 (or should we say 1-2 hours ahead, since EST is 2 hours ahead of MST). Here’s the formula in plain English:

EST Time = MST Time + 2 Hours

For example, if it’s 3:00 PM in Mountain Standard Time, just add 2 hours, and you’ll get 5:00 PM in Eastern Standard Time. The tool does all the heavy lifting for you, so you don’t have to worry about AM/PM confusion or time zone math.
