How It Works

The tool works like a digital calculator, but it's tailored specifically for adding Fahrenheit temperatures. Here's the simple formula it follows:

  • Step 1: Enter a temperature in Fahrenheit (e.g., 32°F).
  • Step 2: Click "Add" to include it in the running total.
  • Step 3: The tool adds the new temperature to the existing total and displays the updated sum.
