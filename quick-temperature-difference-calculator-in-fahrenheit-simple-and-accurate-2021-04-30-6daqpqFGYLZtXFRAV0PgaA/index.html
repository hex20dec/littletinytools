How It Works

The Temperature Difference Calculator works by taking two Fahrenheit temperatures you provide and calculating the absolute difference between them. Here's the formula it uses:

Formula: Difference = |Temperature 1 - Temperature 2|

For example, if you enter 85°F and 70°F, the tool calculates |85 - 70| = 15°F. It's that simple!
