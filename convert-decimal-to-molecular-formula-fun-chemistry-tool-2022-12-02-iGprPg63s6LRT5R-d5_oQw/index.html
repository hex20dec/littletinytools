  • 1 = H (Hydrogen)
  • 2 = He (Helium)
  • 3 = Li (Lithium)
  • 4 = Be (Beryllium)

How It Works

The tool works by mapping each digit of your decimal number to its corresponding element in the periodic table. Here's the formula in plain English:

Each digit in your number → Find its matching element → Combine the elements into a molecular formula.

For example, if you input "123", the tool breaks it down into digits: 1 (H), 2 (He), and 3 (Li). Then, it combines them into the formula "H He Li". Easy, right?
