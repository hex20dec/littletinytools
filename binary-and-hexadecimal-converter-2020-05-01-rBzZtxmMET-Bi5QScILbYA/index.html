How the Binary and Hexadecimal Converter Tool Works

For hexadecimal to base-62 conversion, we use the formula: Base-62 Number = Σ (Hexadecimal Digit * 16^m), where m is the position of the hexadecimal digit, starting from 0 for the least significant digit.
