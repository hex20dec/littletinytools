How the Calorie Calculator Works

The Calorie Calculator uses a formula to estimate an individual's daily calorie needs based on their BMR and activity level. The formula is as follows:

BMR (men) = 66 + (6.2 x weight in kg) + (12.7 x height in cm) - (6.76 x age in years)

BMR (women) = 655 + (4.35 x weight in kg) + (4.7 x height in cm) - (4.7 x age in years)

Daily calorie needs are then calculated by multiplying the BMR by an activity factor, ranging from 1.2 for sedentary individuals to 1.9 for extra active individuals.

Activity Level Calorie Needs Multiplier
Sedentary (little or no exercise) 1.2
Lightly active (light exercise/sports 1-3 days/week) 1.375
Moderately active (moderate exercise/sports 3-5 days/week) 1.55
Very active (hard exercise/sports 6-7 days a week) 1.725
Extra active (very hard exercise/sports & physical job or 2x training) 1.9
