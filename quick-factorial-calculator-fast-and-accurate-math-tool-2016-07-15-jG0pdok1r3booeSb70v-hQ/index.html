Enter a number to calculate its factorial.

Only positive integers are allowed.

How does it work? It's simpler than you think! A factorial is just the product of all positive integers up to that number. For example, the factorial of 5 (written as 5!) is calculated as 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1, which equals 120. Our tool does all the heavy lifting for you—just enter a number and let it handle the rest.
