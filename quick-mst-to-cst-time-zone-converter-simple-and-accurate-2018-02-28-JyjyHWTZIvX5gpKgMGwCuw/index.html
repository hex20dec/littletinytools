How It Works

The MST to CST Converter follows a simple formula: add 1 hour to the given MST time. Why? Because Central Standard Time (CST) is 1 hour ahead of Mountain Standard Time (MST). For example, if it's 3:00 PM MST, it becomes 4:00 PM CST. The tool also lets you switch between 12-hour and 24-hour formats, so you can view the time in a way that makes the most sense for you.
