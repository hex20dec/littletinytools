How It Works

The Public Holidays Difference Calculator is as simple as it gets. Here’s the magic formula behind it:

  • 1. Select your first country from the dropdown menu.
  • 2. Choose the second country you want to compare it with.
  • 3. Hit the Calculate button.

The tool instantly shows you the number of public holidays in each country and calculates the difference. No math, no stress—just pure holiday bliss!
