How It Works

The Slashes Counter works by scanning your text and counting every forward slash (/) it finds. Think of it as a digital tally counter, but way faster and more accurate. Here’s the formula it follows:

Total Slashes = Number of "/" in your text

For example, if your text is https://example.com/path, the tool will count 4 slashes. Easy, right?
