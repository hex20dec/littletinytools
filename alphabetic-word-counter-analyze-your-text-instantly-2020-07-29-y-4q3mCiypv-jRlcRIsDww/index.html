How It Works

The Alphabetic Word Counter is as straightforward as it gets. Here's the magic behind it:

  1. You paste or type your text into the box.
  2. The tool splits your text into individual words.
  3. It then checks the first letter of each word and tallies how many words start with each letter of the alphabet.
  4. Finally, it displays the results in a clean, easy-to-read table.
