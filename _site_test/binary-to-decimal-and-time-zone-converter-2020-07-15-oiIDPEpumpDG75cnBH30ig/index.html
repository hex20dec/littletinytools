How it Works

The binary to decimal converter uses a simple formula to convert binary numbers to decimal numbers. The formula is based on the positional value of each digit in the binary number, with each digit representing a power of 2. The formula is as follows: decimal = (binary digit * 2^n), where n is the position of the digit, starting from 0 on the right.
