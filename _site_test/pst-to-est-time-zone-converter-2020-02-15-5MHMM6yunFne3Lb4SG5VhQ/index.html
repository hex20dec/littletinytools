PST to EST Converter

The PST to EST Converter is a free online tool that helps you convert time from Pacific Standard Time (PST) to Eastern Standard Time (EST). This tool is perfect for those who need to schedule meetings, appointments, or events across different time zones. With its simple and user-friendly interface, you can easily convert time and stay on top of your schedule. Whether you're a business professional, a traveler, or just someone who needs to keep track of time across different zones, this tool is an essential resource for you.

How it Works

The PST to EST Converter uses a simple formula to convert time: EST = PST + 3 hours. This means that for every hour in PST, you add 3 hours to get the equivalent time in EST. For example, if it's 10:00 AM PST, the equivalent time in EST would be 1:00 PM EST.
