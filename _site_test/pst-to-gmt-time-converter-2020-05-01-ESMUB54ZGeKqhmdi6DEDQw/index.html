PST to GMT Time Converter

The PST to GMT Time Converter is an online tool that allows users to easily convert Pacific Standard Time (PST) to Greenwich Mean Time (GMT). This tool is particularly useful for individuals who need to schedule meetings, appointments, or events across different time zones. With its simple and intuitive interface, users can quickly and accurately convert PST to GMT, eliminating the need for manual calculations or complex time zone conversions.

How the PST to GMT Time Converter Works

The converter uses a simple formula to calculate the equivalent GMT time based on the input PST time. The formula is as follows: GMT = PST - 8 hours. This means that if it is 12:00 PM (noon) in PST, the equivalent GMT time would be 8:00 PM.
