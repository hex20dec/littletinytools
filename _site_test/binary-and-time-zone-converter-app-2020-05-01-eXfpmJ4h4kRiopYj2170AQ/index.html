How it Works

The binary to decimal conversion is based on the formula: Decimal = (Binary Number) in base 2, where each binary digit (0 or 1) is multiplied by the corresponding power of 2 and added together. For time zone conversion, the app uses the formula: Target Date/Time = Original Date/Time + (Target Time Zone Offset - Original Time Zone Offset), where the offsets are calculated in minutes.
