How it Works

The Time Zone Converter uses a simple formula to calculate the time difference between two time zones. The formula takes into account the offset of each time zone from Coordinated Universal Time (UTC) and calculates the converted time based on this offset. For example, if you want to convert a time from UTC-5 to UTC+2, the converter will add 7 hours to the original time to get the converted time.
