How it Works

The binary to decimal conversion is based on the principle that each digit in a binary number represents a power of 2. The formula to convert binary to decimal is: decimal = (binary digit * 2^(position - 1)) + ... + (binary digit * 2^0), where position starts from the right at 1 and increases as you move left.
