How it Works

The CST to JST Time Zone Converter uses a simple formula to calculate the time difference between Central Standard Time (CST) and Japan Standard Time (JST). The formula is based on the fact that JST is 15 hours ahead of CST. To convert CST to JST, you simply add 15 hours to the CST time. For example, if it's 10:00 AM CST, the equivalent time in JST would be 1:00 AM (next day).
