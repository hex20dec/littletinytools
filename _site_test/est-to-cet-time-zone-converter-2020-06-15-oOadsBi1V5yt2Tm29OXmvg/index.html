How Our EST to CET Converter Works

Our EST to CET converter uses a simple formula to calculate the time difference between these two time zones. The formula is based on the fact that CET is 6 hours ahead of EST. To convert time from EST to CET, you simply need to add 6 hours to the EST time. For example, if it's 12:00 PM (noon) EST, the equivalent time in CET would be 6:00 PM.
