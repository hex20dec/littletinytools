How it Works

The binary to decimal conversion is based on the positional notation of the binary number system, where each digit represents a power of 2. The conversion formula is: Decimal Equivalent = (Binary Number)2, where the subscript 2 denotes the base-2 number system.

The IST to EST conversion is based on the time difference between the two time zones. IST is 10 hours and 30 minutes ahead of EST. The conversion formula is: EST Time = IST Time - 10 hours 30 minutes.
