How it Works

The binary to decimal converter uses a simple formula to calculate the decimal equivalent of a binary number: Decimal = (2^n * d_n) + (2^(n-1) * d_(n-1)) + ... + (2^0 * d_0), where d_i is the ith digit of the binary number and n is the number of digits.
