How Does the Alphabetical Word Counter Work?

The tool works by analyzing the first letter of every word in your text. Here’s how it breaks down:

  1. It scans your text and splits it into individual words.
  2. It checks the first letter of each word and assigns it to the corresponding letter in the alphabet (A-Z).
  3. It counts how many words start with each letter and displays the results in a neat table.

For example, if your text has 5 words starting with “A” and 3 starting with “B,” the table will show those counts instantly. It’s that simple!
