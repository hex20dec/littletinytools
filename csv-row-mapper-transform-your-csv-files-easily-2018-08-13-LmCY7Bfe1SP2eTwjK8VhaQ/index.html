How It Works:

The CSV Row Mapper follows a simple yet powerful formula to make your data work for you:

  1. Upload Your CSV: Drag and drop your file or select it from your device.
  2. Preview Your Data: Instantly see your CSV in a clean, readable table format.
  3. Map and Transform: Choose specific columns or rows, rename headers, reorder data, or apply filters.
