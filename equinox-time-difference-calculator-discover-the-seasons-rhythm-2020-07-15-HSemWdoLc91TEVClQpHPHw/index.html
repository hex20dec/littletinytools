What are Equinoxes?

Equinoxes occur when the sun crosses the celestial equator, marking the beginning of spring and autumn. The spring equinox typically falls on March 20 or 21, while the autumn equinox falls on September 22 or 23.

How does the calculation work?

The calculation works by finding the difference in time between the two input dates. The result is displayed in days, hours, minutes, and seconds.

How the Calculator Works
