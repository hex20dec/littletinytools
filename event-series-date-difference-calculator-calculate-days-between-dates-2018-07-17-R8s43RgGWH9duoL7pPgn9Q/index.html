How It Works

Here's the simple formula behind the magic: the tool subtracts the start date from the end date to find the total number of days in between. Think of it as counting the days on a calendar, but without the hassle. Just pick your start and end dates, hit "Calculate," and voilà—your answer appears in seconds!
