How Does It Work?

The Line Word Counter works behind the scenes to make your life simpler. Here’s the magic formula (without the techy jargon):

  1. Words: The tool splits your text into individual words by looking for spaces. Each chunk of text between spaces is counted as a word—easy, right?
  2. Lines: It checks how many times you’ve pressed “Enter” or started a new line. Each new line gets counted, so you know exactly how many lines you’ve written.

No complicated math, no extra steps. Just type or paste, and the numbers pop up like magic!
