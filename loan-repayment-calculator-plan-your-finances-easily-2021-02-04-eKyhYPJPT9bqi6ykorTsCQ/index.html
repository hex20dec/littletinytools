How It Works:

Our Loan Repayment Calculator uses a simple formula to determine your monthly payment and total interest. Here’s the breakdown:

  • Loan Amount: The total amount you’re borrowing.
  • Interest Rate: The annual interest rate on your loan.
  • Loan Term: The number of years you’ll take to repay the loan.

The calculator converts the annual interest rate into a monthly rate, calculates the total number of payments, and uses these to determine your monthly payment. It then multiplies the monthly payment by the total number of payments to find the total amount repaid, subtracting the loan amount to reveal the total interest paid.
