How the Factorial Calculator Works

The factorial of a number is calculated using a simple formula: n! = n * (n-1) * (n-2) *... * 2 * 1. For example, the factorial of 5 is calculated as 5! = 5 * 4 * 3 * 2 * 1 = 120.
