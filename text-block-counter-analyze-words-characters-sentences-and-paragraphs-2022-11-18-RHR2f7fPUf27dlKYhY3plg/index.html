How It Works

The Text Block Counter uses a straightforward approach to analyze your text. It breaks down your input into smaller components to give you accurate counts:

  • Words: Counts every group of letters separated by spaces.
  • Characters (with spaces): Includes every single character, even spaces.
  • Characters (without spaces): Excludes spaces, counting only letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Sentences: Detects sentences based on punctuation marks like periods, exclamation points, and question marks.
  • Paragraphs: Identifies paragraphs by line breaks or empty lines.
