How It Works

Here’s the simple formula behind the magic: the tool subtracts one temperature from another and gives you the absolute difference. That means it doesn’t matter which temperature is higher; you’ll always get a positive number. For example, if you enter 20°C and 15°C, the difference is 5°C. Easy, right?
