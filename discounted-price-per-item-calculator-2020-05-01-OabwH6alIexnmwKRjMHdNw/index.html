Discounted Price Per Item Calculator

The Discounted Price Per Item Calculator is a free online tool designed to help individuals and businesses calculate the discounted price per item after applying a percentage discount to the original price. This calculator is particularly useful for shoppers, retailers, and wholesalers who need to determine the discounted price of items after applying discounts, promotions, or sales. By using this calculator, users can easily calculate the discounted price per item, total cost before discount, and total cost after discount, making it an essential tool for anyone looking to save money or make informed purchasing decisions.

How it Works

The calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the discounted price per item: Discounted Price Per Item = (Original Price Per Item * Number of Items - (Original Price Per Item * Number of Items * Discount Percentage / 100)) / Number of Items.
