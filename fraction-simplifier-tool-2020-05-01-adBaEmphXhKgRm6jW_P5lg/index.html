How it Works

The Fraction Simplifier Tool uses a simple yet effective formula to reduce fractions to their simplest form. The formula involves finding the greatest common divisor (GCD) of the numerator and denominator, and then dividing both numbers by the GCD to obtain the simplified fraction.
