How It Works

Converting EST to MST is as easy as pie! Here’s the simple formula behind the magic:

MST Time = EST Time - 2 Hours

For example, if it’s 8:00 AM EST, simply subtract 2 hours to get 6:00 AM MST. The tool automatically adjusts for AM/PM, so you don’t have to worry about flipping between morning and evening!
