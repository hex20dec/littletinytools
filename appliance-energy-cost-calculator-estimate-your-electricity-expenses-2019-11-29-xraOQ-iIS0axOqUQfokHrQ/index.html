How It Works

The Appliance Energy Cost Calculator uses a straightforward formula to estimate your energy expenses:

Daily Cost = (Power in Watts ÷ 1000) × Daily Usage in Hours × Electricity Cost per kWh

For example, if your appliance uses 1000 Watts, runs for 4 hours a day, and your electricity costs $0.12 per kWh, the daily cost would be:

(1000 ÷ 1000) × 4 × 0.12 = $0.48

Multiply this by 30 for monthly costs or 365 for yearly costs, and you’ll have a clear picture of your energy spending.
