How It Works

The Custom Word Length Counter works by analyzing your input text and counting how many words match the exact length you specify. Here’s the simple formula:

  1. Input your text: Paste or type your text into the provided box.
  2. Specify the word length: Enter the number of letters you want to count (e.g., 5 for 5-letter words).
  3. Click "Count Words": The tool splits your text into words, filters out those matching the specified length, and displays the count instantly.

It’s like having a super-smart assistant who can instantly tell you how many 7-letter words are in your novel or how many 3-letter words are in your poem. No fuss, no hassle—just results!
