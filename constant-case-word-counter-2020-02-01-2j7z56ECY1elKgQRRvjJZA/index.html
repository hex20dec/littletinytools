How Our Constant Case Word Counter Works

The tool operates on a simple yet effective principle: it splits the input text into individual words based on spaces and then counts them. The formula behind this process can be explained as follows: Word Count = Number of Spaces + 1, assuming that words are separated by a single space. This straightforward approach allows for quick and accurate word counting.
