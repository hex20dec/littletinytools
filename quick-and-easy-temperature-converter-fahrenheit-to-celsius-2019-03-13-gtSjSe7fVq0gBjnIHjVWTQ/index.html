How It Works

The Fahrenheit to Celsius Converter uses a straightforward formula to switch between the two temperature scales. Here's the breakdown:

  • Fahrenheit to Celsius: Subtract 32 from the Fahrenheit value, then multiply by 5/9.
  • Celsius to Fahrenheit: Multiply the Celsius value by 9/5, then add 32.

For example, if you want to convert 68°F to Celsius: (68 - 32) x 5/9 = 20°C. Easy, right?
