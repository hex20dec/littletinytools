    How It Works

    The Text Segment Counter uses a simple yet powerful approach to break down your text:

    1. Input Your Text: Paste or type your text into the provided box.
    2. Choose a Delimiter: Select a standard delimiter (like a period or comma) or specify your own custom character.
    3. Count Segments: Click the button, and the tool will instantly split your text into segments based on the chosen delimiter.
    4. View Results: See the total number of segments and optionally list them out for further analysis.

    For example, if you use a period as the delimiter and input "Hello. How are you? Goodbye!", the tool will count 3 segments.
