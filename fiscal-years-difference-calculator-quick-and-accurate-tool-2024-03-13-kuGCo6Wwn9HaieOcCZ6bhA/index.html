How It Works

The Fiscal Years Difference Calculator is as simple as it gets. All you need to do is input two fiscal years in the format YYYY-YYYY (like 2020-2021 or 2015-2016). The calculator then takes the starting year of each fiscal period and calculates the difference between them. For example, if you enter "2020-2021" as the start and "2025-2026" as the end, the tool will tell you the difference is 5 fiscal years. It's like having a math whiz in your pocket!
