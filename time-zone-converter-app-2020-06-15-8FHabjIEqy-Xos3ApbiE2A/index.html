How it Works

The Time Zone Converter App uses a simple formula to convert dates and times between different time zones. The formula takes into account the offset of the source time zone and the destination time zone, and then applies this offset to the original date and time to produce the converted result. This process can be broken down into the following steps:

  • Identify the source time zone and its offset from Coordinated Universal Time (UTC)
  • Identify the destination time zone and its offset from Coordinated Universal Time (UTC)
  • Calculate the difference in offset between the source and destination time zones
  • Apply the offset difference to the original date and time to produce the converted result
