How the Circle Radius Calculator Works

The Circle Radius Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the radius of a circle based on the input parameter. The formula is as follows:

  • For circumference: radius = circumference / (2 * π)
  • For diameter: radius = diameter / 2
  • For area: radius = √(area / π)
