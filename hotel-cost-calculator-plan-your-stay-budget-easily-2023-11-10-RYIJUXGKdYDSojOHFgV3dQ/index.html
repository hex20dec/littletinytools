How It Works

The Hotel Cost Per Night Calculator uses a simple formula to determine your total hotel expenses. Here's how it breaks down:

Total Cost = Nightly Rate × Number of Nights

For example, if your hotel costs $150 per night and you're staying for 5 nights, the total cost would be $750. It’s that straightforward!
