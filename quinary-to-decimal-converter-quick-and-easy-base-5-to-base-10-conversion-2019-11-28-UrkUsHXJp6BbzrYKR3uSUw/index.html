How It Works

The Quinary to Decimal Converter works by breaking down the quinary number into its individual digits and then calculating their decimal value based on their position. Each digit is multiplied by 5 raised to the power of its position index (starting from 0 on the right). The results are then summed up to give you the final decimal number.
