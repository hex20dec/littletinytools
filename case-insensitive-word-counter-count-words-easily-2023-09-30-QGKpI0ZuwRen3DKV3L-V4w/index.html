Case-Insensitive Word Counter

Ever found yourself squinting at a long block of text, trying to count how many times a specific word appears? Maybe you’re editing an essay, analyzing a document, or just curious. But let’s face it—counting words manually is tedious, and who has time for that? Enter the Case-Insensitive Word Counter, your new best friend for effortless word tracking! This nifty tool does all the heavy lifting for you, scanning your text and tallying up word occurrences—no matter if they’re in uppercase, lowercase, or a mix of both. Whether you’re a writer, student, or just someone who loves efficiency, this tool is here to save the day. Say goodbye to frustration and hello to quick, accurate results!

Enter your text and the word you want to count. The tool will count all occurrences, ignoring case.

How It Works

This tool is as simple as it gets. Here’s the magic formula:

  1. Paste or type your text into the text box.
  2. Enter the word you want to count in the second box.
  3. Click "Count" and let the tool do its thing!

The tool scans your entire text, ignoring uppercase or lowercase differences, and counts how many times your chosen word appears. It’s like having a personal word detective at your fingertips!
