Tip Calculator

Stop Guessing Your Tips—Get It Right Every Time

Let’s face it: calculating tips can be awkward. Whether you’re splitting the bill with friends or just trying to figure out how much to leave after a great meal, the math shouldn’t be the hard part. That’s where this handy tool comes in. Just plug in your bill amount, choose your tip percentage, and let the calculator do the rest. It’s fast, accurate, and takes the guesswork out of tipping. Now you can focus on enjoying your meal, not stressing over the math!

Calculate your tip and total bill quickly and easily.

How It Works

This tool uses a simple formula to calculate your tip and total bill. Here’s the breakdown:

Tip Amount = Bill Amount × (Tip Percentage ÷ 100)

Total Bill = Bill Amount + Tip Amount

If you’re splitting the bill, the tool also calculates how much each person owes by dividing the total bill by the number of people. It’s math made easy!
