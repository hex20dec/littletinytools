How Does It Work?

Here’s the magic behind the tool: Each Roman numeral symbol represents a specific value. For example, I is 1, V is 5, and X is 10. The tool reads your input, matches each symbol to its value, and adds them up. If a smaller symbol appears before a larger one (like IV), it subtracts instead of adds.
