How It Works

The Time Elapsed Calculator is as straightforward as it gets. Here's the magic behind it:

  • 1. Enter your start time – the moment when the clock starts ticking.
  • 2. Enter your end time – the moment when the clock stops.
  • 3. Click Calculate, and voilà! The tool does the math for you and displays the elapsed time in hours, minutes, and seconds.
