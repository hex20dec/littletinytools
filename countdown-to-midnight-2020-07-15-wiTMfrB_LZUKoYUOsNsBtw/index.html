How it Works

The Countdown to Midnight tool uses a simple formula to calculate the number of seconds remaining until midnight. The formula is as follows: (midnight time - current time) / 1000 = seconds remaining. This formula takes into account the current time and the time of midnight, and it calculates the difference in seconds.
