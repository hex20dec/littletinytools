How it Works

The Temperature Absolute Difference Calculator uses a straightforward formula to calculate the absolute difference between two temperatures. The formula is: Absolute Difference = |Temperature 1 - Temperature 2|, where | | denotes the absolute value. This means that the calculator subtracts the second temperature from the first and then takes the absolute value of the result, ensuring that the difference is always a positive number.
