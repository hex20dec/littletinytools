How the Remainder Calculator Works

The Remainder Calculator operates based on a simple formula: Dividend = Divisor * Quotient + Remainder. This formula is applied when the user inputs the dividend and divisor, and the calculator computes the quotient and remainder using the following steps:

  • Divide the dividend by the divisor to obtain the quotient.
  • Multiply the quotient by the divisor to find the product.
  • Subtract the product from the dividend to determine the remainder.
