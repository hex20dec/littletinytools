How It Works

The Personal Debt Payoff Calculator uses a simple formula to determine how long it will take to pay off your debt and how much interest you'll pay over time. Here's the breakdown:

  • Step 1: Enter your total debt amount, monthly payment, and annual interest rate.
  • Step 2: The calculator divides your annual interest rate by 12 to find the monthly interest rate.
  • Step 3: It then calculates how much of your monthly payment goes toward interest and how much reduces your principal debt.
  • Step 4: This process repeats each month until your debt is fully paid off, giving you a clear timeline and total interest paid.
