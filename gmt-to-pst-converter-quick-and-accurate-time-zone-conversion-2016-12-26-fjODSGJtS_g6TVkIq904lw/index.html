Note: This tool assumes standard time (no daylight saving adjustments).

Here's how the magic happens: The tool takes your input in GMT (Greenwich Mean Time) and subtracts 8 hours to convert it to PST (Pacific Standard Time). It’s like sliding a clock backward to match the West Coast vibe. For example, if it’s 10:00 AM GMT, the tool will show 2:00 AM PST. Easy, right?
