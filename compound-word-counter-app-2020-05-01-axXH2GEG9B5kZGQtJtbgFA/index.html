    How it Works: A Formula for Success

    The Compound Word Counter App employs a straightforward yet effective formula to identify and count compound words. The formula can be broken down into the following steps:

    1. Text Input: The user inputs a text into the app's interface.
    2. Text Analysis: The app analyzes the text to identify individual words.
    3. Compound Word Identification: The app checks each word to see if it contains a space or a hyphen, which are common indicators of compound words.
