How It Works

The tool works by taking a 24-hour time input (like 14:30) and converting it into a 12-hour format with AM or PM. Here's the breakdown:

  1. If the hour is between 00 and 11, it stays the same (or becomes 12 for midnight) and is labeled as AM.
  2. If the hour is between 12 and 23, it’s converted to 1–12 and labeled as PM.
  3. The minutes stay the same, and the result is displayed in a clean, easy-to-read format.
