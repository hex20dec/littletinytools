How It Works

The Percentage Difference Calculator uses a straightforward formula to determine how much two numbers differ in percentage terms. Here's the breakdown:

  1. Find the average of the two numbers by adding them together and dividing by 2.
  2. Calculate the absolute difference between the two numbers (ignore whether it's positive or negative).
  3. Divide the difference by the average, then multiply by 100 to get the percentage difference.

For example, comparing 50 and 60:

  • Average = (50 + 60) / 2 = 55
  • Difference = |50 - 60| = 10
  • Percentage Difference = (10 / 55) * 100 ≈ 18.18%
