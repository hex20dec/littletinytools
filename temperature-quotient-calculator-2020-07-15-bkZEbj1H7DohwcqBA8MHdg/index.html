How it Works

The Temperature Quotient Calculator uses a simple formula to convert temperatures from Fahrenheit to Celsius: Quotient (°C) = (Temperature (°F) - 32) * 5/9. This formula is based on the definition of the Celsius scale, where 0°C is the freezing point of water and 100°C is the boiling point.
