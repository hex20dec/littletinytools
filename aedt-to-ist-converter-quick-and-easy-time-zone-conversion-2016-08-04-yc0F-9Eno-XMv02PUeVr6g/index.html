How It Works

The AEDT to IST Converter works by calculating the 5.5-hour time difference between AEDT and IST. Here's the simple formula behind it:

  • Step 1: Subtract 5 hours from the AEDT time.
  • Step 2: Subtract 30 minutes from the result.
  • Step 3: If the minutes go below 0, adjust the hours accordingly.
  • Step 4: If the hours go below 0, wrap around to the previous day.

For example, if it's 10:00 AM AEDT, subtracting 5 hours and 30 minutes gives you 4:30 AM IST. Easy, right?
