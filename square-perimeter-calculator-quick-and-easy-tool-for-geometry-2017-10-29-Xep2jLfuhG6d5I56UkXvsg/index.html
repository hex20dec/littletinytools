How It Works

Calculating the perimeter of a square is as easy as pie—literally! The formula is straightforward:

Perimeter = 4 × Side Length

For example, if one side of your square is 5 units long, the perimeter would be 4 × 5 = 20 units. That’s it! No complex math, no confusion—just quick and accurate results.
