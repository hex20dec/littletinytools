How it Works: The Binary to Decimal Conversion Formula

The binary to decimal conversion process is based on a simple yet powerful formula: decimal = binary * (2 ^ position), where position starts from 0 and increases from right to left. This formula allows you to convert binary numbers to decimal equivalents with ease and accuracy.
