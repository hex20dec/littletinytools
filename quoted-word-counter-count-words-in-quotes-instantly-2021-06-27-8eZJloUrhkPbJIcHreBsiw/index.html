How Does the Quoted Word Counter Work?

It’s simpler than you think! Here’s the magic formula:

  1. The tool scans your text for anything wrapped in double quotes ("like this").
  2. It isolates each quoted section and counts the words inside, ignoring spaces and punctuation.
  3. Finally, it highlights the quotes for easy reference and gives you the total word count.

For example, if you paste: "Hello world" is a common phrase., the tool will highlight "Hello world" and tell you it contains 2 words.
