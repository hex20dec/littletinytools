How It Works

The Date Difference in Dog Years Calculator uses a straightforward formula to convert human years into dog years. Here’s the breakdown:

  1. First, it calculates the difference between your two chosen dates in days.
  2. Next, it converts those days into human years by dividing the total days by 365 (the number of days in a year).
  3. Finally, it multiplies the human years by 7 to get the equivalent in dog years. (Because, as we all know, 1 human year = 7 dog years!)

It’s like magic, but with math. And no, your dog won’t judge you for using a calculator instead of doing it in your head.
