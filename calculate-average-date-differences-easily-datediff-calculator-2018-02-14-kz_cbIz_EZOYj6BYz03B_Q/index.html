    How It Works

    The Average Date Difference Calculator works by taking the list of dates you provide and calculating the time difference (in days) between each consecutive pair. It then adds up all these differences and divides the total by the number of intervals to give you the average. Think of it like measuring the gaps between fence posts and finding the average spacing—it’s that simple!

    In this example, the total difference is 24 days, and the average difference is 12 days (24 ÷ 2 intervals). The tool does all this math for you in a flash!
