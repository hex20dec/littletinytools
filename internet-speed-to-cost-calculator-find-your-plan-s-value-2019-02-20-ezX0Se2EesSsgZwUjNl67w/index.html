How It Works

The Internet Speed to Cost Calculator uses a simple formula to determine the cost per Mbps of your internet plan. Here's how it breaks down:

Cost per Mbps = Monthly Cost ÷ Internet Speed (Mbps)

For example, if you pay $50 for a 100 Mbps plan, your cost per Mbps is $0.50. Below is a table showing how this formula applies to different scenarios:
