How It Works

The Milestone Duration Calculator uses a simple formula to determine the time between two dates. Here's how it breaks down:

  1. It calculates the total number of milliseconds between the two dates.
  2. Converts milliseconds into days by dividing by 86,400,000 (the number of milliseconds in a day).
  3. Breaks down the total days into weeks, months, and years for a more comprehensive view.
