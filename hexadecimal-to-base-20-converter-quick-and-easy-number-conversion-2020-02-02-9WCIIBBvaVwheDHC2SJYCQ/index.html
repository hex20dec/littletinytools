How It Works

Our tool works in two simple steps: first, it converts your hexadecimal input (like 1A3F) into a decimal number. Then, it takes that decimal number and transforms it into base-20. Here’s the magic formula in plain English:

  1. Take your hexadecimal input (e.g., 1A3F).
  2. Convert it to its decimal equivalent by treating each character as a power of 16.
  3. Divide the decimal number by 20 repeatedly, keeping track of the remainders.
  4. Map the remainders to base-20 digits (0-9, A-J).
  5. Voilà! You’ve got your base-20 result.
