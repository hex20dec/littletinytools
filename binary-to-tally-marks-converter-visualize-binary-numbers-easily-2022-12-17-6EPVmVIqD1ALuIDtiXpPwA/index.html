How It Works

The Binary to Tally Marks Converter works by taking your binary input (a sequence of 0s and 1s) and converting each digit into a tally mark. Here's the breakdown:

  • Every 1 in your binary number is represented by a vertical tally mark (|).
  • Every 0 is represented by a space, creating a clear separation between the marks.

For example, if you enter 1010, the tool will display | |, showing two tally marks with spaces in between. It's like turning binary into a visual story!
