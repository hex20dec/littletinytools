How It Works

The CSV Field Length Padder follows a simple formula to ensure your data is perfectly formatted:

  1. Upload Your CSV: Select the file you want to process.
  2. Set Field Lengths: Specify the desired length for each column and choose a padding character if needed.
  3. Choose Padding Direction: Decide whether to pad fields on the left or right.
  4. Trim Excess (Optional): Automatically trim any extra characters if your fields exceed the desired length.
  5. Preview & Download: Review the padded data and download the updated CSV file.
