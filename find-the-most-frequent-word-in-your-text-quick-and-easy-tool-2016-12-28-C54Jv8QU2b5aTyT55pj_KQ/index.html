Here’s how the Most Frequent Word Finder works:

  1. You paste your text into the box.
  2. The tool cleans up the text by removing punctuation and converting everything to lowercase for fairness.
  3. It splits the text into individual words and counts how often each one appears.
  4. Finally, it highlights the word(s) with the highest count, so you know exactly what’s dominating your writing.

It’s that simple! No complicated formulas or confusing steps—just quick, actionable insights to help you refine your writing.
