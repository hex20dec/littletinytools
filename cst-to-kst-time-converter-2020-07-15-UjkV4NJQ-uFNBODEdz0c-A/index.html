How the CST to KST Converter Works

The CST to KST converter uses a simple formula to calculate the time difference between the two time zones. Since KST is 14 hours ahead of CST, the converter adds 14 hours to the given CST time to determine the equivalent time in KST. This can be represented by the formula: KST Time = CST Time + 14 hours.
