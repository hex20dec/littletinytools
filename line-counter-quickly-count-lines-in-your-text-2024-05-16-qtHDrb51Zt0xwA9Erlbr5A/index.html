Line Counter

Juggling long blocks of text and need to know how many lines you’ve written? Whether you're drafting a poem, coding, or preparing a document, keeping track of lines can be a hassle. That’s where the Line Counter comes in—a simple, no-fuss tool that instantly tells you how many lines your text has. Just paste or type your content, hit the button, and voilà! No more guessing or manual counting. It’s like having a tiny assistant who’s always ready to help you stay organized. Say goodbye to line-counting stress and hello to smoother workflows!

How It Works

The Line Counter works by breaking your text into individual lines using the "newline" character (that invisible marker your keyboard creates when you press Enter). It then counts each line and gives you the total. Think of it as slicing a loaf of bread—each slice is a line, and the counter tells you how many slices you have. Easy, right?
