How It Works

Converting time doesn’t have to be rocket science! Here’s the simple formula our tool uses:

For AM Times: If the hour is 12 AM, it becomes 00 in 24-hour format. For all other AM times, the hour stays the same.

For PM Times: If the hour is 12 PM, it remains 12 in 24-hour format. For all other PM times, simply add 12 to the hour.

For example, 3:30 PM becomes 15:30, and 12:45 AM becomes 00:45. Easy, right? No math, no stress—just quick and accurate conversions every time!
