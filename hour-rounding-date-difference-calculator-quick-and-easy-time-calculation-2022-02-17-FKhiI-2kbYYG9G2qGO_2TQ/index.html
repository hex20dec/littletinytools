How It Works

The Hour Rounding Date Difference Calculator works by taking two date-time inputs—your start and end times—and calculating the total hours between them. Here’s the magic formula it uses:

Time Difference = (End Date - Start Date) in hours

If you choose to round to the nearest hour, the tool adjusts the result to the closest whole number. For example, if the difference is 3.7 hours, it rounds to 4 hours. If it’s 3.3 hours, it rounds down to 3 hours. Simple, right?
