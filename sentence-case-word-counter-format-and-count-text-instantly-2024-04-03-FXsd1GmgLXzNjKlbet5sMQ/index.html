How It Works

The Sentence Case Word Counter works its magic in three simple steps:

  1. Input Your Text: Paste or type your text into the input box.
  2. Convert & Count: Click the "Convert & Count" button to instantly transform your text into sentence case and get the word and sentence count.
  3. Reset: If you want to start fresh, hit the "Reset" button to clear everything.

The tool automatically capitalizes the first letter of each sentence and ensures the rest of the text remains lowercase. It also calculates the number of words and sentences, so you don’t have to count manually.
