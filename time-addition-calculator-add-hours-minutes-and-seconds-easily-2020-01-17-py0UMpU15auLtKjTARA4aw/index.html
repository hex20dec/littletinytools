How It Works

The Time Addition Calculator works by breaking down time into its core components: hours, minutes, and seconds. Here’s the magic formula:

  • Step 1: Add up all the hours from your inputs.
  • Step 2: Add up all the minutes and convert any overflow (60+ minutes) into additional hours.
  • Step 3: Add up all the seconds and convert any overflow (60+ seconds) into additional minutes.
  • Step 4: Combine the adjusted hours, minutes, and seconds into a clean, readable format (HH:MM:SS).
