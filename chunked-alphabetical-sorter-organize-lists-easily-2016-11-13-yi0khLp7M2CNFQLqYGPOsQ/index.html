How It Works

The Chunked Alphabetical Sorter uses a simple formula to organize your list:

  1. Input your items: Enter your list, with each item on a new line (e.g., Apple, Banana, Cherry).
  2. Specify the chunk size: Choose how many items you want in each group (e.g., 2 items per chunk).
  3. Sort and chunk: The tool alphabetically sorts your list and divides it into the specified groups.
