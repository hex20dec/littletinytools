How It Works

Converting time from EST to CST is easier than you think! Here’s the simple formula behind the tool:

CST Time = EST Time - 1 Hour

That’s it! The tool takes the time you enter in EST, subtracts one hour, and adjusts for AM/PM automatically. No need to worry about time zone math—just input your time and let the converter do the heavy lifting.
