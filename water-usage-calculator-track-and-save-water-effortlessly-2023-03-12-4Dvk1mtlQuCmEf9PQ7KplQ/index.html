How It Works

The Water Usage Calculator uses a straightforward formula to estimate your water consumption. Here’s the breakdown:

Daily Water Usage = (Shower Usage + Toilet Usage + Dishwashing Usage + Laundry Usage + Other Usage)

  • Shower Usage: Number of showers × shower duration × 10 liters per minute.
  • Toilet Usage: Number of flushes × liters per flush (based on toilet type).
  • Dishwashing Usage: Number of sessions × liters per session (handwashing or dishwasher).
  • Laundry Usage: Number of loads per week ÷ 7 days × liters per load.
  • Other Usage: Any additional water usage you input manually.

Once you input your daily habits, the calculator does the math for you, giving you a clear picture of your water consumption over time.
