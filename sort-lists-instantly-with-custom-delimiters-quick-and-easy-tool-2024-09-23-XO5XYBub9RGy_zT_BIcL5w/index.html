Sort by Custom Delimiter

Why is organizing your lists always such a headache? Whether it's a jumbled list of groceries, a messy to-do list, or a chaotic inventory, sorting manually feels like solving a puzzle blindfolded. Enter the Sort by Custom Delimiter tool—your new best friend for turning chaos into order. Simply paste your list, specify the delimiter (like a comma, space, or semicolon), and let the tool do the rest. Whether you're alphabetizing your favorite movies or arranging numbers in ascending order, this tool makes sorting effortless. Say goodbye to tedious manual work and hello to a neatly organized life!

Enter your list of items separated by a custom delimiter, choose sorting options, and get your sorted list instantly.

How It Works

This tool follows a simple yet powerful process:

  1. Input Your List: Paste your list of items into the text box. For example: apple, banana, cherry.
  2. Choose Your Delimiter: Specify the character that separates your items, like a comma (,), semicolon (;), or even a space.
  3. Pick a Sorting Order: Decide if you want your list sorted in ascending (A-Z or 0-9) or descending (Z-A or 9-0) order.
  4. Get Your Sorted List: Click the "Sort List" button, and voilà—your neatly organized list appears instantly!
