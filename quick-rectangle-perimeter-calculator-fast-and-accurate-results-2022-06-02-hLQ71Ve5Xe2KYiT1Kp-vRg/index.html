How It Works

The perimeter of a rectangle is calculated using a straightforward formula: Perimeter = 2 × (Length + Width). Think of it as adding up all the sides of the rectangle and then doubling the result. For example, if your rectangle has a length of 5 units and a width of 3 units, the perimeter would be 2 × (5 + 3) = 16 units. It’s that simple!
