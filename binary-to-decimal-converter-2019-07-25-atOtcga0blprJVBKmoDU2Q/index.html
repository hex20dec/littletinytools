How it Works: Binary to Decimal Conversion Formula

The binary to decimal conversion formula is based on the positional notation of the binary number. The formula is as follows:

Decimal Value = (bn × 2n) + (bn-1 × 2n-1) + ... + (b0 × 20)

Where bn is the nth bit of the binary number.
