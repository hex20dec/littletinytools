How the Binary to Decimal Converter Works

The Binary to Decimal Converter uses a simple formula to convert binary numbers to decimal numbers. The formula is as follows:

Decimal Number = (Binary Number)2

This formula works by parsing the binary number from right to left, with each digit representing a power of 2. The decimal equivalent is then calculated by summing the powers of 2 for each digit that is a 1.
