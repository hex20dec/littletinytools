How It Works

The Fence Length Calculator uses a simple formula to determine the total length of fencing required for your area. Here’s the magic behind it:

Total Fence Length = 2 × (Length + Width)

This formula calculates the perimeter of your space, ensuring you know exactly how much fencing material you’ll need. Whether you’re working in meters or feet, the tool adapts to your preference. Below is a quick reference table to give you an idea of how the calculations work:
