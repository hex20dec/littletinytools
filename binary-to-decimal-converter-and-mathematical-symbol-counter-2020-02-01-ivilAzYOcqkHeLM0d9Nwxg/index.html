How it Works

The binary to decimal converter uses the following formula to convert binary numbers to decimal equivalents:

Decimal Number = (Binary Digit 1 * 2^n) + (Binary Digit 2 * 2^(n-1)) + ... + (Binary Digit n * 2^0)
