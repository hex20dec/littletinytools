How It Works

The Custom Date Range Difference Calculator uses a simple formula to determine the difference between two dates. Here’s the breakdown:

  • Days: The total number of days between the two dates is calculated by subtracting the start date from the end date.
  • Weeks: The total number of weeks is derived by dividing the total days by 7.
  • Months: The difference in months is calculated by comparing the year and month values of the two dates.
  • Years: The difference in years is simply the number of full years between the two dates.
