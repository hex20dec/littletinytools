How It Works

The Average Speed Calculator uses a straightforward formula: Average Speed = Total Distance ÷ Total Time. For example, if you drove 100 kilometers in 2 hours, your average speed would be 50 km/h. It’s that simple! The tool automatically handles the math, so you don’t have to worry about errors or miscalculations.
