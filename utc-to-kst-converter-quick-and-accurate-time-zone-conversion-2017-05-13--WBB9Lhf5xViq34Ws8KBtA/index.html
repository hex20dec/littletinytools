How It Works

The tool works by adding 9 hours to the UTC time you provide. Why 9 hours? Because Korean Standard Time (KST) is 9 hours ahead of Coordinated Universal Time (UTC). It’s like adding a time travel boost to your UTC input! For example, if it’s 12:00 PM UTC, the tool will calculate it as 9:00 PM KST. Easy, right?
