How the Calculator Works

The calculator uses a simple formula to calculate productivity and total earnings: Productivity = Hourly Wage x (Total Minutes Spent / 60). This formula takes into account the user's hourly wage, time spent on tasks, and task type to provide an accurate calculation of productivity and total earnings.
