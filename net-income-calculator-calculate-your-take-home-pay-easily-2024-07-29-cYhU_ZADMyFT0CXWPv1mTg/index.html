How It Works

The Net Income Calculator follows a simple formula to determine your take-home pay:

Net Income = Gross Income - (Gross Income × Tax Rate) - Deductions

Here’s a quick breakdown: Start with your gross income (your total earnings before taxes). Then, subtract the taxes you owe based on your tax rate. Finally, subtract any additional deductions like retirement contributions or insurance premiums. What’s left is your net income—the amount you actually take home.
