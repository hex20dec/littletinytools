How It Works

This tool uses a straightforward formula tailored to each language and script:

  • Latin & Custom Scripts: Counts every single character, including spaces and punctuation.
  • Chinese, Japanese, & Korean: Counts each individual ideogram or character, even if they look like multiple symbols.
  • Arabic, Cyrillic, Devanagari, & Greek: Counts characters while ignoring spaces, perfect for languages with complex scripts.
