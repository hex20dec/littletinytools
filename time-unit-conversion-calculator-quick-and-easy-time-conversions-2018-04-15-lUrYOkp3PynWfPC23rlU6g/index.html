How It Works

This tool uses a simple formula to convert time units. Every time unit is first converted to seconds, which acts as a common base. For example:

  • 1 minute = 60 seconds
  • 1 hour = 3,600 seconds
  • 1 day = 86,400 seconds

Once the input value is converted to seconds, it’s then transformed into the desired output unit. Here’s a quick example: If you want to convert 2 hours into minutes, the tool first calculates 2 hours in seconds (2 x 3,600 = 7,200 seconds) and then converts those seconds into minutes (7,200 ÷ 60 = 120 minutes).
