How It Works

The PST to KST Converter works by adding 17 hours to the Pacific Standard Time (PST) you input. Since Korean Standard Time (KST) is 17 hours ahead of PST, this simple calculation ensures you get the correct time in Korea. For example, if it’s 10:00 AM in PST, adding 17 hours gives you 3:00 AM the next day in KST. The tool also handles edge cases, like when the calculation goes past midnight, so you don’t have to worry about the math!
