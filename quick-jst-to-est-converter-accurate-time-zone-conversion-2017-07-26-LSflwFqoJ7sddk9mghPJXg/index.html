How It Works

The JST to EST Converter works by subtracting 13 hours from the given Japan Standard Time (JST). Here’s the simple breakdown:

EST Time = JST Time - 13 Hours

For example, if it’s 14:30 (2:30 PM) in JST, subtracting 13 hours gives you 1:30 AM in EST. Easy, right? The tool also automatically adjusts for AM/PM formatting, so you don’t have to worry about a thing.
