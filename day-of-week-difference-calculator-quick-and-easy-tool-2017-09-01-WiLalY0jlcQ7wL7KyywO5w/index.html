How Does It Work?

The magic behind this tool is simple. It calculates the difference between two days of the week by assigning each day a number (Monday = 0, Tuesday = 1, and so on). Then, it subtracts the first day's number from the second day's number, adjusts for the week's cycle, and gives you the exact number of days between them. Think of it like counting steps between two points on a circle—no matter where you start, the tool ensures you land on the right number of days.
