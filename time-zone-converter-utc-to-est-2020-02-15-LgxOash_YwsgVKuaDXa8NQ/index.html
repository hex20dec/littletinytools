How it Works

The Time Zone Converter uses a simple formula to convert UTC time to EST time. The formula takes into account the 5-hour difference between the two time zones and adjusts the time accordingly. Here's a breakdown of the process:

1. Input the UTC time in the format HH:MM.

2. The tool subtracts 5 hours from the input time to account for the time difference between UTC and EST.

3. If the resulting time is less than 0, the tool adds 12 to the hours and changes the AM/PM designation.

4. The tool displays the converted EST time in the format HH:MM AM/PM.
