How It Works

The Zero-to-Front Sorter works like a digital organizer for your numbers. Here’s the simple formula it follows:

  • Step 1: Separate all the zeros from the rest of the numbers.
  • Step 2: Group the non-zero numbers together, keeping their original order.
  • Step 3: Place all the zeros at the front, followed by the non-zero numbers.

For example, if you input 1, 0, 2, 0, 3, the tool will rearrange it into 0, 0, 1, 2, 3. Easy, right?
