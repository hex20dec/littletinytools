How It Works

The tool calculates the total earnings for each wage over your chosen time period. Here's the simple formula it uses:

Total Earnings = Hourly Wage × Hours Worked in the Time Period

Depending on whether you choose daily, weekly, monthly, or annual calculations, the tool adjusts the hours worked accordingly. For example:

  • Daily: 8 hours (standard workday)
  • Weekly: 40 hours (8 hours × 5 days)
  • Monthly: 160 hours (40 hours × 4 weeks)
  • Annually: 1,920 hours (160 hours × 12 months)

Once it calculates the totals, it shows the difference between the two wages, so you can easily see which one pays more—and by how much.
