GMT Offset to Time Zone Converter

The GMT Offset to Time Zone Converter is a user-friendly online tool designed to help individuals convert Greenwich Mean Time (GMT) offsets to their corresponding time zones. This tool is particularly useful for travelers, business professionals, and anyone who needs to coordinate activities across different time zones. By entering the GMT offset, users can determine the equivalent time zone and view a list of corresponding cities, making it easier to manage global communications and schedules.

    How it Works

    The GMT Offset to Time Zone Converter uses a simple formula to calculate the time zone based on the GMT offset. The formula is as follows: Time Zone = UTC + GMT Offset, where UTC is the Coordinated Universal Time and GMT Offset is the number of hours ahead of or behind UTC.
