How Does the Salary Raise Percentage Calculator Work?

The tool calculates the percentage change between your current salary and your new salary using a straightforward formula. Here’s how it breaks down:

Step 1: Subtract your current salary from your new salary to find the difference.
Step 2: Divide that difference by your current salary.
Step 3: Multiply the result by 100 to get the percentage change.

For example, if your current salary is $50,000 and your new salary is $60,000, the calculation would look like this:
(($60,000 - $50,000) / $50,000) * 100 = 20%. That’s a 20% raise!

The tool also handles decreases in salary, so you’ll always know exactly where you stand.
