Temperature Range Calculator

The Temperature Range Calculator is a simple online tool designed to help users calculate the temperature range and average temperature between two given temperatures in Celsius. This tool is perfect for scientists, researchers, and students who need to work with temperature data. With its user-friendly interface and accurate calculations, this tool is an essential resource for anyone looking to simplify their temperature-related calculations.

How it Works

The Temperature Range Calculator uses a simple formula to calculate the temperature range and average temperature: Temperature Range = Upper Temperature - Lower Temperature, and Average Temperature = (Upper Temperature + Lower Temperature) / 2.
