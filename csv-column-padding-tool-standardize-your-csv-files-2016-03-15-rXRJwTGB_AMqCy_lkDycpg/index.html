How It Works

The tool follows a simple process to ensure your CSV columns are perfectly padded:

  1. Upload your CSV file—it’s quick and easy.
  2. Choose a padding character: a space, a zero, or even a custom character of your choice.
  3. Set the desired padding length (e.g., 10 characters per column).
  4. Let the tool work its magic! It automatically adjusts each column to the specified width, ensuring consistency across the entire file.
  5. Preview the results and download your perfectly padded CSV.

Think of it as giving your data a uniform look, so it’s always ready for presentation or analysis.
