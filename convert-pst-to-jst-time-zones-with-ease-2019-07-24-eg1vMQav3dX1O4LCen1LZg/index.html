How It Works

The conversion from PST to JST involves adding 17 hours to the PST time. This is because JST is 17 hours ahead of PST. To put it simply, when it's 10:00 AM PST, it would be 3:00 AM (next day) JST.

PST Time JST Time
12:00 AM PST 5:00 PM JST (same day)
6:00 AM PST 11:00 PM JST (same day)
12:00 PM PST 5:00 AM JST (next day)
6:00 PM PST 11:00 AM JST (next day)
12:00 AM PST (next day) 5:00 PM JST (next day)
