How It Works

The tool works by taking your list of items, splitting them into individual lines, and then sorting them alphabetically. The magic happens because it ignores whether letters are uppercase or lowercase, ensuring a clean and consistent order. Think of it like organizing a bookshelf where the size of the book titles doesn’t matter—only the order of the words.
