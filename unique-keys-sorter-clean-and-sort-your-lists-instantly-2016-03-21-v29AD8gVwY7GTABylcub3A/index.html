How It Works

The Unique Keys Sorter works like a well-oiled machine. You provide a list of keys (one per line or separated by commas), and the tool does the heavy lifting. First, it removes any duplicate keys to ensure everything is unique. Then, it sorts the remaining keys alphabetically or numerically, depending on your input. The result is a clean, organized list ready for whatever you need.
