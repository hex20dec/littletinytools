How It Works

The AEDT to KST Converter uses a simple formula to make time zone conversion a breeze. Since AEDT is 2 hours ahead of KST, the tool subtracts 2 hours from the AEDT time you provide. For example, if it's 3:00 PM AEDT, the tool will calculate it as 1:00 PM KST. If the calculation goes below 0 (like at midnight), it wraps around to the previous day, so 1:00 AM AEDT becomes 11:00 PM KST the day before. Easy, right?
