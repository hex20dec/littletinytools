How It Works

Converting CST to MST is all about understanding the time difference. CST is one hour ahead of MST, so the formula is simple: MST = CST - 1 hour. For example, if it's 3:00 PM CST, it’s 2:00 PM MST. Our tool does the math for you, so you don’t have to!
