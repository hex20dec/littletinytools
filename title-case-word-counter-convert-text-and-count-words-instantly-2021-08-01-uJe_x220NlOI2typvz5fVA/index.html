How It Works:

The Title Case Word Counter works by analyzing your input text and applying title case rules automatically. Here's the simple breakdown:

  1. It capitalizes the first letter of each word, except for minor words like "a," "an," "the," and conjunctions (unless they’re the first word).
  2. It counts the number of words in your text, ignoring extra spaces or empty entries.

For example, if you type "the quick brown fox," it becomes "The Quick Brown Fox," and the word count updates instantly.
