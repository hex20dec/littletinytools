How It Works

The CET to PST Converter works by adjusting the input CET time by 9 hours, since CET is 9 hours ahead of PST. Here's the simple formula behind it:

PST Time = CET Time - 9 Hours

For example, if it's 14:30 (2:30 PM) in CET, subtracting 9 hours gives you 05:30 (5:30 AM) in PST. Easy, right? The tool also handles both 12-hour and 24-hour formats, so you don’t have to worry about AM or PM confusion.
