How does the Kilometers per Liter Calculator work?

It’s as straightforward as it gets! The tool uses a simple formula to calculate fuel efficiency:

Fuel Efficiency (km/L) = Distance Traveled (km) ÷ Fuel Consumed (L)

For example, if you drove 300 kilometers and used 20 liters of fuel, your fuel efficiency would be 15 km/L. The calculator does all the heavy lifting, so you don’t have to!
