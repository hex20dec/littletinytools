How It Works

The Adjusted Workweek Difference Calculator works by comparing two sets of hours: your standard workweek hours and your adjusted workweek hours. Here’s the simple formula it uses:

Difference = Adjusted Hours - Standard Hours

For example, if your standard workweek is 40 hours and you adjust it to 35 hours, the tool calculates 35 - 40, giving you a difference of -5 hours. This means you’re working 5 hours less than usual. Easy, right?
