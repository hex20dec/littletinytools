Sum Calculator

Juggling numbers and trying to add them up can feel like herding cats—especially when they’re scattered across your screen. Whether you’re tallying expenses, calculating scores, or just trying to make sense of a list of numbers, the Sum Calculator is here to save the day. Simply drop in your numbers (separated by commas or spaces), and voilà—it instantly gives you the total. No math headaches, no second-guessing. It’s like having a math wizard in your pocket, ready to crunch numbers faster than you can say “addition”!

Enter numbers separated by commas or spaces to calculate their sum.

How Does It Work?

The Sum Calculator works by taking a list of numbers you provide, whether separated by commas or spaces. It then:

  1. Cleans Up: Filters out any non-number entries (like letters or symbols).
  2. Converts: Turns the remaining valid numbers into a format it can work with.
  3. Adds: Adds them all together to give you the final sum.

For example, if you input 1, 2, 3 or 1 2 3, the tool will calculate the sum as 6.
